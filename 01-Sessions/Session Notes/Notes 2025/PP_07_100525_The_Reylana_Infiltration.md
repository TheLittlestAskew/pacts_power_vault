## Section 2 — Orphie's POV Overview

We slept in the mushroom god's house because it was the safest roof left in a city that's been gutted, and the dreams came for all of us. Mine put me across a counter from my own ancestor, Athena, swimming in pints with Blazebringer on her back like she'd never set it down. She told me the axe has lasted hundreds of years because it's part-forged from some far-off metal, that it'll never rust the way I never will. She told me my path mirrors hers, that I'll walk strange skies and meet demons I might be related to, and she told me a thing I'm carrying like a splinter: there's an oil out on some other world that doesn't wash off, that's eaten whole planes, and that it once killed Ogre dead. Don't touch the oil. I made her promise me twice and I'll make him promise it back.

We woke knowing what the war actually needs from us: ten guilds, ten tokens or ten people, brought to the heart of the city's hidden lines to drag a dead dragon back into the world. And we went straight into the worst of it, Sanis's own childhood home, turned into a war camp with his family's name still on the gate. We crept through the basement he used to hide in as a boy and found cells where the playroom used to be. We found a girl who looks like Selena, who looks like Sanis, whose arm comes off in your hand and grows back, one of a whole line of stolen sisters. We freed her. We pressed our ears to a vent and heard the metal-faced witch and the snake-haired queen trade notes on how they mean to march through a hollowed-out ghost district and gut our allies from the soft side. Then a blade found me in the dark because I was a half-second too slow, and I stood there and let it go through me without a sound, because Sanis needed us quiet more than I needed to swing. It's in me now, that wound and that information both. Next time the quiet ends.

## Section 3 — Session Analysis

### Narrative Summary

*Rest & the four dreams.* Battered after the Bone Mine, the party beds down in [[Thal]]'s Myconid cavern. [[Ogre]] dispatches two lingering, harmless [[Eternals]] outside ("back to the blind eternity"; 9 + 15 psychic) and casts **Shatter** (L3, 22) to widen the cave mouth (now large-creature sized). On watch rotation, **every PC dreams — and the dreams prove to be real, happening *now*:**
- **[[Ogre]] → the [[Gruul Clans\|Gruul]] red wastes.** He finds **[[Borborygmos]]** (deposed former Gruul leader) humiliated and depressed — the new leader **[[Domri Rade]]** smashed his "cup." [[Sharp Dog]] (the perpetually-stoned Gruul) eggs him on. Ogre restores Borborygmos's confidence (gives him his leathers as a cover) and then sprints down to **punch Domri Rade in the crotch** (Eldritch Blast 22 hits; 9 uppercut). Sharp Dog: *"You lit the fire, homie. Keep the trees burning."* A nat-20 tells Ogre it was a dream "connected with more."
- **[[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]] → an Aphonexa-sent vision.** [[Aphonexa]] (heightened senses since ascending) shows him the **[[Reylana Estate]]** in [[Wayport]] surrounded by a Devkarin/[[Lotleth]] army awaiting orders. He forces himself awake to warn the party.
- **[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] → [[Ugin]]'s meditation realm.** Ugin is back in old-man form at full power (the Tarkir timelines having shifted; safe now because [[Nicol Bolas\|Bolas]] is physically occupied on Ravnica). He delivers the **central lore-dump:** the **[[Guildpact]]** is the agreement of the **10 guilds** to live in harmony; Ravnica's ley lines converge at a **central Nexus = the center of the [[implicit maze]]**; to revive **[[Niv-Mizzet]]** and re-establish the Guildpact, the party must bring **a representative or symbol/token of power from each of the 10 guilds** to that Nexus. Parting charge: *"You must turn into dragon form during the final battle… this is a brother's war of dragons in the end."* (Varis's Guildpact History recall fails — nat 1, then 1 on advantage.)
- **[[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]] → the [[Rakdos]] Carnarium under siege.** [[Eternals]] swarm the amphitheater; **[[Penny]]** defends a coven of blood witches ritually trying to wake **[[Rakdos]]** (his giant caged heart). Rin casts **Wall of Fire** (L4) and incinerates 100+ Eternals, then uses **Thaumaturgy** to amplify a crowd "Rakdos, Rakdos" chant. Rakdos wakes — *"The show shall begin."* (Arcana 22, Insight 25, History 22, Intimidation 10.)
- **[[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]] → drinks with [[Athena]].** Her ancestor (also a Blazebringer-wielder) reveals: **[[Blazebringer]] is partly forged of Amonkhet/[[Hazoret]] metal** (why it never rusts/breaks); **[[Ogre]] once died on [[Dominaria]] after touching Phyrexian oil** — *"don't touch the oil… it's destroyed worlds"*; Athena "only sleeps with demons," so Orphie's demonic ancestor is among them (unnamed). Orphie wakes **tipsy**.

*The plan.* Awake, the party inventories which **guild tokens/representatives** they can muster (Dimir — Varis's token/[[Benji]]; Rakdos — [[Penny]]; Selesnya — Rin's recovered amulet; Boros — [[Molly]]; Izzet — [[Dahlia]]/an Amla; Orzhov — Varis's amulet; Azorius — [[Lavinia]] or [[Clarissa]]; Gruul — [[Borborygmos]]/[[Sharp Dog]]). **Golgari and Simic are the gaps.** "Sis" ([[Pippi]]) confirms the route: go to the **First Church of [[Orzhov Syndicate\|Orzhov]]** so she can recalibrate its portal to the implicit maze's entrance. Lore correction: when Ogre Mage-Handed [[Salena\|Selena]] into the essence pool, the god-souls (**[[God-Eternal Oketra\|Oketra]]** and the bat death-god **[[Aclazotz]]**) went *with* her; "Sis" now holds only **[[Argo Lot]]** (Ogre's "daddy," a voiceless battery-like essence) plus the absorbed Eternal souls.

*The Reylana infiltration.* Since they're already in Golgari, they head for Sanis's family estate. **Pass Without Trace + stealth (avg ~26)** carries them through the gutted, civilian-empty district to the **[[Reylana Estate]]**, now a Golgari **war camp**. They spot ~5 girls Selena's age being marched around. **[[Vraska]]** (Gorgon; Golgari guild leader; the one who killed [[Jace]]) appears; **[[Liliana Vess\|Liliana]]** arrives by portal and the two go inside. The party sneaks into the **basement** via Sanis's boyhood route. Sanis uses his **Reylana necklace** to unlock a *new* hidden door (inscription: *"That which is dead may never die, but those who seek life shall live eternal"*). The corridor beyond — once a ritual hall — is now lined with **jail cells**:
- One holds a **picnic basket/blanket** (the recurring unseen "Time Lord" pair) with a note — *"thought y'all might need this"* — and a **Potion of Command Undead** (to Ogre).
- One holds **[[Brula Galadora]]** (who once delivered Selena's message), beaten, her powers suppressed. Orphie kicks the door in (Athletics 22). Brula's **arm falls off and regrows** — she's a flesh golem like Selena, a "soul sister," one of several girls who could be Selena's/**Sanis's** blood relations (Insight 21). Her "father" was the doctor the party killed; she has no mother. **Necromantic magic** (an arcane shell, Arcana 17) blocks her transforming; Ogre's **Dispel Magic** (L3, 15) fails. They send her to stealth out to the **[[Selesnya Conclave]]**.

The party then enters the now-empty ritual room, finds a vent, and **eavesdrops on [[Liliana Vess\|Liliana]] & [[Vraska]]** (Liliana **winks** at them — she knows they're there):
- The **Rakdos invasion was thwarted** — the guild roused Rakdos with the help of "an Aasimar ghost conjuring a wall of flame" (**Rin's dream was real**).
- **[[Borborygmos]] has roused** after "being punched in the gruul jewel with a makeshift cup" (**Ogre's dream was real**); the Gruul front is losing momentum, [[Domri Rade]] opposed by the old guard.
- The **[[Simic Combine\|Simic]]** are still loyal, building monstrosities with **Eternal plating** and holding the **[[Immortal Sun]]** (trapping planeswalkers so their sparks can be harvested).
- **[[Tezzeret]]** confirmed dead.
- The **[[Orzhov Syndicate\|Orzhov]]** have "donated their entire spirit" — the district is a **deserted ghost town**; the Golgari plan to **march through hollow Orzhov to hit the Alliance's weak flank**.
- **[[Kaya]]** has been "dealt with" — alive but out of commission (Insight 28).

Vraska gets word of the basement transformation attempt and calls the guards. The party snaps on **invisibility gauntlets** (a failed activation = blinded 1 min + the gauntlet breaks — Rin's breaks). They hide in **Lord Reylana's study**; Sanis & Rin try to memorize the battle plans (Int nat-1/advantage 7 — fail) but salvage **the route the Golgari intend to take through Orzhov**. Sanis half-remembers (forgotten lore): his **Dhampir powers** came from the **[[Golgari Lich]]** (Vraska's predecessor) who implanted **[[Argo Lot]]**'s head/essence into him and bound him by pact to **kill [[Vraska]]**. Guards enter; Orphie fails to slip aside (Acrobatics 8), is brushed, then **stabbed** (Con save 9 — fails, makes a sound). **Combat is triggered and initiative rolled — but deferred to next session (cliffhanger).**
