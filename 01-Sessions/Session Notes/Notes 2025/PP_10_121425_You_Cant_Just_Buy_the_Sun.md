## Section 2 — Orphie's POV Overview

We floated over the wreck of that drowned lab with Rin tucked into my pack like a sick kitten, and watched a blimp shaped like a goblin's ugly grinning face sail off with the Sun we came for. So it goes. Half this war is showing up, and the other half is paperwork. We chased the grin across the city to a tower with that same face leering off the top of it, and found out the goblin we ran errands for five years ago has made himself a king with a whole secret city under the streets. He won't even take a meeting. His tiny accountant on her giant desk told us the Sun goes to the highest bidder tonight, like the fate of every soul on this plane is a lot at an estate sale.

So now we're passing a hat. Us. The people who keep saving this place are out here begging the guilds for coin and rounding up stoners and old friends to either buy the world back or steal it. Sanis dragged his dead father's ghost around like a bad smell, and the old man had the gall to offer fatherly advice from beyond the grave. I carried my best friend on my back through a field of refugees and watched my found family beg a health-insurance lady for potions. It's not glorious. But we got gold from the weed girls, muscle from the planeswalkers, and a plan with a knife behind its back if the auction goes sideways. Tonight we either win the Sun clean or we take it. Either way, that blimp's grin is coming down.

## Section 3 — Session Analysis

### Narrative Summary

In the wreck's aftermath, the party (on dragon-[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]], with the new **Lord Raylana Specter** and a sick, unconscious [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]]) watches a **goblin-faced blimp armed with cannons** fly off toward the Izzet/[[Tin Street]] — soon ID'd (via [[Benji]]) as **[[Krenko]]'s**. Deduction: **Krenko's crew blew up the lab and took the [[Immortal Sun]].** [[Benji]] — now in the **Dimir "Inner Circle"** (the guild is leaderless/chaotic since the party crashed its information network) — drops intel, gifts **5 basic healing potions**, and ferries **[[Vuthala]]** to the [[Selesnya Conclave]]. He also clocks Ogre's **"Sis"/[[Leather Daddy Game Boy]]** as partly **Dimir property** ("I'll need that at some point").

The party follows the blimp to **Krenko HQ** on **[[Tin Street]]** (their old neighborhood — they attended **Tin Street / 10th Street High**; Krenko is the mob boss who sent them after the **[[Sword of Feast and Famine]]** five years ago, now risen to "**Krenko Incorporated**," a 20–25-story tower over a **sprawling underground city**). They long-rest on a rooftop:
- **[[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]]'s watch:** a goblin messenger delivers a **letter from [[Krenko]]** ("come talk before I sell the sun to a dragoner…").
- **[[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]]'s watch:** his **father's Specter** attempts conversation — he can *see the pact threads* binding Sanis (the **pact to overthrow [[Vraska]]**), notes the **[[Reylana Estate]] now passes to Vraska**, and muses on his lineage. Sanis spots a **shadowy medium figure** slipping into Krenko HQ.

Next morning they enter Krenko HQ — greeted by **[[Rekka]]** (the guard from the Bone Mine, now Krenko's, "Australian-Scottish"), then routed to **[[Kiku]]** (a tiny goblin assistant atop a comically huge desk). The reveal: the **[[Immortal Sun]] will be auctioned tonight to the highest bidder** (alongside the Temporal Spear, Hell Spear, an Izzet signet, etc.). The letter was only a **parlay request, not a binding contract** (Sanis tries to argue otherwise; **Krenko** is "too busy" to meet). Sanis's **persuasion fails (nat 1)**. They leave plotting a heist — and Sanis glimpses **[[Narset]]** (planeswalker ally) shimmering in the underground city.

**Heist/auction prep** (the bulk of the session):
- **Plan A — buy it.** [[Benji]] (re-summoned by saying his name thrice) agrees to canvass allies for funds — but the **[[Orzhov Syndicate\|Orzhov]] banking system is dead** ([[Kaya]] "dealt with"; the district a ghost town). They must **restart the bank at the First Church of [[Orzhov Syndicate\|Orzhov]]** to pool money. Targets: **[[Dahlia]]** (Izzet leader), **[[Emmara Tandris]]** (Selesnya), what's left of the Orzhov, **Hikaru** (Rakdos).
- **Plan B — steal it.** Use the **[[Gruul Clans\|Gruul]]** ([[Borborygmos]]/[[Sharp Dog]], mid-civil-war vs [[Domri Rade]]) as a distraction/strike force; **[[Johnny]], [[Nissa]], [[Chandra Nalaar\|Chandra]]** sign on to lie in wait with them. Sanis texts Sharp Dog ("np homie, we got you fo sho").
- At the **[[Selesnya Conclave]]** (now a vast refugee camp — Gruul, Orzhov, Simic fleeing the Second Sun/Allies war): **[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]'s parents, [[Lord Aestra]] & [[Lady Aestra]]** (Orzhov refugees), agree to rally tellers and liquidity to **reopen the First Church bank** once the party scouts it. **[[Emmara Tandris]]** pledges Selesnya funds once banking is live. **[[Saskia]] & [[Myra]]** (the weed/smut-book duo) donate **200 gp**; Varis gifts Saskia the **signed copy of "The Sultry Simic Maiden"** — Sanis being its canonical author. [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]] redeems his family's **"premium tier insurance"** for **5 Superior Healing Potions (8d4+8)** at the Selesnya doctor.

The session ends with the party about to head to the **First Church of [[Orzhov Syndicate\|Orzhov]]** to reopen the bank — leaving **Lord Raylana's Specter** behind to be peppered with questions by ~25 planeswalkers. ([[Ogre]] flags they never dug into the **Vraska pact** — likely important lore.)

### Locations Visited

Simic-district lake surface → [[Tin Street]] (10th District marketplace; rooftop) → **Krenko HQ** (ground floor + the −9th floor underground city) → **[[Selesnya Conclave]]** refugee camp (Orzhov quarter; Varis's parents' tent; Emmara; the doctor).

### Quests / Objectives

- **New central objective:** **Acquire the [[Immortal Sun]]** before/at tonight's **auction** at Krenko Incorporated — by **purchase (Plan A)** or **theft (Plan B)**.
- **Sub-quest (Plan A):** **Reopen the First Church of [[Orzhov Syndicate\|Orzhov]] bank** so the guilds can pool auction funds. (Varis's parents + Orzhov refugees ready; party must scout the church next.)
- **Sub-quest (Plan B):** **[[Gruul Clans\|Gruul]] distraction/strike** (Borborygmos/Sharp Dog) + planeswalker muscle (Johnny, Nissa, Chandra).
- **Ally handoff:** **[[Vuthala]]** delivered to the [[Selesnya Conclave]] by Benji.
- **Open threads:** Who is the **shadowy figure** in Krenko HQ? Why is **[[Narset]]** here (and others)? The **Vraska pact** (undug). The **Dimir's claim on "Sis"/the LDGB**. **[[Kaya]]**'s fate ("dealt with").
- **Loose end:** Krenko still wants the **[[Sword of Feast and Famine]] hilt** (party declined to surrender it without the Sun).

### Themes & Emotional Beats

The unglamorous machinery of heroism (fundraising, banking, paperwork) vs. world-ending stakes; the absurdity of bidding on the multiverse's fate; Sanis haunted (literally) by his father's ghost offering hollow paternal advice; the party's loyalty (Orphie carrying sick Rin all session); community and mutual aid (refugees, the weed-girls' easy generosity, "friends in low places").
