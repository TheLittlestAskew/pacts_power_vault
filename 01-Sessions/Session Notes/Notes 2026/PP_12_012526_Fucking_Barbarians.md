## Section 2 — Orphie's POV Overview

The bidding turned to blood the moment the snake-haired woman threw her gaze at Rin and turned my friend halfway to stone. So I did what I do. I shut my eyes — because you don't let a gorgon look you in the face — and I let my brother teleport me up into her box, and I swung at a killer I could not see. Three daggers in me, poison singing in my blood, and I still wouldn't fall, because behind me Sanis was calling out where she stood and Varis was raining fire and Rin pulled herself out of the stone and back into the fight. We are very hard to kill when we decide not to be. I felt her crumple in my arms before I ever saw her, and when I finally opened my eyes she was dead at my feet, cursing the Dragon with her last breath. I took her head for a trophy. Sanis took it from me, not by choice — something old and patient in him needed it, and when it turned to dust in his hands, the last ghost riding his blood finally went quiet. He's just himself now. About time.

And then, flying out over a burning auction house with a stolen tavern in the dragon's claws and a borrowed child playing tag on his spine, I felt the chains come off. The ones I never even knew were there, holding all of us to this one plane like nails in a board. The Sun is gone from here, and the sky filled up with light, souls finally free to leave. For one breath I thought we'd won something clean. Then the Dragon screamed NO loud enough to crack the city, and carved a district in half with a wave of his hand, and I remembered we don't get clean wins. We get this. We keep getting up.

## Section 3 — Session Analysis

### Narrative Summary

Picking up the S11 cliffhanger mid-auction:

*The Guildpact shreds & the reveal.* Krenko's second lot is **the shreds of the First [[Guildpact]]** (a tattered, millennia-old tome — useful to *reforge the Guildpact*). A bidding war erupts: the party ([[Ogre]] stealing other bidders' paddles via Mage Hand), the **two black-masked figures**, and [[Janna]] on the chandelier. [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]] crosses the aisle to Hold-Person the suspicious pair — it fails, and one is revealed a **Gorgon** who **petrifies her** (nat-1 Con save); Rin collapses, restrained, turning to stone. [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] Fireballs the box (burning the mask off) and **teleports [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]]** up — revealing **[[Vraska]]**, Golgari Guild Leader and Jace's killer. **Combat begins.**

*The fight.* The amphitheater catches fire (Varis's Fireball + a behind-stage explosion — [[Ava]]'s diversion); auction-goers flee; [[Krenko]] panics and calls the guards. Key beats:
- **[[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]] fights blind** (eyes shut against the gorgon gaze, taking disadvantage) — raging, tanking **Vraska's** dual poisoned daggers (27+19, then 34+20 damage), and landing a **nat-20 crit (51 dmg)**.
- **[[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]] is un-petrified**: [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] hands her his **[[Mizzium Apparatus]]/Elysium Apparatus gauntlet** (DM hand-waved attunement → anyone can cast off their own class list with it — a deliberately *broken/OP* ruling, flagged); Rin casts **Greater Restoration** through it (nat-20 + guidance = **34**), shatters the stone, and **sprouts wings**.
- **[[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]]** Misty-Steps up and shoots Vraska with **[[Ava's Longbow of Power]]**, guiding blind Orphie ("to your right!"). A **voice** in his head urges *"Do it… fulfill the pact… finish her"* — **not** [[Argo Lot]] ("Zaddy Daddy"); Sanis fails to ID it.
- **[[Ogre]]** reaches the caged **[[Immortal Sun]]** (electrified — 16 lightning; his Mage Hand disintegrates on contact), then **drops the [[Sword of Feast and Famine]] hilt → it bonks [[Krenko]] prone** (10 dmg).
- **[[Narset]]** drops to the Sun, plants an **Izzet apparatus bomb** ("hot potato"), casts Jump on Ogre, and — when it detonates — **bags the Immortal Sun and escapes backstage** with her crew. The Sun is now in *friendly hands*, leaving the plane.
- **[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]** unloads Scorching Rays (two nat-20 crits, 43 dmg), chugs Kalmar's **Potion of Haste**, and goes **full dragon**.
- **[[Vraska]]'s ultimate**: a wave of **black void energy** — *the same energy [[Ogre]]'s blade absorbed from Bolas* — for **78 force** (the party shrugs it off; "what the **** is wrong with these people?"). **Varis's tail attack finishes her.** Dying words: ***"This isn't the last of it, Bolas. The power he granted me wasn't enough. In the end, it's all up to [[Liliana Vess\|Liliana]]."*** [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]] **decapitates her and takes the head** (gorgon eyes = a petrification weapon).
- **[[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]] Hold-Persons [[Krenko]]** and **steals the entire display case of Guildpact shreds** (+ psychic damage, + a fire-safety rant).

*Escape.* [[Ava]] (who set the back of the building ablaze) has recovered **Ogre's Tavern 2** (the Airstream, keys in hand: "took you long enough"). On dragon-[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]'s back — with the Airstream, the **Guildpact shreds**, Vraska's head, and the tag-along halfling **[[Janna]]** — they flee, **Force-Breathing Krenko's goblin-blimp to shreds** on the way out. They glimpse a **[[Ravnica Trading Incorporated]]** carriage (red-haired driver — likely [[Rekka]]) leaving; [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]] recalls (nat-20) it's **[[Shaylin]]'s** company — [[Narset]]'s crew (the Tarkir troop with the doppelganger [[Kalmar]]). All allies.

*Aftermath at the Selesnya Conclave.*
- **[[Janna]]** (a halfling girl from the "Blister Coils") refuses to go home; she mentions a friend who "stands on corners and sells to people" — blue skin, white hair, trench coat — **[[Lance Filo\|Lance]]** (Orphie's long-lost former companion, an Air Genasi), **revealed alive on Ravnica** (apparently a dealer). The party parks Janna in the Conclave's medicine workshop ("Janna smash!" — destructive energy redirected to grinding herbs).
- **Sanis's voice resolved:** it was the **[[Golgari Lich]]** (**Yorvo Vod Savo**, the first Golgari Guild Leader/lich) who resurrected Sanis as a **dhampir** by infusing the spirit of the long-dead Dimir Guildmaster **[[Szadek]]** into him. His pact: **kill [[Vraska]]** and deliver her **head as tribute**. Compelled, Sanis takes Vraska's head from Orphie (she grapples him); it **disintegrates** — *"pact fulfilled."* The last independent shred of **[[Argo Lot]] ("Zaddy Daddy")** then **merges fully into Sanis** — no more separate voice. Sanis **unlocks his full dhampir potential**: permanent **+10 ft speed** (now 40) and a new ability.
- **[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] & his parents:** he tells the **[[Lord Aestra\|Aestras]]** he reopened the Orzhov bank and holds guild leadership; he "blesses" his father as **bank manager** (clumsy ritual, rolled a 3 — a sash appears anyway). The Aestras will lead the refugees back to reopen the **[[Orzhov Syndicate\|Orzhov]]** with the ~100k reserve. Varis intends to appoint a permanent leader later; keeps the diamond.

*The turning point.* Flying off, all PCs feel **planeswalking restored** — the "chains/shackles" since [[Ral Zarek\|Ral]]'s Beacon are gone. Reason (Rin/Varis Arcana 26): **the [[Immortal Sun]] is no longer on Ravnica** ([[Narset]] took it off-plane). Hundreds of light-beams streak the sky as **trapped planeswalkers leave the plane.** Then a booming **"NO!"** — **[[Nicol Bolas\|Bolas]]** (dragon form) climbs his **[[Bolas's Citadel\|Citadel]]** in the Golgari District and fires a beam of black energy across the **[[Azorius Senate\|Azorius]] District**, disintegrating swaths of it and **cutting the Azorius HQ in half** (thoughts of **[[Lavinia]]**). **Session ends on Bolas wreaking destruction.** The party **levels up to 12** (milestone).
