## Section 2 — Orphie's POV Overview

He sat up there on his stolen throne drinking down the souls of a thousand worlds, and for the first time I understood that being scared and going in anyway is the only kind of brave there is. So I went in. My hands shook around the haft and I went in. And when the fire-axe I'd carried through every fight finally asked to become something new, my grandmother handed me a hammer instead, and told me a hammer is for building as much as breaking. I chose it. I smashed his armor and his clawed feet and everything I could reach, and I ran and hid and breathed and went back and smashed again, and slowly, between all of us, the dragon bled.

It wasn't one of us that ended him. It was all of us, and the ones we'd saved along the way: the puppet-queen we freed turning her gods on her old master, the partying god who couldn't miss a show, the healer who carried the woman she loves back from the dark, the quiet one who shoved a god through a door, and a sister-goddess who burned herself out one last time so the rest of us could live. The monster who haunted the edges of our whole lives was sealed away by an old dragon who chose to be a prison so the world wouldn't have to. And then it was over. Truly over. We climbed down into a city full of cheering and lanterns, and we poured the drinks ourselves, and an old friend I thought I'd lost forever walked back through a door just to say he'd missed me. I don't know who I'll be without a dragon to chase. But I think I'd like to build something, for once. I think I've earned the quiet.

## Section 3 — Session Analysis

> Direct continuation of S19: the party is atop the Citadel, **[[Liliana Vess]]** freed and her **God-Eternals turned on [[Nicol Bolas\|Bolas]]**, while Bolas siphons the orb of harvested sparks toward his ascension. **Initiative resumes.**

### A. The battle for the summit
- **House rule — "Desperate Measures"** (borrowed from Critical Role): while **bloodied**, a PC may pre-fail death saves for power (dash+disengage / +5 to a failed d20 / reroll; 2 saves = max damage on a hit; 3 saves = extra action or regain a spell slot — but 0 HP then means instant death). High risk, high reward, used all fight.
- **[[Nicol Bolas\|Bolas]]** opens by shrugging off the God-Eternals' strike and unleashing AoE breath (fire/necrotic/force) and a necrotic blast that **blinds** much of the party. He is a **Dragon-God** (spell save DC 22) and near-untouchable while siphoning.
- **[[Teferi]] returns** through a portal (Dan, guest), joining the fray and answering Orphie's running "rewind time so we can't lose" bit.
- The fight is a war of attrition: **Molly** duels a God-Eternal (down, healed, back up, repeatedly); **[[Rakdos]] appears**, drops **Penny** onto Bolas's shoulder to hack at his scales, and leaves "his mark" before departing; **Liliana's God-Eternals (Bontu/Rhonas/Kefnet)** steadily drain Bolas's power; **[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] transforms into a dragon** (Ugin's power) and goes all-out; **[[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]]** support-fires with Ava's Longbow + Lightning Arrow.
- ⭐ **Orphie's HellSmasher.** Reaching for Athena via her unanswered-prayer moment, **[[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]]** is pulled into a vision: **[[Athena]]** offers Blazebringer's **final form** — a sword (would be "Hellraiser"), an axe, or a hammer. Orphie chooses the **hammer**: *"I've always wanted a hammer. Let's try something new."* Athena names it **HellSmasher** (a maul dealing **1d20** damage, +10), with the lesson that *"the greatest bonds can be forged with a hammer"* — for building as much as breaking. Orphie returns and starts caving Bolas in (including a gleeful **66-damage smash to his pinky toe**).
- ⭐ **The key tactic — choking the siphon.** **[[Ogre]]** (now **[[Ur-Ogre-lan]]**, the halfling death-god, wiser) realizes he can't move Bolas, so he uses **Mage Hand + Eldritch Blast** to **push the orb of sparks away** from Bolas, foot by foot, starving his siphon. Rin's Arcana confirms it's *working* ("positive").
- **Casualties mount:** Varis is dropped (caught mid-fall by Ogre's Mage Hand); Molly and Penny go down and are healed; the party leans hard on **Rin's Mass Cure Wounds**. **[[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]]'s Divine Intervention fails the entire session** — Dol Arrah stays silent (the campaign-long running gag; *"my god really does fucking suck"*).

### B. The fall of Bolas
- **[[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]] lands the decisive blow:** a **Hail of Thorns + Ava's Longbow** shot to Bolas's exposed solar plexus. Bolas drops to a knee; when he tries to yank the spark-orb back to him, **the tether frays and snaps.** He coughs black blood: *"No, no, this cannot be,"* and collapses.
- **The orb destabilizes** — an amalgamation of hundreds of thousands of Planeswalker sparks, about to detonate the multiverse's primordial energy.
- ⭐ **Aphonexa's sacrifice.** **[[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]]** calls to his sister-goddess **[[Aphonexa]]** (Selena). She appears beneath the orb (mushroom cloak, mycelium), says *"don't worry, all will be safe,"* and **envelops the unstable orb**, holding it to the bursting point and **releasing the sparks safely** back into the multiverse. The cost: **Aphonexa is gone** — the blessing-warmth in Sanis's chest goes cold; he feels a hand on his cheek and a whispered *"I love you."* (Sanis loses his sister a second, final time.)
- **The harvest & the seal.** Bolas down but not dead, **Liliana's God-Eternals (Lazotep-plated) absorb his spark**. **[[Ogre]] tries to siphon Bolas's soul with Duskblade and fails** — Bolas is an elder dragon, *part of what made existence.* **[[Ugin]] appears, fully corporeal**, and counsels that Bolas **cannot be destroyed, only contained.** Bolas's soul is sealed into **[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]'s Dragon Spirit Box (Dragonhedron)**, and **Ugin sentences himself to imprison Bolas** in his own realm. Before departing, Ugin **bestows on Varis the mantle of protecting and heralding dragons on Tarkir** (an immense well of power + a call to Tarkir). Ugin bows, thanks them for ending a multi-millennia family feud, and vanishes with the box.
- **Victory.** [[Vitu-Ghazi]] marches up with **Nissa**, **Myra Deathbloom**, and **Saskia Vostavo**; **Vraska** and **Narset** appear (the Immortal Sun gone, back on Ixalan). The fighting across the battlefield ceases — Tarkir dragons, the Jeskai, pirates, merfolk, Ixalan's dinosaur priestesses, and all ten guilds celebrate. **[[Niv-Mizzet]]** declares a coming time of peace for Ravnica. **No PC died** in the final fight (Ogre "died" and returned; Varis went down and lived).

### C. Epilogue — Ogre's Traveling Tavern
The DM ran **one-on-one epilogue scenes** with each player (this is what the late "bare-number" DDB rolls are — Divine-Intervention and misc rolls, incl. **Rin finally rolling under 12** at the party):
- ⭐ **Lance returns.** The original DM, **Stephen**, surprise-joins to play **[[Lance Filo\|Lance]]** — now a monk-like figure. He gives **[[Ogre]]** a halfling-etched hammer engraved *"To the god of death — Hi, I'm Ogre — I miss you, buddy,"* with a tiny carving of the two of them.
- **[[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]]:** Dol Arrah *finally answers* her Divine Intervention — but only after the danger has passed: *"We're having a party now… No, I couldn't [come sooner]."* Rin's future: founds an **Azorius/Boros worker-safety inspection agency** ("Rin becoming OSHA"), funds the tavern, and builds stables for her tamed gorgers **Timmy & Tommy**.
- **[[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]]:** **Molly** asks him to dinner next week — he finally *keeps* a partner; he inherits the now-empty **Reylana estate** as sole heir.
- **[[Ogre]]:** **[[Ur-Ogre-lan]]** (the death-god) departs to "fulfill a wish and keep a promise," leaving the **innocent Ogre** back in the world to keep adventuring and running the tavern in the Rakdos district (a final "Hi, I'm Ogre" gag).
- **[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]:** hands the **[[Orzhov Syndicate\|Orzhov]] Guild** to his adopted father and departs for **Tarkir** to steward Ugin's dragons.
- **[[Teferi]]:** returns to the **Gatewatch**, off to Dominaria to help Ajani; leaves Orphie **two Polymorph scrolls** (for T-Rex).
- **[[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]]:** chases a new life-goal — finding the **Ur-Dragon** (Bolas's progenitor); means to pass down the bloodline and Blazebringer; granddaughter of **[[Athena]]**.
- The DM gifts the table **commissioned party art** and **custom Magic: The Gathering cards** for each PC. The party toasts in the tavern; the campaign closes after ~3.5 years.

### Themes & Emotional Beats

**Courage, sacrifice, and building over breaking.** The finale answers every long arc: Orphie's fear-but-go-anyway (and HellSmasher's "build as much as break"), Sanis losing Selena a final time to save everyone, Rin's faith tested to the breaking point and answered as a punchline, Ogre's death-god duality resolved by *choosing* to leave the innocent Ogre behind, and Bolas — the shadow over the whole campaign — *contained, not destroyed*, because some evils you can only hold at bay. The Lance reunion lands the human core: a table that's been together 3.5 years saying goodbye.
