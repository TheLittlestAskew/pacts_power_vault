## Section 2 — Orphie's POV Overview

We crossed into the gold-and-bone district and the cold ran straight through us, because there was nothing left there to be warm. No people, no ghosts, not even bodies. A whole quarter of the city scraped down to silence, the kind of quiet that means something with a big appetite already came and went. We found the ghost-girl who ran this place dying on her own marble steps, half here and half gone, whispering that she opened every vault and freed every soul because the Dragon promised to save her home, and then his locusts ate all of it. She begged to keep a home she'll never see again. We gave her the only mercy left and let her scatter. I wanted to be the one to end her, once. Standing over what was left, I didn't want it anymore.

Then we put on masks and walked into the goblin king's auction like we belonged among thieves, because tonight we did. My brother-in-arms touched a ledger and suddenly knew how to run a bank like he was born to it, and pulled gold out of the air to bid against drunks and a goblin granny throwing her purse like a wrecking ball. I caught that purse out of the air, kept it, threw nothing back. We bought our own stolen tavern back from the people who copied it, better than we built it, and I'll take that small, stupid win. Somewhere above all that chaos, a covered cage swayed on a chain with the trapped Sun inside it, and a friend in shadow was already climbing toward it. Everybody in that room has a plan. So do we. The lights are about to go out, and I cannot wait.

## Section 3 — Session Analysis

### Narrative Summary

*The dead Orzhov.* Recapping the auction plan, the party (with [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]] finally awake) walks to the **[[Orzhov Syndicate\|Orzhov]] district** to investigate and reopen the bank. Crossing the border, a **wave of cold** hits — the district is utterly **void**: no wind, no spirits, no bodies, **no leyline energy or ambient magic** (Varis: "like a forest fire that left no forest"). Magic still *functions* (Varis's Prestidigitation works), but the *life* of the place is gone. At the ruined **First Church of [[Orzhov Syndicate\|Orzhov]]**, they find **[[Kaya]]** dying on the marble stairs — half-ghostly, half-physical (Rin, Arcana 24: her innate plane-shifting biology has been **severed**; she's at the brink of death). Her confession: she **"opened everything up"** — freed every Orzhov spirit and unsealed the vaults — because **[[Nicol Bolas\|Bolas]]** promised to save her home; instead, **the Eternals fed on everything**, consuming all the souls and magic. Her home, **[[Tovada]]**, is "stuck between reality and the ghostly realm; I was the only one who got out." Last words: ***"The account closes, the balance transfers, we depart, but the interest remains."*** **[[Ogre]] mercy-kills her** with the Witch-blade — but she **does not** enter the sword/[[Leather Daddy Game Boy]]; she simply **disperses into the blind eternities**. [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] feels no change.

*Varis and "the account."* Upstairs in his family's quarters, **[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]** finds a **diamond** (Chromatic Orb component), assorted jewelry, and a **loan-agreement document** he can read *by touch alone*. He suddenly, innately **knows how to reinstate the bank** (the party jokes he's "infected by the account"). Via the basement portal he recalibrates the coordinates to **[[Pecunia]]** (Ravnica's second moon — the **Orzhov Grand Vault**). The vault is nearly drained (one pile, ~100,000 gp, down from a far larger hoard — the Eternals took the rest). Standing on the Orzhov insignia, Varis recites ***"By ledger written in bone and gold, the debts and interest laid upon me shall draw, connect to those below"*** — **re-knitting the leyline lattice and reopening the First Bank of [[Orzhov Syndicate\|Orzhov]]**. He can now **conjure gold and issue loans at will** (snapping coins from the vault into his hand) — effectively the acting head of the Orzhov, bonded to its debt-magic (it "hurts" to spend the vault, but he'll do it to save Ravnica).

*Recruiting Ava.* The party descends the [[Tin Street]] sewers to **[[Ava]]**, the **copper dragon** (appears as a gold-hoarding little girl). Through Ogre's circular "the shiny ball makes things not shiny" logic (Persuasion 28), she agrees to help with the heist for **any *other* shiny loot she finds** (not the Sun) and on the condition they **never tell [[Niv-Mizzet]] ("old Nibby") she's here**. [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]] borrows **[[Ava's Longbow of Power]]** (she hisses at his Perun's-Last-Word bow but lends hers).

*The Masquerade Auction.* At **Krenko HQ**, a masked ballroom precedes the amphitheater auction. **[[Rekka]]** (guard) is secretly **helping the party** — earpiece, winks, a "tap-it-if-things-go-down" token for Rin, and a warning that "something will go down tonight; the Sun is the last item." The bar serves literal **essence-of-sleep / paralysis / restraining cocktails**; the **two jet-black-masked figures** anonymously send [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]] a **sleep-spiked drink** (Rin sniffs it out, Medicine 30, and Karens the butler/manager/bartender "Mickey"). **[[Isiah Ja-Faank\|Isaiah]]** is present (Ixalan garb, blitzed on paralysis cocktails, going by "Johnny") with **[[Jareth Wig]]** — revealed as **[[Kwatli Pakal]]**, an Ixalan ruler. **[[Ogre]]** befriends **[[Kalmar]]** (a Goliath guard — the **doppelganger** from a past one-shot) and learns Kalmar is part of **[[Narset]]'s crew**, whose plan is to **steal the Sun to destroy it** (the party's own goal); Kalmar's job is to let Narset in after the auction.

The auction (**[[Krenko]]** as emcee "Franco," 3 items, 1 min each, highest bidder):
- **Item 1 — "Ogre's Tavern 2"** (an *upgraded* Airstream recreation; better etheric battery/ovens — "a Cadillac to the old Honda CRV"). Bidding war among [[Isiah Ja-Faank\|Isaiah]], a **plump goblin lady**, and **[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]** (spending Orzhov bank gold). Rin's stealth Thaumaturgy to dim the display **fizzles on an anti-tamper barrier** (Krenko calls it out); Orphie's intimidation of the goblin lady fails (Intimidation 8, disadv). The goblin lady hurls her **50-lb money-stuffed handbag** at Isaiah, knocking him off the box-seat balcony; **[[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]] catches it (Athletics 27)** and **keeps it**. The two brawl. **[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] subtle-casts upcast Hold Person** on *both* Isaiah and the goblin lady (both fail, DC 17), freezing the fight, and takes the winning bid at **41,000 gp** — **the party reclaims "Ogre's Tavern 2"** (leaving ~59k, not enough for the Sun → committing to Plan B/the heist).

**Cliffhanger:** As chaos reigns, **[[Ogre]] (nat-20 Perception)** spots **[[Narset]]** creeping Spidey-style toward the **covered, chain-hung cage** holding the **[[Immortal Sun]]** (the final lot). Session ends "to be continued."
