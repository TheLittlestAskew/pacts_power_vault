## 2. Orphie POV Overview

We did it. After everything — the maze, the trials, shedding the worst things we carry, and then watching those very things come back wearing our faces — we actually did it. The thing that nearly broke me last time was still there at the start, dripping its black smoke, throwing Sanis's own shadow back at him with his parents' faces on it. So I did the only thing I know how to do when I feel that small again: I climbed up onto the pedestal and I hit it, and I hit it again, and I let Blazebringer's fire light up every slash across its dark. I needed to feel something good, and the universe was kind enough to hand me something to swing at.

But it wasn't my axe that won it. It was Ogre cramming a shrink mushroom into a god's mouth like he was burping a baby, and a tiny helium-voiced Niv-Mizzet popping out the other side going *"thanks, that would've taken me another eighteen seconds."* It was Rin, two levels of exhaustion deep, gripping a live Leyline with tears running down her face and holding on anyway. And it was Niv themselves — reborn, white-aured, the whole Guildpact stitched back together in a crest on their chest — reaching out with all five colors and unmaking the hate that wore our memories. Then we got greedy and grabbed at the raw power ourselves, because of course we did. Most of us walked away glowing. Sanis hesitated half a second and the Leyline nearly hollowed him out. We came up onto the surface in the same plaza where this whole nightmare started, the tree still thrashing but somebody riding it now, Bolas's tower spitting out the dead while the Golgari finally bite back. We touched the butt. And, somehow, we survived.

## 3. Session Analysis

> Direct continuation of S16: the **[[Augur of Bolas]]** fight resumes at the top of the order, with **[[Niv-Mizzet]]'s claw and shoulder already through the portal** and the party having just cleared the hateful shadow-echoes (Rin's Channel Divinity, end of S16).

**Narrative Summary.** The session is one continuous climactic encounter followed by a long, lore-heavy denouement.

- **The Augur fight (resolution).** Physical attacks are confirmed near-useless (the Augur **absorbs** them — Sanis's arrows get sucked into its form, then **fired back out** at him for **18 piercing + 14 necrotic**, its shadow flickering between his father's and mother's faces). The party leans on what *does* work — **light, fire, and radiant** illuminate/erode its incorporeal body. **[[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]]** cop-slides across the central pedestal, activates her **tattoo**, and lays into it with **Blazebringer** (reckless), her fire-slashes lighting up the dark. **[[Ogre]]** casts **Shadow of Moil** (becoming his shadow-wreathed self, blade **"Dusk"**), provokes an opportunity attack to retaliate, and lands a **Hexblade's Curse** (crit on 19s, +damage, HP on kill). **[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]** pumps **Scorching Ray / Fireball** — *four of five rays absorbed*, the fifth landing. **[[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]]** alternates **Ava's Longbow** and **Shortsword** (Dreadful Strikes' bonus psychic).
- **The mushroom gambit (how it's actually won).** **[[Ogre]]** feeds one of his **shrinking mushrooms** to the half-emerged **[[Niv-Mizzet]]**, who instantly compresses from gargantuan to **Large**, pops through the portal, and — in a **high-pitched helium voice** — thanks Ogre for saving "about 18 seconds." Now free, Niv reveals a **decagon crest** on their chest bearing **all ten guilds' colors**, and with **all five colors of mana compressed in one claw**, **obliterates half the Augur's form**.
- **The Augur's last gasp.** It **shrieks** and yanks the ceiling's **stalactites** down like marionette strings (**Dex save DC 18; 4d4 piercing**, several PCs fail). Then **[[Niv-Mizzet]]** finishes it on initiative — one claw collapses the Augur into the floor: ***"the Ogre is no more."*** The black mana is absorbed into Niv's crest. **Combat ends.**
- **Denouement — the Living Guildpact.** Niv stands wreathed in a **white aura** (all colors at peace) and addresses the party. They **endorse the party's replacement of [[Ral Zarek\|Ral]] with [[Dalia Bara]]** as Izzet leader; **stabilize the unstable red [[Leyline]]** (the one that disintegrated the Tarkir dragons/Eternals near Azorius) — *stemming the bleeding* but unable to undo damage already done ("there is no undo button… we technically do have one, but I don't think we want to use it"). A shadow resembling **[[Teferi]]** ("the time guy") is glimpsed through a self-righting **crack in the Blind Eternities**; **[[Benji]]** is felt to be present but pointedly *not helping* ("so Dimir").
- **The Leyline power-grab.** Niv gathers all ten Leylines to recharge. The party, *taking it personally*, grabs on too (Constitution saves):
  - **[[Ogre]]** — repeated successes (Con **28** nat-20, **24**, **21**); restores his highest spell slots multiple times, eyes flashing black/white. "I'm just as good as Niv-Mizzet."
  - **[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]** — succeeds; restores a slot amid pain/euphoria.
  - **[[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]]** — **two levels of exhaustion deep**, grabs anyway with an Orphie-granted **Bardic Inspiration** (DC 15); *barely* survives (tears, shaking) and restores a slot.
  - **[[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]]** — grabs despite minimal arcane aptitude; gets her **expended rage back**.
  - **[[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]]** — **hesitates**, grabs a swirling **black-and-green** Leyline at the wrong moment, and **fails**: **3 levels of exhaustion** + **loses all highest-level spell slots**.
- **Surface arrival.** Niv glyphs the whole ritual **die/platform** and teleports the party (briefly through the **Blind Eternities**) up to the **central plaza** — the very spot where **[[Jace Beleren\|Jace]]** drunkenly gave his speech before his assassination (full circle). They see **[[Vitu-Ghazi]]** still thrashing but now **ridden by a small female figure** (harness/reins — possibly the woman they sent, "Nessa"); **[[Nicol Bolas\|Bolas]]'s Citadel** risen at the **[[Golgari Swarm\|Golgari]]**, with **overgrown buildings and hulking beasts** pouring out to *reap the Eternals* — the tide turning toward the allies. **[[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]]** feels his **pact** coming to fruition as he watches the Golgari rise.
