## 2. Orphie POV Overview

There are some days you cannot win, only refuse to lose, and this was one of them. We came down out of the sky onto a wall a mile long with a sea of the dead pressing against it, and two dead gods half a mile out throwing spears the size of my brother through the air like they were nothing. The first one would have pinned Varis to the clouds; he shrugged it off out of pure stubbornness, and we landed in the noise and the smoke and just got to work. I'm no caster. I can't reach a god from across a field. But I can stand on a wall and be a wall. I dropped down where the bodies were piling over the top, shook off every weight I've ever carried, and swung Blazebringer like a bat until the heads flew, until the soldier I yanked out of the way got to live another minute. That's the whole job. Buy minutes. Hold the gap.

And then, when it was almost too much, our friends came through the air and the ground both. Pirates and merfolk pouring through a door in the world, dragons raining fire over a district we'd already grieved. For one breath I let myself feel it, all the people fighting for this stupid, beautiful city we grew up in. And that's exactly when I felt the deeper wrong, the one no axe fixes: the lines of power that hold this whole plane together are coming apart at the seams, and a wound opened in the Azorius ground that swallows dragons whole. We can win the wall and still lose the world. Then the great old tree at the heart of the Conclave, the one Ogre somehow befriended, pulled its roots up out of the earth and stood up. I don't know yet if that's salvation or one more thing trying to kill us. Lately it's usually both.

## 3. Session Analysis

**Narrative Summary.** Continuing from S13's cliffhanger, **[[Nicol Bolas\|Bolas]]** — atop his **[[Bolas's Citadel\|Citadel]]** in the Golgari District — is razing the **[[Azorius Senate\|Azorius]] District** with spark-fueled black energy and broadcasting his ultimatum: *if he can't have the planeswalkers to ascend to godhood, he'll lay waste to all Ravnica.* He deploys his **God-Eternals** (the Amonkhet zombie-gods, metal-grafted): **[[Bontu]]** & **[[Kefnet]]** + the bulk of the Eternal horde to the **[[Boros Legion\|Boros]]/[[Izzet League\|Izzet]] front line**, and **[[Rhonas]]** + an army to the **Azorius**. (Oketra was killed earlier; [[Ogre]] holds its essence.) Knowing the **Azorius is likely lost** — and that if it falls, the horde gets a straight shot to the refugee-packed **[[Selesnya Conclave]]** (where **[[Janna]]** is) — the party chooses to **save the Boros/Izzet front**.

After **two short rests** (heals; Ogre's big Healing Words; hit dice — note: Ogre's familiar-blade is rebranded **"Dusk"**, post-darkness, less "emo"), they fly in on dragon-[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]. The God-Eternals open at half a mile: **[[Bontu]]** hurls a **god-metal spear** (Varis burns a **Legendary Resistance**; 34 necrotic, would've paralyzed), then **[[Kefnet]]** a golden spear — **[[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]]** (Hail-of-Thorns arrow, blessed) **knocks it off course (−2 DC)** and Varis saves (30 force, immune). The spears are forged of the same **Amonkhet god-metal** as Tezzeret's and **teleport back** to the wielder.

They land beside **[[Molly]]**, who's directing **Boros Angel Fire** and **Izzet artillery** against the Eternal **pyramids** climbing the walls. The party joins the defense (narrated as a running montage, not strict initiative):
- **[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]** **Force Breaths** pyramids off the wall (53 force; a Boros Angel salutes him).
- **[[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]]** lays **Faerie Fire** to mark kill-zones for Angel focus-fire (one Angel falls to zombie-archers).
- **[[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]]** rages — "ankle/wrist weights off" (Rock-Lee style), swinging **Blazebringer like a baseball bat** with **Barbaric Grace** (ballet/pirouette flavor), mowing Eternals off the battlements.
- **[[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]]** casts **Wall of Fire** (incinerating hundreds), **Holy Word** ("**fuck zombies**", radiant), and **Bless**; she's introduced to a grateful soldier ("the famous Breakfast Club!") who's then beheaded.
- **[[Ogre]]** **Mage-Hand-deflects [[Bontu]]'s returning spear** into ~25 Eternals; he also keeps angling to **grab a god-spear and teleport back to its god** (a recurring plan).
- **[[Dahlia Barra\|Dahlia]]** (Izzet) arrives in an **Emrakul/"Emla"-inspired mech battle-suit** (an energy-sword-whip, aetheric 50-ft slam, "feel the power of **9.8 m/s²**") and single-handedly plugs a wall breach. ([[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]] is *furious* with jealousy; Dahlia: "it's a prototype — I can make more.")

The fight drags ~an hour; **millions** of Eternals pour from **portal gates at Bolas's Citadel**. Bolas rakes a beam of black energy across the field — vaporizing *thousands of his own Eternals* and a stretch of battlements. Just as it tips toward collapse, **reinforcements arrive**: **[[Jareth Wig\|Jareth]]/[[Kwatli Pakal]]** opens a portal disgorging **hundreds of Ixalan pirates, merfolk, and priests** (incl. **Admiral [[Beckett Brass]]** and **Captain [[Lanary Storm]]**, who winks at Sanis) to plug the breach; **[[Narset]]** opens portals over the Azorius spilling **dragons** that firebomb the Eternals. Both fronts stabilize. Jareth's tally: *"Immortal Sun gone, planeswalkers off Ravnica, Bolas throwing a hissy fit — everything's going as planned."*

**The deeper crisis (Arcana — Rin 28, Varis 24):** Ravnica's **[[Leylines]] are unraveling.** Jace's death frayed them; Bolas's Citadel **taps** them; the cumulative stress (mass death, guild-leader upheavals, the Azorius destruction) is like cracks spreading through one pane of glass, threatening to **shatter the plane**. A **giant crater opens in the Azorius District**, exposing a raw **Leyline tentacle** that vaporizes dragons and Eternals alike. The party confirms **reforging the [[Guildpact]] would help stabilize the Leylines** (with the shreds + the box in hand). [[Ogre]] inventories his "MacGuffins": the **shreds of the First [[Guildpact]]** and the **[[Black Lotus]]** (a "**conditional wish spell**," condition unknown).

They break off the wall (now held by the allied/Ixalan/dragon forces) to fly toward the **Azorius**/the Leyline crater. **Cliffhanger:** a Leyline surge pulses across the Conclave and strikes the **Great Tree of [[Vitu-Ghazi]]** — and the **Great Tree begins to move, uprooting itself and standing up.** ([[Ogre]]: "Glad I made friends with that tree.") **Session ends.**
