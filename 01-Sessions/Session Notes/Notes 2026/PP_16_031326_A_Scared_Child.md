## 2. Orphie POV Overview

I have walked into rooms full of gods and zombies and never once felt what that black fog made me feel. It came up out of my own shadow wearing my father's voice, the old one, the one from before we ever made our peace, telling me I rush in too fast, that I'll get myself killed, that I'm doing it wrong like I always do. And just like that I wasn't a barbarian or a hero or a grown woman who's buried worse than this. I was a kid bracing for a hand I couldn't see coming. It dragged me down into the cold and the dread, and it carved a piece out of me that won't grow back tonight, and in the dark I did something I almost never do: I stopped reaching for the rage and reached for Athena instead, for whatever it is in me that's stronger than the fear. I asked for help. And for the first time, nobody answered. So I ran. Not proud, not strong, just a scared child getting out of a burning house.

And then Rin saved us all. The quiet one, the one who heals while the rest of us swing, looked her own ghost in the face, the bully who hit her when she was already down, and instead of breaking she remembered exactly who she is. She struck the ground and burned every one of those echoes to nothing with her god's own light, and the fog around Ogre tore away with them. We're still in it. The dragon's only halfway back into the world, the ceiling is starting to come down on our heads, and I'm hollowed out and shaking. But we shed our hatred to get into this place, and now it's wearing our worst memories and trying to take us apart with them, and we are still, somehow, standing.

## 3. Session Analysis

> Direct continuation of S15: the **[[Guildpact]] reforging** is underway, **[[Ogre]] is pulling [[Niv-Mizzet]] back through the ceiling portal**, and the **[[Augur of Bolas]]** (the corrupted-Leyline hatred-manifestation, born of the hatreds the party shed at the Hatred trial) has engaged them. **Initiative resumes.**

**Narrative Summary.** The **[[Augur of Bolas]]** ("Ogre of Bolas") opens by hurling a black bolt that loops past each PC's chest and strikes their **shadow** — seeding the fight's gimmick. As combat unfolds, it **cloaks itself in black smog** (granting it concealment; attacks against it at disadvantage), lashes with **solidified necrotic tendrils**, and **drags PCs into the mist** (cold, dread, life-draining). Crucially, it raises a **shadow-echo from each PC's shadow**, each wearing the face/voice of what that character hates most:
- [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]] — her **abusive father, pre-reconciliation**, scolding her ("you always rush in… you should fight ranged… it'll get you killed").
- [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]] — his **father**.
- [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]] — the **bully who beat her while she was down** (while Penny was away).
- [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] — his **high-school kidnappers** (masked).
- [[Ogre]] — **[[Nicol Bolas\|Bolas]]** himself.

Through the fight:
- **[[Ogre]]** stays at the portal, **pulling [[Niv-Mizzet]] through** (the dragon's claw, then arm, slowly emerge). He casts **Shadow of Moil** (becoming a shadow-wreathed "evil" version of himself — his blade now **"Dusk"**), and lands a **Mage-Hand Eldritch Blast crit** once the Augur is visible.
- **[[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]]** shoots with **Ava's Longbow** (using cover) and casts **Confusion** on the shadow-echoes (DC 18 — they fail; lash out randomly), saved from an opportunity-attack by [[Ogre]]'s **Cutting Words + Mage Hand**.
- **[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]** throws **Fireball**, a **4th-level Scorching Ray** (29 fire, quickened), and **Mind Sliver** — but the Augur **absorbs** the fire/psychic energy (black mist billowing from it).
- **[[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]]** has a brutal arc: dragged into the smog, her **max HP cut by 22** (to 41 max), she relives a **genuine childhood-abuse flashback** and — instead of raging — **prays to [[Athena]] for help**. The roll comes up empty (d4 = 1): ***"no help comes."*** She flees the mist as "a scared child," declining to fight further (a deliberate, in-character choice).
- **[[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]]** delivers the **turning point**: terrified by her bully's echo, she remembers *"I'm the champion of [[Dol Arrah]],"* slams the ground with **Channel Divinity** (30-ft radiant AoE, **28 dmg**) — **evaporating every shadow-echo** and **dispelling the black smog** cloaking the Augur (radiant light routs the dark). The Augur is visible and exposed.

The Augur **shrieks** (party-wide Con-save force damage), and the shriek **shakes the ceiling's stalactites** — threatening a collapse next round. **Session ends mid-fight** (a late weeknight): the **[[Augur of Bolas]] is hurt but standing** (absorbing energy), **[[Niv-Mizzet]] is still only partway through the portal**, and **falling stalactites loom.** [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]] ends drained (reduced max HP, no Greater Restoration available).
