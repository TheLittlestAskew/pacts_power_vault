**[[Session 1]]: The Breakfast Club** - The party was greeted in a cavern by a [[dark-robed magician]] who tethered the party to a crystal staff. Ancient and evil, the figure began untying the party’s golden tether of its knots, which unbound the party from reality and the plane of [[Planes of The Multiverse/Arcavios]]. - In a dreamlike trance, the party met the [[dragon paragons]] of Strixhaven, who freed the party and redirected them to a different plane. - The party flew through time and space, during which their most significant childhood moments played out before their eyes. - Their first memory together cemented them as “[[The Breakfast Club]],” when they defeated a [[Hag]] in their school library, the  [[Tin Street High School library]]. - The last scene that played in front of the party was back in [[Strixhaven]]: a vision of five robed figures restrained in front of the dark-robed magician, whom they no longer remember. - “The Breakfast Club” wakes back up, in a cave on the plane of [[Ravnica]], their reality forever changed.
