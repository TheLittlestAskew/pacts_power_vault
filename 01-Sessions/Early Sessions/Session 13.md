**[[Session 13]]: Brains vs. Braun

- The party made it through the sewer exit, chasing after the Gruul guard that escaped. Alerting the city, the [[Skaarg]] guard sound the siren and begin patrolling the streets for the party.
    
- The party splits into two, with the guards on [[Orphie]] and [[Sanis]]’s tail. Right when they were about to get caught, a mysterious person turns Orphie and Sanis invisible. After the guards were led away, the entire party reconvened and the mysterious person revealed herself as Penny, [[Rin]]’s best friend from childhood.
    

- [[Sanis]] confessed that he was a power bottom but that it was too much work.
    

- [[Penny]] explained that the [[Gruul]] leadership had been in flux since the Shattering of the [[Guildpact]]. She further explains that the [[Borborygmos]] clan was in shambles and shame after the embarrassment the party provided in saving [[EMLA]]. [[Clan Gor-tah]] had also been waning in power due to the death of the local hydra.
    
- A mysterious artificer and two assistants had taken over the clan and the construction of weird structures and a huge tower structure in the [[Rage Pits]].
    
- Penny then further explained that there were prisoners in the nearby encampment.
    
- The scene changed to where [[Braun]], played by Clay, was in prison with [[Ral Zarek]] and [[Isiah Ja-Faank]]. Braun then remembered how they got to prison in the first place.
    
- Ral went to the [[Boros]] academy and picked up the first recruit he found, which was Braun, to go to the Red Wastes to recover the stolen [[Izzet]] blueprints.
    
- On making it to the sewers, the duo were met with the towering, 15 headed hydra who charged at them. With their best attempts, Ral and Braun managed to dispatch the hydra, at cost of also being knocked out themselves. A [[Gruul]] patrol in the area, checking out the commotion, apprehended them.
    
- Later that night, the group ventured over to the prison and engaged the guards there, taking them out and saving Isiah, Ral, and Braun.
    
- After the battle, everyone introduced themselves, with Braun suddenly becoming the father-like figure of the group.
    

- And also being a silent horny.
    

- The group then discussed their intention to acquire the [[blueprints]], with Penny nowhere to be seen.
    

- Rin also told Ral to work with [[Dalya Bara]] to research and develop a cheap alternative/modification for [[jungle juice]], during which, they found out that you do not need to inject the juice into the horse’s rear, it could be injected anywhere there was a port.
    
- Ogre also received a Izzet-boy from Ral, with Pong and Snake on it.
    

- The group then decided to take a long rest.
