[[Session 9]]: Sparks Abound**

- [[The Breakfast Club]] made their way to [[New Prahv]] and the Azorius District. Their tavern landed outside its border, and they post up to think over their next move.
- The group found a red-and-green tracker inside the Drawer of Holding and, after mulling it over, destroyed it.
- Knowing that [[Clarissa]] was working as an accountant inside the Senate building, [[Orphie]] beast-sensed using [[Cerberus]]. Humming T Swift songs to herself, she went on to explore the Senate building. After maneuvering around the [[Azorius]] members and workers, Cerberus made their way to just outside Clarissa’s office, where Orphie heard her voice, confirming that she was alive and well. Clarissa wasn’t the only one forced to work: many other [[guildless]] workers were forced to work against their will in the Azorius Senate in order to keep the organization functioning.
- Before Cereberus could leave, Orphie and Cereberus met a large blue-and-white, imposing [[Sphinx]], who could be deduced to be [[Isperia]]. Orphie was unable to deduce Isperia’s disposition or intentions. Orphie overheard that, due to [[Dovin Baan]]’s death, the Azorius Senate was in debate over who would be the new [[guildmaster]]: [[Lavinias]] or Isperia.
- The group then headed over to [[Nivix]] and the [[Izzet]] headquarters. Once there, they meet with [[Dalya Bara]] and her lab in Metallurgy. They see [[Emla]] again and discuss the state of the guild. Apparently, the Labs of Orientation and Storms and Electricity were all broken into.
- Niv Mizzet appeared and asked the group as to why someone would break into the labs. The only lead that they have is in [[The Red Wastes]]. [[Niv Mizzet]] then lead the group to [[the Lab of Storms and Electricity]] and unlocked their [[planeswalking]] spark, save for [[Lance]], who was passed out back in the tavern. Niv Mizzet shocked everyone with the power of electricity and reduced them all to 1 HP.
- The party then went to Emla to rest, where they opened back up to [[Planes of The Multiverse/Arcavios]], and where a black-robed figure could see them.
