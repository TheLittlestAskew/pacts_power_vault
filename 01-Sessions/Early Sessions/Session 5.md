**[[Session 5]]: Battle**

- Surprised by the [[Golgari]] [[Rhizome Lurcher]] that burst from the ground, [[Ogre]], [[Varis]], and [[Sanis]] fought for their lives, all while trying to find [[Salena]], Sanis' sister.
- Meanwhile, [[Lance]], [[Rin]], and [[Orphie]] unearthed [[Gideon“Blackblade” Jura]] from the rubble. In turn, he blessed the group with Protection from Good and Evil.
- After Gideon, Lance, Rin, and Orphie arrived on the scene, the gang found Salena and fled from the battle, as their old friend [[Isiah Ja-Faank]] led the 8+ enemies away.
- At this point, the group split once again, with Sanis, Rin, Varis, and Salena running off to the [[Selesnyan Conclave]] in order to get medical attention for Salena, who was in dire straits.
- Ogre, Orphie, and Lance made their way to Lance’s father and the [[Blistercoils]], which had been devastated by a massive explosion.
- Though split, the party used their [[Sending Stones]] to communicate and pressed onward to their destinations.
