[[Session 3]]: Foreboding Fortunes** - [[The Breakfast Club]] is escorted to the [[Azorius]] [[Eighth Precinct Jail]]. There, they meet [[Isiah Ja-Faank]], whose tall, dark, and handsome persona, coupled with his haughty attitude, wooed [[Orphie]]. Meanwhile, [[Varis]] trailed the [[Dimir]] insurgent and received a couple of items, for reasons unknown to the group. - Attempting to talk his way through to freeing his friends, Varis was turned away. Unswayed, he re-entered using invisibility, but ended up being revealed by a Door of Warding, which removed his invisibility. - Thinking on his feet, Isaiah magically beckoned the nearby guard to him and falcon-punched him to steal the jail keys. The group ran over to Varis, and they made their escape using some Dust of Disappearance that he acquired from the Dimir agent. - Back in the [[10th District]], the group landed at [[Sanis]]’ family mansion, where they enjoyed hearty food, had some target practice, and watched the Spring Festival fireworks. - The group made their way to the Great Concourse of [[Selesnya]] and had their visions foretold in [[The Great Tree of Vitu-Ghazi]]. The oracle, after some persuading, in clear language, told them that the entire universe was in danger and that they were intimately involved. The oracle then passed out.
