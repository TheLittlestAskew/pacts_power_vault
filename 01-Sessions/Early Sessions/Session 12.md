**[[Session 12]]: A Treatise with [[Treants]]**

- As the group made their way to [[The Red Wastes]], they found [[Lance]]’s letter in the [[Traveling Tavern]], expressing Lance’s desire for introspection with a focus on himself and his father. Lance promised that he would re-join the party at a later date if he could.
- Making it to the outskirts of the Red Wastes, the group then entered the ring of thicket surrounding the Red Wastes. Though their journey through the trees was uneventful, they were spooked by a certain energy. [[Ogre]] scouted from the treetops and noticed smoke coming from the direction they were headed in.
- After they made it out the other side, [[The Breakfast Club]] was greeted by mechanically-cut trees and burnt trees. Before they were able to move on, they were ambushed by twisted, burnt treants that were corrupted by their felling and that of their kin.
- The party engaged the errant treants, defeating one, before friendly treants of the forest, Hont and Borv, arrived on the scene to dispatch the other corrupted treant, Peter.
- From [[Hont and Borv]], the party learned that even before [[the shattering of the Guildpact]], the [[Gruul]] had been cutting down trees for weapons for their eventual attack on The [[10th District]]. As a thank you for the party’s help, the treants volunteered to take the party to the sewers of the [[Skaarg Pits]].
- Once at the sewers, the [[Hydra]] that they had met last time was now skeleton and bones, with an End Raze Forerunner and trio of [[Rubblebelt Boars]] scavenging the area.
- After they stealthily made their way away from the porcine pillagers, the group ventured through the sewers to a pair of guards at the exit. Taking no chances, [[Sanis]] jumped one of the guards and turned the encounter into a skirmish. The group took one of the guards out, but the other managed to escape into the exit passage leading to the Skaarg.
