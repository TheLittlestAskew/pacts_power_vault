**[[Session 6]]: Dank and Dirty**

- The "[[Blistercoils]] Group" (consisting of [[Lance]], [[Orphie]], and [[Ogre]]), ventured to the Blistercoils and noticed that the [[Fortress of Nivix]], bastion and headquarters of the [[Izzet]] guild, had been attacked. Once they arrived at Lance’s house, they discovered Lance’s father unconscious. After taking him to the Izzet emergency medical encampment, they head to the [[Selesnyan Conclave]].
- The [[Selesnyan]] group (consisting of [[Rin]], [[Varis]], [[Sanis]], and [[Salena]]), noticed both the [[Boros]] and [[Azorius]] guild districts were fully mobilized. Once at the conclave, they handed Salena over to the Selesnyan surgeons, who tragically could not save her. Dejected, the group make their way to Rin and [[Orphie]]’s apartment to have a long rest.
- The next morning, the reunited party all met Varis’ parents in [[Orzhova]] and on Lance’s tip, made their way to tunnels under the [[Kamen fortress]].
- Upon entering a cavern in the tunnel, they avoided some [[Drudge Beetles]] and [[Poisonous Groodions]]. But before they were able to leave, a massive, mawing [[Rotworm]] burst through the exit, blocking their path.
