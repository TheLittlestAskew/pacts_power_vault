**[[Session 4]]: Welcome to the Plot Parade**

- [[The Breakfast Club]] made their way to the [[Spring Festival]] and enjoyed its food and variety of games. While half of the group decided to chase down [[A Shady Individual]], who turned out to be Rin’s childhood friend, the other half successfully caught a greased-up pig.
- Later in the day, the gang witnessed a drunken [[Jace]] give the Living Guildpact’s Spring Festival speech, but during its concluding message, he was ambushed. Jace and his friends, the [[Gatewatch]], ended up fighting against three conspiring guild leaders of [[Ravnica]]. The gang provided supporting fire and distractions during the fight, but alas, Jace was slain and his very soul was absorbed by [[Vraska]], guild leader of the [[Golgari]]
- The shattering of the [[Living Guildpact]] opened the plane to widespread chaos, with [[Gruul]] legions and Golgari creatures destroying buildings and murdering innocent people in the city's central plaza.
- [[Sanis]], [[Ogre]], and [[Varis]] ran off to where they left Sanis’ little sister, [[Salena]], but were greeted by a hulking [[Gruul]] spellbreaker and Golgari [[Rhizome Lurcher]] that burst from underground.
