**[[Session 7]]: [[Steve]]**

- The massive, mawing [[Rotworm]] that blocked [[The Breakfast Club]]’s path soared towards the opposite side of the cavern, chasing the groodions and beetles towards the party.
- After a few rounds of combat, the Rotworm woke up and introduced itself as Steve. The group then helped Steve kill the beetles and [[Groodions]] for food. [[Rin]] and [[Varis]] prepared some Ragout de Scarabee aux Herbes out of one of the killed beetles for [[Steve]], and in thanks, Steve gave the party one of his teeth to summon him whenever they need him.
- The group then made their way to [[Wayport]] and [[Sanis]] Reylana’s family mansion, where everything was too quiet. Sneaking into the secret basement, Sanis and [[Lance]] discovered a cult-like ritual going on and, upon its completion, the room was engulfed in true darkness.
- The rest of the party prepared [[Ogre’s traveling tavern]] that was parked in the [[Reylana mansion]] garage. After the group rendezvoused, they inserted a [[Jungle Juice]] canister into their Izzet horse [[Scrappy]]'s behind and took off into the sky. Just before they left, Sanis spotted a [[mysterious box]] being carried into a carriage on the other side of the mansion.
