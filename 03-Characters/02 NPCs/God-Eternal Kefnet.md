# God-Eternal Kefnet

A massive undead god of [[Amonkhet]], reanimated as a **God-Eternal** in service of [[Nicol Bolas]] and the [[The Second Sun|Second Sun]]. One of the metal-grafted Amonkhet zombie-gods Bolas unleashed on [[Ravnica]], deployed alongside [[God-Eternal Bontu|Bontu]] to shell the [[Boros Legion|Boros]]/[[Izzet League|Izzet]] front line.

> **Spelling note:** canonical page form **God-Eternal Kefnet** (matching [[God-Eternal Oketra]]).

## Campaign Role
A god-tier ranged threat in Bolas's invasion. In [[PP_14_021326_Hold_the_Wall|PP 14]] he and [[God-Eternal Bontu|Bontu]] open at half a mile with enormous **god-metal spears** of the same [[god-metal|Amonkhet metal]] as [[Tezzeret]]'s gear, which **teleport back to the wielder.**

## Abilities & Combat Notes
- Hurled a **golden god-metal spear** at dragon-[[Varis Aestra|Varis]] — **[[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] knocked it off course** with a blessed Hail-of-Thorns arrow (18 with advantage; **−2 to the DC**), and Varis saved (30 force; immune).
- His spears are **forged of Amonkhet god-metal** and **teleport back** to his hand after striking.

## Key Events

### [[PP_14_021326_Hold_the_Wall|PP 14 — Hold the Wall]] (02/13/26)
- Deployed by [[Nicol Bolas]] (with [[God-Eternal Bontu|Bontu]] + the bulk of the Eternal horde) to the **[[Boros Legion|Boros]]/[[Izzet League|Izzet]] front line.**
- Threw a **golden god-metal spear** at dragon-[[Varis Aestra|Varis]]; **[[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]'s** blessed Hail-of-Thorns arrow **knocked it off course (−2 DC)**, and Varis saved (Dex save adv 20 → 30 force, immune).
- Still standing at session's end alongside [[God-Eternal Bontu|Bontu]], [[God-Eternal Rhonas|Rhonas]], and [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] as the party breaks off the held wall.
