# Kaya Cassir

## Description / Appearance
Human planeswalker and ghost assassin. Full name confirmed as Kaya Cassir per MTG canon. "Kaya" is acceptable shorthand.

## Backstory
Kaya is a planeswalker with the ability to interact with and destroy ghosts. She became the Obzedat's enforcer at some point prior to the campaign's current events. She killed [[Szadek]] (confirmed by the Glass Staircase of Truth in Session 01). She had been magically tethered to [[Sanis]] prior to Session 01.

## Abilities & Class Features
- Planeswalking
- Ghost interaction/destruction
- Tether magic (was tethered to Sanis; now severed)

## Relationships
- [[Sanis]] — Was magically tethered to him. Tether severed in Session 01.
- [[Szadek]] — Killed him. Confirmed by Ghost Mind Glass Staircase.
- [[Orzhov Syndicate]] — Former Obzedat enforcer.
