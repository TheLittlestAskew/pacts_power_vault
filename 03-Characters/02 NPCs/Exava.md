# Exava

The **Bloodwitch of [[Cult of Rakdos|Rakdos]]** — introduced in the DM's **#Lore Dump** (08/20/23) alongside the Rakdos Harlequin **[[Hikara|Hekara]]**. A blood-magic priestess of the Cult of Rakdos.

## Campaign Role
One of the named faces of the **[[Cult of Rakdos]]** — the guild of chaos and performance whose sleeping demon-god **[[Rakdos]]** the party later helped rouse (PP 7). Part of the Rakdos contingent that includes Hekara and the party's contact **[[A Shady Individual|Penny]]**.
