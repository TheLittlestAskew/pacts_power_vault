# Tezzeret

A scheming artificer and lieutenant of [[Nicol Bolas]] — a **cyborg** with a biomechanical right hand and red eyes. Long an enemy of [[The Breakfast Club]] (the crystal-tower affair) and the **torturer of [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]]**. Killed by [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] at the [[Bone Mine]] in [[PP_05_090725_Do_You_Smell_That_Its_Breakfast_Time|Transcript 05]].

> Transcripts render his name as "Tessera / Tezzaret / Tezara," etc. Canonical: **Tezzeret**.

## Description / Appearance
A black-and-red figure with a **biomechanical right hand** and **red eyes**. Half man, half metal — the Myconids described him only as a "human machine, arms of metal."

## Backstory (party-known)
- The schemer at the top of the **crystal tower**, where a giant crystal absorbed energies and exploded (an earlier party encounter).
- **Took the [[Immortal Sun]]** (recalled by the party in T05).
- **Tortured [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]]** during her captivity tied to [[Strixhaven]].

## Abilities Observed (Transcript 05)
- **Regeneration:** weapon wounds knit themselves back up; Orphie's axe "kept bouncing off."
- **Essence-drain tendrils:** black, electricity-wreathed tendrils that deal necrotic **and reduce the target's hit-point maximum** ("stealing essence").
- **Domination / mind control:** forced [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]] (Wis save w/ disadvantage) to attack the party; also Command-charmed [[Rin]].
- Conjures **black-and-red bolts** (counterspelled by [[Ogre]]).
- Corrupted the Myconid **essence pits** via installed pumps feeding a small black portal.

## Death (Transcript 05, 09/07/25)
Found at the [[Bone Mine]]'s marrow/essence pits, corrupting them for [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] behind three corrupted giant Myconids. After dominating Chandra and draining [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]]'s essence twice, he was overwhelmed: [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]'s Phantasmal Force rattled him, [[Ogre]] counterspelled him, [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] breathed force at point blank, and **Orphie decapitated him with [[Blazebringer]] in a double nat-20 crit for 114 damage** ("Do you smell that? It's breakfast time"). His severed head whispered **"The harvest is coming."** [[Ogre]] could not claim his soul.
