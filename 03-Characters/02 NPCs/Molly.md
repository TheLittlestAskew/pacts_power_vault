# Molly

## Description / Appearance
A [[Boros Legion]] officer/commander. In [[PP_02_072725_I_Cant_Rebuild_You|Transcript 02]] she is seen at Boros HQ giving orders to couriers and runners amid the chaos of the renewed war — visibly frazzled but holding the line.

## Backstory
A familiar ally to the party from prior sessions; a ranking Boros commander coordinating the allied defense of the [[Tenth District]] against the [[The Second Sun|Second Sun]]'s forces. The Campaign Dashboard lists her as the [[Boros Legion]]'s potential representative for the Guildpact ritual.

## Personality
Pragmatic, overworked, wry under pressure. Greets the party warmly as familiar faces and is candid about how dire the situation is.

## Relationships
- [[Boros Legion]] — Commanding officer.
- [[The Breakfast Club]] — Trusted ally; relays intelligence and coordinates with the party.
- [[House Dimir]] — Relies on Dimir contacts for intelligence; those contacts have gone silent (a consequence of the party's [[Ghost Mind]] destruction).

## Key Events

### [[PP_02_072725_I_Cant_Rebuild_You|Transcript 02 — I Can't Rebuild You]] (07/27/2025)
- Briefed the party at Boros HQ on the overnight [[Eternals]] invasion across the DMZ and the [[Gruul Clans|Gruul]] betrayal from the north
- Reported her [[House Dimir|Dimir]] intelligence contacts had gone silent ([[Rin]] admitted the party caused this by destroying the [[Ghost Mind]])
- Had no information on the [[Gatewatch]], [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]], or [[Gideon "Blackblade" Jura|Gideon]]
- Agreed to relay any word of Chandra or Gideon to the party

### [[PP_03_081025_Thats_Why_No_One_Will_Remember_Your_Name|Transcript 03 — That's Why No One Will Remember Your Name]] (08/10/2025)
- Arrived commanding **Boros Skyknight Legionnaires** on griffins to reinforce the front ("Carve a path to the God-Eternal"); her wing turned the immediate battle and helped bring down [[Oketra]] (though [[Oketra]] killed two of her griffin-riders)
- After Oketra fell and [[Niv-Mizzet]] died, urged the party to **fall back and regroup** rather than charge [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]; began fortifying the line with [[Izzet League|Izzet]] armored vehicles and [[Selesnya Conclave|Selesnya]] healers
- [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] needled her for the cautious call ("that's why nobody will remember your name")

### [[PP_04_082625_The_Spark_and_the_Swarm|Transcript 04 — The Spark and the Swarm]] (08/26/2025)
- Found **atop the barricades directing the fortified front-line defense** (Izzet makeshift walls, [[EMLA]] units, Boros Angels); the party spotted her on a Perception check (all but [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]])
- Waved the party through the lines with a resigned **"good luck"**, knowing they'd go regardless
- Gave a scouting report: the [[Eternals]] front line had **pulled back** after the party's help downing [[God-Eternal Oketra\|Oketra]], but the [[Golgari Swarm\|Golgari]] district is densely populated and heavily damaged

### [[PP_14_021326_Hold_the_Wall|PP 14 — Hold the Wall]] (02/13/26)
- **Held the Bulwark.** Found on the [[Izzet League|Izzet]]/[[Boros Legion|Boros]] battlements directing **Boros Angel Fire** and **Izzet artillery** against the Eternal **pyramids** climbing the wall; the party landed beside her and joined the defense.
- Her line held through the God-Eternal spear-barrage ([[God-Eternal Bontu|Bontu]]/[[God-Eternal Kefnet|Kefnet]]) and a friendly-fire beam from [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]], stabilizing once **[[Kwatli Pakal|Jareth]]'s [[Ixalan]] armies** and **[[Narset]]'s dragons** arrived. A Boros Angel saluted dragon-[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] for clearing pyramids off the wall.
- Stands as the [[Boros Legion]]'s candidate representative for the Guildpact ritual.

### [[PP_18_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 18 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26)
- **At the grand council** of all ten guilds (surfaced [[Maze's End]]), Molly attends for the **[[Boros Legion]]** alongside the angelic guildmaster **[[Aurelia]]** as the guilds put their forces on the ritual die to formally unite against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]].

### [[PP_19_041926_Hi_Im_Ogre|PP 19 — Hi, I'm Ogre]] (04/19/26)
- **Duels a God-Eternal at the summit — [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]'s pick.** Chosen by [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] as one of the party's personal allies for the Citadel climb (paladin / his love interest). Molly + **Boros griffin-riders** (her griffin **"Buddy"**) duel the Chain-Veil-controlled **God-Eternals** overhead at the **400-ft pyramid** — most of her griffins are shot from the sky, but she **eventually grapples [[God-Eternal Bontu|Bontu]] down onto the pyramid** to fight.
- [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] **support-fires her fight** (Lightning Arrow, 23+14 — she takes the splash) and she yells back *"keep going, it's okay."* **Holding strong, mid-duel with Bontu** as the session ends (continues into the finale).

### [[PP_20_051426_I_Just_Need_Your_Soul|PP 20 — I Just Need Your Soul]] (05/14/26)
- **Duels God-Eternal [[God-Eternal Bontu|Bontu]]** at the Citadel summit (with [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] supporting) — a near-unkillable god-tier brawl. Molly is **bloodied, then healed**; she keeps fighting regardless of her own life: *"I fight for Ravnica. My death means nothing if this plane doesn't exist."*
- When the party **frees [[Liliana Vess|Liliana]]** and she flips the God-Eternals against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]], Molly's brawl with Bontu turns from a holding action into an asset for the finale (05/31). [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] protective/healing of her continues their thread (*"who's gonna go on dates with me?"*). *(Session ends mid-encounter — do not import the finale.)*

### [[PP_21_053126_I_Miss_You_Buddy|PP 21 — I Miss You, Buddy]] (05/31/26)
- **Duels a God-Eternal through the whole battle — and survives.** In the finale Molly keeps **dueling a God-Eternal** at the summit (down, healed, back up, repeatedly) as part of the war of attrition that wears Bolas down — *"the healer who carried the woman she loves back from the dark"* (Orphie's POV nods to the Molly/Sanis thread). **No PC or Molly death.**
- ⭐ **Epilogue — the partner who stays.** In the tavern epilogue **Molly asks [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] to dinner next week** — at last he *keeps* a partner. Their long-running love-interest thread finally lands.
