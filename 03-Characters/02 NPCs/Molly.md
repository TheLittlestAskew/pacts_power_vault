# Molly

## Description / Appearance
A [[Boros Legion]] officer/commander. In [[PP_02_072725_I_Cant_Rebuild_You|Transcript 02]] she is seen at Boros HQ giving orders to couriers and runners amid the chaos of the renewed war — visibly frazzled but holding the line.

## Backstory
A familiar ally to the party from prior sessions; a ranking Boros commander coordinating the allied defense of the [[Tenth District]] against the [[The Second Sun|Second Sun]]'s forces. The Campaign Dashboard lists her as the [[Boros Legion]]'s potential representative for the Guildpact ritual.

## Personality
Pragmatic, overworked, wry under pressure. Greets the party warmly as familiar faces and is candid about how dire the situation is.

## Relationships
- [[Boros Legion]] — Commanding officer.
- [[The Breakfast Club]] — Trusted ally; relays intelligence and coordinates with the party.
- [[House Dimir]] — Relies on Dimir contacts for intelligence; those contacts have gone silent (a consequence of the party's [[Ghost Mind]] destruction).

## Key Events

### [[PP_02_072725_I_Cant_Rebuild_You|Transcript 02 — I Can't Rebuild You]] (07/27/2025)
- Briefed the party at Boros HQ on the overnight [[Eternals]] invasion across the DMZ and the [[Gruul Clans|Gruul]] betrayal from the north
- Reported her [[House Dimir|Dimir]] intelligence contacts had gone silent ([[Rin]] admitted the party caused this by destroying the [[Ghost Mind]])
- Had no information on the [[Gatewatch]], [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]], or [[Gideon "Blackblade" Jura|Gideon]]
- Agreed to relay any word of Chandra or Gideon to the party

### [[PP_03_081025_Thats_Why_No_One_Will_Remember_Your_Name|Transcript 03 — That's Why No One Will Remember Your Name]] (08/10/2025)
- Arrived commanding **Boros Skyknight Legionnaires** on griffins to reinforce the front ("Carve a path to the God-Eternal"); her wing turned the immediate battle and helped bring down [[Oketra]] (though [[Oketra]] killed two of her griffin-riders)
- After Oketra fell and [[Niv-Mizzet]] died, urged the party to **fall back and regroup** rather than charge [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]; began fortifying the line with [[Izzet League|Izzet]] armored vehicles and [[Selesnya Conclave|Selesnya]] healers
- [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] needled her for the cautious call ("that's why nobody will remember your name")
