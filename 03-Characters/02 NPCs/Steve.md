# Steve

A colossal subterranean rot wurm allied with [[Ogre]], summoned by breaking **Steve's tooth**. Enormous and growing — the party has never seen his head and tail in the same place; estimated at 60–70 ft when first met, far longer now. Speaks in grand, archaic declarations.

## Description / Appearance
A gargantuan burrowing wurm that travels underground and erupts to devour enemies. When [[Ogre]] feeds him an enlarge mushroom he roughly doubles in size (described as "the size of the whole city").

## Abilities & Combat Notes
- Burrows through the earth; erupts to swallow enemies whole (devoured ~7 [[Eternals]] in T02)
- Can be enlarged via [[Ogre]]'s mushrooms (~1 hour)
- "Steamroll" rolling attack across the battlefield
- Enlarging him while underground may cause infrastructure/earthquake damage

## Relationships
- [[Ogre]] — Master/friend; summons and rides him.
- [[The Breakfast Club]] — Beloved ally ("We love Steve").

## Key Events

### [[PP_02_072725_I_Cant_Rebuild_You|Transcript 02 — I Can't Rebuild You]] (07/27/2025)
- Summoned at the front via Steve's tooth; enlarged by [[Ogre]]'s mushroom
- Erupted from a tunnel and devoured [[Eternals]] (3 on emergence; +4 on a 4d8=18 roll)
- Part of "Operation Ogre-Sized" alongside dragon-[[Varis Aestra|Varis]]

### [[PP_03_081025_Thats_Why_No_One_Will_Remember_Your_Name|Transcript 03 — That's Why No One Will Remember Your Name]] (08/10/2025)
- Continued eating [[Eternals]] on the front (still doubled from the T02 embiggening mushroom)
- Helped restrain **God-Eternal [[God-Eternal Oketra|Oketra]]** by coiling her legs, upgrading her from grappled to **restrained**, so the party could kill her
- After the kill, **rushed off toward [[Bolas's Citadel]]**, leaving a ginormous tunnel; departed without leaving a tooth (the party had hoped for another)

### [[PP_17_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 17 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26)
- **Now the Golgari's tunnel route — ridden by [[Yarod Votsavo]].** Steve has been **tunneling under Ravnica with [[Dalya Bara|Dahlia]]** but can't reach [[Bolas's Citadel]]; he appears at the **grand council** at the surfaced [[Maze's End]] with the new Golgari leader **[[Yarod Votsavo]]** (the Golgari Lich) riding atop him.
- **His kin caged.** Steve's **ancient Rotworm relatives** are trapped in the **[[The Rot Warren|Rot Warren]]** (the Golgari spawning grounds) by [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]'s force-field dome — the reason the party drills down there.
- **Freed.** After the party destroyed the **6 pylons** and dropped the force field, **Steve + Yarod surfaced** to rally the freed ancient Rotworms — now able to **tunnel a straight path to [[Bolas's Citadel]]** for the final assault.
