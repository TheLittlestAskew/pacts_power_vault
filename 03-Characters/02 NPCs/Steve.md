# Steve

A colossal subterranean rot wurm allied with [[Ogre]], summoned by breaking **Steve's tooth**. Enormous and growing — the party has never seen his head and tail in the same place; estimated at 60–70 ft when first met, far longer now. Speaks in grand, archaic declarations.

## Description / Appearance
A gargantuan burrowing wurm that travels underground and erupts to devour enemies. When [[Ogre]] feeds him an enlarge mushroom he roughly doubles in size (described as "the size of the whole city").

## Abilities & Combat Notes
- Burrows through the earth; erupts to swallow enemies whole (devoured ~7 [[Eternals]] in T02)
- Can be enlarged via [[Ogre]]'s mushrooms (~1 hour)
- "Steamroll" rolling attack across the battlefield
- Enlarging him while underground may cause infrastructure/earthquake damage

## Relationships
- [[Ogre]] — Master/friend; summons and rides him.
- [[The Breakfast Club]] — Beloved ally ("We love Steve").
