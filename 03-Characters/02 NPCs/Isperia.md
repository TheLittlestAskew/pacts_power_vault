# Isperia

The **Supreme Judge and Guildmaster of the [[Azorius Senate|Azorius]]** — introduced in the DM's **#Lore Dump** (10/08/23) alongside the Azorius officer **[[Lavinia]]**.

## Campaign Role
The head of the law-and-order guild. Azorius (its district later razed by [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]], its Senate HQ cut in half in PP 13) is one of the ten guilds whose token the party needed for the [[Niv-Mizzet]] / [[Guildpact]] revival; Isperia is its guild-leader figure, with [[Lavinia]] as a key lieutenant.
