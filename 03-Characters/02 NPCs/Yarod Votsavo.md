# Yarod Votsavo

The **Golgari Lich** — [[Vraska]]'s predecessor as a power of the [[Golgari Swarm]]. He implanted **[[Argo Lot]]**'s head/essence into [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]], giving Sanis his **Dhampir powers** and binding him by **pact to kill [[Vraska]]**.

## Campaign Role
The buried origin of Sanis's vampiric/Dhampir nature and of a dormant pact-obligation that resurfaces inside Vraska's own war camp. A Golgari necromantic power who came before Vraska's leadership.

## Key Events

### [[PP_07_100525_The_Reylana_Infiltration|PP 7 — The Reylana Infiltration]] (10/05/25)
- Surfaced in [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]'s recovered (forgotten-lore) memory inside the [[Reylana mansion|Reylana Estate]]: the Golgari Lich **implanted [[Argo Lot]]'s head/essence into Sanis**, the source of his **Dhampir powers**, and **bound him by pact to kill [[Vraska]]** (the Lich's successor).

### [[PP_13_012526_Fucking_Barbarians|PP 13 — Fucking Barbarians]] (01/25/26)
- **The Golgari-Lich Pact resolved.** The voice urging [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]] mid-fight (*"Do it… fulfill the pact… finish her"*) was the Lich — **Yorvo Vod Savo**, the first Golgari Guild Leader / lich. Backstory confirmed: he **resurrected Sanis as a dhampir** by infusing the spirit of the long-dead Dimir Guildmaster **[[Szadek]]** into him; the price was **kill [[Vraska]]** and deliver her **head as tribute**.
- After [[Vraska]] was killed and decapitated, Sanis (compelled) **took her head and it disintegrated** — *"Pact fulfilled."* The pact is **severed**; the last independent shred of **[[Argo Lot]]** then merged fully into Sanis, and Sanis **unlocked his full dhampir potential** (permanent +10 ft speed → 40, plus a new ability).
- **Spelling note (raw transcript):** rendered **"Yorvo Vod Savo"** this session (canonical page **Yarod Votsavo**, alias kept).

### [[PP_18_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 18 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26)
- **Surfaces as the new Golgari Swarm leader.** With [[Vraska]] dead (PP 13), Yarod Votsavo now **leads the [[Golgari Swarm]]** — appearing atop **[[Steve]]** the Rotworm at the **grand council** at the surfaced [[Maze's End]], representing Golgari among the ten guilds uniting against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]. (This resolves the open "Golgari leadership now open" gap from S13 — the Golgari seat is filled by the Lich.)
- **The Rot Warren.** He and Steve guided the party (via the [[The Drillinator|Drillinator]]) to the **[[The Rot Warren|Rot Warren]]**, the Golgari spawning grounds where **Steve's ancient Rotworm kin were caged** by Bolas's force-field dome. After the party dropped the dome (6 pylons), **Yarod and Steve surfaced to rally the freed ancient Rotworms** — now able to **tunnel a path to [[Bolas's Citadel]].**
- [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]'s **Golgari-Lich pact** continues to feel like it's "coming to fruition" as the Golgari rise under Yarod's leadership (cf. the surface Golgari surge in PP 17).
- **Spelling note (raw transcript):** rendered "Yirad/Urod Votsavo" (canonical **Yarod Votsavo**, alias kept).

## The Golgari-Lich Pact (lore)
The dormant obligation carried inside [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]] since his resurrection: the Lich made Sanis a **dhampir** (via the [[Szadek]] spirit + an [[Argo Lot]] essence-fragment) in exchange for the **death of [[Vraska]]**, the Lich's successor as a Golgari power, with her **head delivered as tribute**. Fulfilled in PP 13 — tribute disintegrated, pact closed, Sanis made whole/"just himself."
