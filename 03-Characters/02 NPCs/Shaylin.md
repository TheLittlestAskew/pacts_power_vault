# Shaylin

The **leader of [[Ravnica Trading Incorporated]]** and a Tarkir ally tied to **[[Narset]]**'s crew. Her company runs the carriages and logistics behind Narset's heist to take the **[[Immortal Sun]]** off-plane.

> Spelling unconfirmed (raw transcript); canonical page form **Shaylin**.

## Campaign Role

A behind-the-scenes ally surfacing as the party reconstructs who pulled off the Immortal Sun heist. [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]] recalls (nat-20) that the **[[Ravnica Trading Incorporated]]** carriage leaving the auction belongs to **Shaylin's** company — [[Narset]]'s Tarkir troop (which includes the doppelganger [[Kalmar]] and likely the red-haired driver [[Rekka]]). All read as allies.
