# Carl

A meek, gilled aquatic janitor at the [[Second Sun]]'s underwater [[Simic Combine|Simic]] research facility ([[Second Sun Labs]]) — originally from "**Zonath 3**" (a Simic Zonot). Mocked and demeaned by the lab's scientists, he is befriended and emboldened by the party in PP 8.

## Campaign Role

Carl is the party's moral-compass beat in the drowned horror of the Simic labs — "the only decent soul in the whole place." [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] and [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]] champion him against the jeering [[Second Sun]] researchers, telling him he holds the building together and should be the highest-paid person there. A likely recurring ally and a potential foothold inside the Simic facility (de-facto Simic contact).

## Key Events

### [[PP_08_101925_Blood_Runs_Thicker_Than_Water|PP 8 — Blood Runs Thicker Than Water]] (10/19/25)
- Gave the party a tour of the underwater [[Second Sun Labs]] after the gatekeeper [[Larissa]] (offscreen) cleared them in.
- Nervous to enter the vivarium because the scientists mock him; [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] (Persuasion adv 14) and [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]] talked him into finishing the tour — *"you hold this building together… you should be the highest-paid person here."*
- When the researchers jeered and threw **anchovy pizza** at his feet, [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] picked it up and **slapped it down the lead scientist's face** (unarmed strike, 30) and told Carl *"don't clean that one up."*
- Found his courage and stood up to the staff: *"I clean your messes, so you better respect that."* The party asked for a photo of him (a keepsake/running gag).

### [[PP_09_113025_What_Are_You_Waiting_For_Sanis|PP 09 — What Are You Waiting For, Sanis?]] (11/30/25)
- **Not present in the fight** (on the other side of the door). When the [[Second Sun Labs|lab]] collapsed during the [[Lord Reylana|Reylana]] battle, the party determined (Rin, Insight) **their fight did not cause it** — *something else* did.
- **Suspected of blowing up the lab** — the "we radicalized him" janitor gag deepens into a running theory that Carl is the saboteur. **Whereabouts unknown.** (Unconfirmed; no evidence shown.)

### [[PP_17_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 17 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26)
- **The de-facto Simic leader.** At the **grand council** of all ten guilds (surfaced [[Maze's End]]), Carl — the Simic crab the party once befriended and accidentally doomed — **shuffles in as the only Simic present.** The party's logic: *"if he's here, he's the guild leader."* The tragicomic janitor is now, by default, the **[[Simic Combine]]** representative, filling the long-open Simic gap for the coalition.
- Joined the other guild reps in **putting Simic's energies on the ritual die** to formally unite against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]. (Confirmed **de-facto Simic leader** in the session's leadership flag.)
