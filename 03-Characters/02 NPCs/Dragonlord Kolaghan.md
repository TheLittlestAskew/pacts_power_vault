# Dragonlord Kolaghan

The **ruler of the former Mardu** — a Dragonlord of **[[Tarkir]]**, introduced in the DM's **#Lore Dump** (11/17/24). The Mardu clan is referenced in the **statue passwords** ("Mardu") at *Amara, the Draconic Demon Blade* (see [[Passwords & Riddles]]).

## Campaign Role
Part of the **[[Tarkir]]** dragon-lore woven through the campaign's draconic threads — the plane [[Ugin]] hails from and **mantles [[Varis Aestra|Varis]]** with at the finale ([[PP_20_053126_I_Miss_You_Buddy|PP 20]]). Mardu and the Dragonlords frame the Tarkir backdrop behind Varis's "Ancestral Draconic Bulwark" and the statue-password puzzle.
