# Ugin

The **Spirit Dragon** — an ancient planeswalker and the counterpart/sibling-figure to [[Nicol Bolas]] in "a brother's war of dragons." His essence appears throughout the campaign (the white flame in [[Varis Aestra|Varis]]'s and [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]'s magic; a strand of his spirit powered [[Salena|Selena]]'s cure in T05). He delivers the campaign's central lore-dump in [[PP_07_100525_The_Reylana_Infiltration|Transcript 07]].

## Campaign Role
The party's distant patron of dragon-power and the source of the **Guildpact / ten-token** quest framing. [[Liliana Vess|Liliana]] cited a vision of Ugin when delivering the marching orders in T06; in T07 Ugin appears directly to [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] and explains exactly how to revive [[Niv-Mizzet]] and remake the [[Guildpact]].

## Key Events

### [[PP_07_100525_The_Reylana_Infiltration|PP 7 — The Reylana Infiltration]] (10/05/25)
- Appeared to [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] in a concurrent dream in his **meditation realm**, back in **old-man form at full power** (the Tarkir timelines having shifted; safe now because [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] is physically occupied on [[Ravnica]]).
- Delivered the central lore-dump: the **[[Guildpact]]** is the agreement of the **10 guilds** to live in harmony; Ravnica's ley lines converge at a central **Nexus = the center of the [[implicit maze]]**; to revive **[[Niv-Mizzet]]** and re-establish the Guildpact, the party must bring **a representative or symbol/token of power from each of the 10 guilds** to that Nexus.
- Confirmed the party already carries Niv-Mizzet ("you do have Niv-Mizzet with you" — the Dragon Spirit Box).
- Parting charge: *"You must turn into dragon form during the final battle. After all, this is a brother's war of dragons in the end."*
- (Varis's Guildpact-History recall failed — nat 1, then 1 on advantage.)
