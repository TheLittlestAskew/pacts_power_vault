# Liliana Vess

A planeswalker necromancer and former [[Gatewatch]] member, operating in disguise as **"Professor Onyx."** **Revealed in [[PP_06_091725_Soul_Integrated_Soul_Storage_Get_It_Right|Transcript 06]]** as the black-robed betrayer aiding [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] — the "[[Onyx]]" who sold out [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]]'s [[Flameseekers of Truth]].

## The Chain Veil & Contract
She bears the **[[The Chain Veil|chain veil]]**, an artifact that **controls the dead** (it deflected [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]]'s Scorching Ray; commands [[Eternals]]). It came with a **demon contract**; her "loophole" was to kill the demons, after which she **contracted with [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] instead**. [[Rin]] (Arcana 21) confirms the contract is **real and far stronger/stricter** than the one that once bound [[Penny]] (which [[Rakdos]] transferred). The consequence of breaking it is **unknown** — she insists she must remain on Bolas's side.

## Role — the Campaign's Marching Orders (T06)
Claiming a "little birdie" (a vision of [[Ugin]]) told her a god's apotheosis would happen at the [[Bone Mine]], she delivers the party's central quest: **unite the guilds, find the center of the Leylines / the [[Implicit Maze]] (the Nexus), and revive [[Niv-Mizzet]]** with the box — recreating the [[Guildpact]]. She also tasks [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]] and [[Isiah Ja-Faank]] with taking [[Nissa]] to the [[Simic Combine]], where surviving Flameseekers are being experimented on.

## Key Events
### [[PP_06_091725_Soul_Integrated_Soul_Storage_Get_It_Right|Transcript 06]] (09/17/2025)
- Entered the [[Bone Mine]]; dropped the "Professor Onyx" disguise; revealed ~15 (illusioned to *hundreds* of) [[Eternals]] behind her.
- Cornered/grappled by [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]]; provoked into a 4th-level Fireball; **dimension-doored away** (scroll) with Chandra (KO'd), [[Isiah Ja-Faank]], and the Eternals.

- Overheard by the hidden party through a vent — and **winked at them** (she knows they're there; the double-agent ambiguity continues). Her exchange with Vraska confirmed the **Rakdos invasion was thwarted**, **[[Borborygmos]] roused**, the [[Simic Combine|Simic]] still loyal with the [[Immortal Sun]], **[[Tezzeret]] dead**, **[[Kaya Cassir|Kaya]] dealt with**, and the **Golgari flanking march through hollow [[Orzhov Syndicate|Orzhov]]**.
