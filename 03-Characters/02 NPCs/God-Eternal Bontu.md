# God-Eternal Bontu

A massive undead god of [[Amonkhet]], reanimated as a **God-Eternal** in service of [[Nicol Bolas]] and the [[The Second Sun|Second Sun]]. One of the metal-grafted Amonkhet zombie-gods Bolas unleashed on [[Ravnica]] — the sibling-counterpart to [[God-Eternal Oketra]] (killed earlier; her essence held by [[Ogre]]) and [[God-Eternal Kefnet]].

> **Spelling note:** the raw transcript renders her **"Bantu"**; canonical page form **God-Eternal Bontu** (matching [[God-Eternal Oketra]]).

## Campaign Role
A god-tier siege engine in Bolas's invasion. In [[PP_14_021326_Hold_the_Wall|PP 14]] she shells the [[Boros Legion|Boros]]/[[Izzet League|Izzet]] front line alongside [[God-Eternal Kefnet|Kefnet]] with enormous **god-metal spears** — forged of the same [[god-metal|Amonkhet god-metal]] as [[Tezzeret]]'s gear, which **teleport back to the wielder** after each throw (so they cannot easily be captured). [[Ogre]]'s standing plan is to grab a god-spear and ride it back to its god.

## Abilities & Combat Notes
- Hurls a **god-metal spear** from half a mile out — [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] (dragon form) burned a **Legendary Resistance** vs. the opening throw (would have dealt 34 necrotic and paralyzed him).
- Her spears are **forged of Amonkhet god-metal** and **teleport back** to her hand after striking.
- [[Ogre]] **Mage-Hand-deflected her returning spear** into ~25 Eternals (Eldritch Mage-Hand 19) — turning her own weapon on the horde.

## Key Events

### [[PP_14_021326_Hold_the_Wall|PP 14 — Hold the Wall]] (02/13/26)
- Deployed by [[Nicol Bolas]] (with [[God-Eternal Kefnet|Kefnet]] + the bulk of the Eternal horde) to the **[[Boros Legion|Boros]]/[[Izzet League|Izzet]] front line.**
- Opened the engagement at half a mile, hurling a **god-metal spear** at dragon-[[Varis Aestra|Varis]]; Varis burned a **Legendary Resistance** (34 necrotic, would have paralyzed).
- [[Ogre]] **deflected her returning spear into ~25 Eternals** with Eldritch Mage-Hand (19).
- Remained standing at session's end — she, [[God-Eternal Kefnet|Kefnet]], [[God-Eternal Rhonas|Rhonas]], and [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] are all still in play as the party breaks off the held wall.
