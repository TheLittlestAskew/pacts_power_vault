# God-Eternal Bontu

A massive undead god of [[Amonkhet]], reanimated as a **God-Eternal** in service of [[Nicol Bolas]] and the [[The Second Sun|Second Sun]]. One of the metal-grafted Amonkhet zombie-gods Bolas unleashed on [[Ravnica]] — the sibling-counterpart to [[God-Eternal Oketra]] (killed earlier; her essence held by [[Ogre]]) and [[God-Eternal Kefnet]].

> **Spelling note:** the raw transcript renders her **"Bantu"**; canonical page form **God-Eternal Bontu** (matching [[God-Eternal Oketra]]).

## Campaign Role
A god-tier siege engine in Bolas's invasion. In [[PP_13_021326_Hold_the_Wall|PP 13]] she shells the [[Boros Legion|Boros]]/[[Izzet League|Izzet]] front line alongside [[God-Eternal Kefnet|Kefnet]] with enormous **god-metal spears** — forged of the same [[god-metal|Amonkhet god-metal]] as [[Tezzeret]]'s gear, which **teleport back to the wielder** after each throw (so they cannot easily be captured). [[Ogre]]'s standing plan is to grab a god-spear and ride it back to its god.

## Abilities & Combat Notes
- Hurls a **god-metal spear** from half a mile out — [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] (dragon form) burned a **Legendary Resistance** vs. the opening throw (would have dealt 34 necrotic and paralyzed him).
- Her spears are **forged of Amonkhet god-metal** and **teleport back** to her hand after striking.
- [[Ogre]] **Mage-Hand-deflected her returning spear** into ~25 Eternals (Eldritch Mage-Hand 19) — turning her own weapon on the horde.

## Key Events

### [[PP_13_021326_Hold_the_Wall|PP 13 — Hold the Wall]] (02/13/26)
- Deployed by [[Nicol Bolas]] (with [[God-Eternal Kefnet|Kefnet]] + the bulk of the Eternal horde) to the **[[Boros Legion|Boros]]/[[Izzet League|Izzet]] front line.**
- Opened the engagement at half a mile, hurling a **god-metal spear** at dragon-[[Varis Aestra|Varis]]; Varis burned a **Legendary Resistance** (34 necrotic, would have paralyzed).
- [[Ogre]] **deflected her returning spear into ~25 Eternals** with Eldritch Mage-Hand (19).
- Remained standing at session's end — she, [[God-Eternal Kefnet|Kefnet]], [[God-Eternal Rhonas|Rhonas]], and [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] are all still in play as the party breaks off the held wall.

### [[PP_18_041926_Hi_Im_Ogre|PP 18 — Hi, I'm Ogre]] (04/19/26)
- **Pulled back to the Citadel summit, now a [[The Chain Veil|Chain Veil]] puppet.** Bolas has recalled the God-Eternals; atop the **400-ft pyramid** of [[Bolas's Citadel]], Bontu is one of the **3 remaining God-Eternals marionetted by [[Liliana Vess|Liliana]]** (with [[God-Eternal Rhonas|Rhonas]] + an unconfirmed third; [[God-Eternal Oketra|Oketra]] already downed).
- **[[Molly]] + Boros griffin-riders duel her overhead**, and Molly eventually **grapples Bontu down onto the pyramid** to fight; [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] support-fires with Lightning Arrow (Molly takes the splash). **Still up and mid-duel** as the session ends mid-assault (continues into the finale).

### [[PP_19_051426_I_Just_Need_Your_Soul|PP 19 — I Just Need Your Soul]] (05/14/26)
- **Duels [[Molly]] (and [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]], supporting)** on the platform — the near-unkillable god-tier brawl continues from S18 ("gods are sturdy — a couple of arrows won't bring this down"). Molly is **bloodied, then healed**; no PC goes down.
- **⭐ TURNED on Bolas.** When the party frees [[Liliana Vess|Liliana]] (Ogre siphons her soul to break the [[The Chain Veil|Chain Veil]]; [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] rewrites her contract to [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]]), Liliana **flips Bontu — with [[God-Eternal Rhonas|Rhonas]] and [[God-Eternal Kefnet|Kefnet]] — against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]**, the trio "cracking back to life" and stomping toward the spark-orb. **Now an asset re-aimed at Bolas going into the finale (05/31).** *(Session ends mid-encounter — do not import the finale.)*

### [[PP_20_053126_I_Miss_You_Buddy|PP 20 — I Miss You, Buddy]] (05/31/26) — ⭐ HARVESTS BOLAS'S SPARK
- **Drains the dragon through the final battle.** Under [[Liliana Vess|Liliana]]'s command, Bontu (with [[God-Eternal Rhonas|Rhonas]] and [[God-Eternal Kefnet|Kefnet]]) **steadily drains [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]'s power** during the summit war of attrition — Bontu continuing the duel against **[[Molly]]** (down, healed, back up) throughout.
- **⭐ Harvests his spark.** When Bolas falls (siphon broken by [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]), Liliana's **Lazotep-plated God-Eternals absorb his spark** — Bontu among them — finishing the job the party began. The flipped army wins the day.
