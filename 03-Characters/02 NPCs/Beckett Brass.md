# Beckett Brass

An **[[Ixalan]] admiral** who arrived on [[Ravnica]] with the off-plane reinforcements during the defense of the [[Boros Legion|Boros]]/[[Izzet League|Izzet]] front line, brought through [[Kwatli Pakal|Jareth]]'s portal alongside [[Lannery Storm]] and the Ixalan pirate-and-merfolk fleet.

> **Spelling note:** canonical page form **Beckett Brass**; raw transcript, spelling unconfirmed.

## Campaign Role
A military payoff of the [[Immortal Sun]] removal: with planeswalking restored, **off-plane allies can now reach Ravnica to fight.** Admiral Brass leads the Ixalan contingent ([[Kwatli Pakal]]'s reinforcements) that **plugged the wall breach** as the line was about to collapse, helping turn the battle.

## Key Events

### [[PP_14_021326_Hold_the_Wall|PP 14 — Hold the Wall]] (02/13/26)
- Arrived through **[[Kwatli Pakal|Jareth]]'s portal** with hundreds of **Ixalan pirates, merfolk, and priests** (and **Captain [[Lannery Storm]]**) to reinforce the [[Boros Legion|Boros]]/[[Izzet League|Izzet]] wall just as it tipped toward collapse.
- Helped **plug the breach** and stabilize the front line (alongside [[Narset]]'s dragons over the Azorius).

### [[PP_18_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 18 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26)
- **At the grand council.** Admiral Brass is among the allied leaders present at the **grand council of all ten guilds** (surfaced [[Maze's End]]) — the [[Ixalan]] contingent standing with Ravnica's guilds as they formally unite against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]].
