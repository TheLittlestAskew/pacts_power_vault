# Prime Speaker Megandar

The **Prime Speaker** (leader) of the [[Simic Combine]], encountered running the [[Second Sun]]'s underwater research facility ([[Second Sun Labs]]) in PP 8.

> ⚠ **Canon/spelling flag:** The canonical Simic Prime Speakers in published Ravnica lore are **Vannifar, Vorel, and Zegana** — "Megandar" appears to be campaign-specific or a mishearing. Recorded as heard in the transcript (no spell-check pass per Taylor); verify with the DM.

## Campaign Role

Megandar oversees the Simic facility manufacturing [[Eternals|Eternal]]-plated [[Zombie-Simic mutants]] for [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] / the [[Second Sun]]. He emerged from the inner office when the party's "auditor" bluff collapsed; his exact role in the imminent fight (and whether he is hostile) is unresolved at the cliffhanger.
