# Argo Lot

[[Ogre]]'s father-essence — a voiceless, battery-like soul the party nicknames **"Zaddy" / "Zaddy Daddy."** Distinct from **[[Ur-Ogre-lan]]** (Ogre's halfling death-god whole-self). Argo Lot's essence is now held inside **"Sis"** (formerly [[Pippi]]); a fragment of it was implanted into [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] and is the **source of his Dhampir powers.**

## Campaign Role
A power-source / lore linchpin connecting two PCs: he is Ogre's "daddy" essence and, via the [[Yarod Votsavo|Golgari Lich]], the origin of Sanis's vampiric/Dhampir abilities. After [[Ogre]] Mage-Handed [[Salena|Selena]] into the essence pool, the god-souls ([[God-Eternal Oketra|Oketra]] and the bat death-god [[Aclazotz]]) went *with* her; "Sis" now holds only **Argo Lot** plus the absorbed Eternal souls.

## Key Events

### [[PP_07_100525_The_Reylana_Infiltration|PP 7 — The Reylana Infiltration]] (10/05/25)
- Lore correction: after Selena entered the essence pool, "Sis" / [[Pippi]] retains only **Argo Lot** (Ogre's "daddy," a voiceless battery-like essence) plus the absorbed Eternal souls — the god-souls left with her.
- Revealed (Sanis's recovered memory) that the **[[Yarod Votsavo|Golgari Lich]]** implanted **Argo Lot's head/essence into [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]**, making it the **source of his Dhampir powers** and binding him by pact to **kill [[Vraska]]**.

### [[PP_10_113025_What_Are_You_Waiting_For_Sanis|PP 10 — What Are You Waiting For, Sanis?]] (11/30/25)
- During the drowning-lab fight against his parents, [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] **invoked Argo Lot twice** — begging *"Zaddy"/"Zadik"* (also *"Zaddy Daddy"*) for aid via what the table called **"Perun's Last Word"** (a reroll/help-on-a-plea effect; exact rule unconfirmed — flag).

### [[PP_13_012526_Fucking_Barbarians|PP 13 — Fucking Barbarians]] (01/25/26)
- **Merged fully into [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]].** Once the **[[Yarod Votsavo|Golgari Lich]] pact** was fulfilled ([[Vraska]] killed, head disintegrated as tribute), the **last independent shred of Argo Lot ("Zaddy Daddy")** merged fully into Sanis — no more separate voice. Sanis becomes "just himself," a **realized dhampir** (permanent +10 ft speed → 40, plus a new ability).
- The pact-voice that nudged Sanis mid-fight was **not** Argo Lot but the Lich (Sanis failed to ID it — Insight nat-1 ×2).
