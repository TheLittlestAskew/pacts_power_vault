# Ajani Goldmane

A **"beeg beefy Leonin"** planeswalker (per the DM's **#Lore Dump**) — one of the multiverse's heroes. Like the savior-figure **[[Gideon"Blackblade" Jura|Gideon "Blackblade" Jura]]**, he was **stabbed by [[Domri Rade]]** (the Gruul Guildleader).

## Campaign Role
A planeswalker ally on the broader board against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]. In the PP 17 grand council, **[[Teferi]]'s Gatewatch status report confirms "Ajani alive"** (alongside Gideon missing, [[Liliana Vess|Liliana]] with Bolas, [[Kaya Cassir|Kaya]] dead).
