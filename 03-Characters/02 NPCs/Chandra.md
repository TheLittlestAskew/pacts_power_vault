# Chandra Nalaar

Human planeswalker and pyromancer of the Gatewatch; long-time ally of [[The Breakfast Club]]. After roughly **seven years** missing — imprisoned and tortured at [[Strixhaven]] — she resurfaced on the [[Tenth District]] battlefield in [[PP_03_081025_Thats_Why_No_One_Will_Remember_Your_Name|Transcript 03]] and was rescued and brought to the [[Selesnya Conclave]] for care. (Played by guest player Bri.)

## Description / Appearance

Smaller and thinner than the party last remembered — haggard, with sunken eyes and a gaunt face, amber-red hair with flames licking at it. Her **left (non-dominant) arm** bears serious **black, spiral scarring** as though a talon had been raked across it — related to, but not identical to, the marks seen on [[Penny]] and [[Salena|Selena]]. The damage is permanent with current resources.

## Personality

Hardened by captivity but unbroken; deflects concern ("I'm fine. I was there for a reason. I knew the risks."). Fiercely protective of the innocent, quick to anger, and partial to solving problems with Fireball ("If it ain't broke, don't fix it").

## Backstory — Strixhaven (revealed Transcript 03)

Years ago, Chandra planeswalked to [[Strixhaven]] (a magic school) and infiltrated it after sensing something wrong. She discovered students were being abducted and **turned into undead** for their planeswalker sparks. She founded a resistance — **the [[Flameseekers of Truth]]** — and recruited students to fight back. She was **betrayed by someone named [[Onyx]]**, captured, and rotted in a cell, tortured, for years. The undead sorcerers now in [[Nicol Bolas]]'s [[Eternals]] army are drawn from Strixhaven's students.

## Knowledge — The Elder Spell

Chandra knows the enemy's spark-harvesting method: the **[[Elder Spell]]**, cast in tandem with a **[[Spark-Harvester Trident]]**. Once a victim dies, the spell + trident extract the planeswalker's spark; [[Nicol Bolas]] absorbs the harvested sparks atop [[Bolas's Citadel]]. She confirmed (Arcana, dirty 20) that the sparks rising over the district are harvested from planeswalkers.
