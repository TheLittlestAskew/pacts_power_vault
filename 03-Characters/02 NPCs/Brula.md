# Brula

An elf woman dressed in green with two daggers at her side, connected to [[Salena|Selena]]. Revealed (via [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]'s Insight + arcane vision) to be a **were-zombie, the same as Selena**.

## Description / Appearance
A green-clad elf with two daggers. Nervous and visibly anxious; shuffles when she moves and disappears the instant she steps outside the tree.

## Personality
Skittish and apologetic but sincere. [[Rin]] (Insight 26+) read her as meaning well and genuinely anxious; [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]] distrusted her on sight ("stranger danger"). No deception detected.

## Key Events

### [[PP_03_081025_Thats_Why_No_One_Will_Remember_Your_Name|Transcript 03 — That's Why No One Will Remember Your Name]] (08/10/2025)
- Found the party in the private care room inside [[Vitu-Ghazi]] and pressed a **bone sprouting purple mushrooms** into [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]'s hand
- Reported that [[Salena|Selena]] (Sanis's sister) went to the **[[Bone Mine]]**, got hurt, and is **recovering** there, and wanted the party to know
- Said she went to the apartment, found the party's note, and came to the Conclave to find them; offered to lead them to Selena ("I can only show you")
- Agreed to wait at "the fortress" while the party dropped off [[Scrappy]], then **vanished** as soon as she left the tree
- The mushroom-bone magic was deduced (Chandra, Arcana) to resemble the magic seen at [[Strixhaven]] — related to, but not, the [[Elder Spell]]
