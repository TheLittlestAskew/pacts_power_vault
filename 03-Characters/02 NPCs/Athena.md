# Athena

[[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]]'s ancestor and a prior wielder of **[[Blazebringer]]**. A "physical-violence type of glory" Levistus who walked strange skies, met (and slept with) demons, and carried the same indestructible greataxe Orphie now bears. First named in [[PP_05_090725_Do_You_Smell_That_Its_Breakfast_Time|Transcript 05]] when [[Tezzeret]] called Orphie "Athena's descendant"; she appears in person (in a dream) in [[PP_07_100525_The_Reylana_Infiltration|Transcript 07]].

## Campaign Role
The ancestral mentor figure of the Levistus line. In dreams she meets Orphie across a tavern counter — easy, hard-drinking, axe slung on her back — and delivers bloodline lore: Blazebringer's deep history, the danger of the oil that "destroyed worlds," and the hint that a demon walks somewhere in Orphie's ancestry. Establishes that Orphie's path "mirrors hers."

## Key Events

### [[PP_07_100525_The_Reylana_Infiltration|PP 7 — The Reylana Infiltration]] (10/05/25)
- Met [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] in a concurrent prophetic dream, drinking pints with [[Blazebringer]] on her back.
- Revealed that **Blazebringer is partly forged of Amonkhet / [[Hazoret]] metal** — why it never rusts or breaks ("it'll never rust the way I never will").
- Warned of an **oil out on another world that doesn't wash off, that has eaten whole planes** — and that it once **killed [[Ogre]] dead** on [[Dominaria]]. Catchphrase: *"Don't touch the oil. The oil is bad."* Orphie made her promise it twice.
- Told Orphie her path **mirrors Athena's** — she'll walk strange skies and meet demons she may be related to.
- Stated she "only sleeps with demons," implying Orphie's **demonic ancestor** is among her past lovers (named candidates — Malthagor, Jetmir, Belzenlok, Carter, "Face me" — were a **bit**; none confirmed canon).
- Left Orphie waking **tipsy**.
