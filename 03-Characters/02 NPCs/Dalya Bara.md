# Dalya Bara

An [[Izzet League]] researcher/artificer based at the [[Fortress of Nivix]]. Brilliant, eccentric, and good-natured; the next most senior Izzet member after [[Ral Zarek|Ral]]. A trusted friend of the party (especially [[Rin]]).

## Description / Appearance
An Izzet lab researcher in a guild-issued lab coat, perpetually tinkering. Builds gadgets, clones, and weaponry for the party and the guild.

## Personality
Cheerful, enthusiastic, and a chaotic experimenter (adds "two or three, sometimes even five" variables, sometimes even to her control group "to make it more fun"). Devoted to her work to the point of not realizing she isn't paid (the Izzet just provides housing, food, and clothing). Means well; the party worries she'd chase ethically dubious ideas (e.g., finding a soul for the soulless [[Pippi]] clone) if encouraged.

## Relationships
- [[Izzet League]] — Researcher; floated by the party as a better guildmaster than [[Ral Zarek|Ral]].
- [[Ral Zarek|Ral]] — Her boss; she begins to doubt his competence after Rin's "scientific method" argument.
- [[The Breakfast Club]] — Trusted friend; builds gear for them.
- [[Pippi]] — Cloned Pip; built [[EMLA]] to house a clone.
