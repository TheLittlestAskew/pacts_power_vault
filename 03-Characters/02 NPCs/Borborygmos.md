# Borborygmos

Cyclops leader of the [[Gruul Clans]].

## Campaign Role

The party previously helped [[Borborygmos]] and Sharp Dog consolidate control over the [[Gruul Clans]] by dealing with [[Domri Rade]]'s Bolas-aligned faction. [[Ogre]] notably punched the rival Gruul contender "in the crotch" to help Borborygmos retain leadership. After the [[Tenth District]] split, the Gruul aligned with the [[Boros Legion]] and [[Izzet League]] as "The Allies."

## Key Events

### [[PP_01_071325_Into_the_Ghost_Mind|Transcript 01 — Into the Ghost Mind]] (07/13/2025)
- [[Ral Zarek]] reported, during the [[Fortress of Nivix]] briefing, that **Borborygmos had been overthrown** as Gruul leader and that the Gruul have a new (unnamed) leader.

> ⚠ **Continuity flag:** Transcript 01 (07/13/25) reports Borborygmos overthrown, but later vault records list him as an active Gruul ally and a candidate Gruul representative for the [[Niv-Mizzet]] revival ritual (Transcript 07, 10/05/25). These conflict. Per archival rules the discrepancy is preserved, not resolved — confirm with the DM whether he was restored, the report was inaccurate, or the timeline differs.

### [[PP_07_100525_The_Reylana_Infiltration|PP 7 — The Reylana Infiltration]] (10/05/25)
- Appeared in [[Ogre]]'s concurrent prophetic dream of [[The Red Wastes]]: found **deposed, humiliated, and depressed** after the new leader [[Domri Rade]] smashed his "cup." [[Sharp Dog]] looked on.
- [[Ogre]] **restored his confidence** (gave him his leathers as a cover), then sprinted down to **punch [[Domri Rade]] in the crotch** to rally support; a nat-20 told Ogre the dream "was connected with more."
- The eavesdropped [[Liliana Vess|Liliana]]/[[Vraska]] conversation **confirmed the dream was real**: Borborygmos has **roused** after "being punched in the gruul jewel with a makeshift cup," the Gruul front is losing momentum for Bolas, and [[Domri Rade]] is opposed by the old guard. (This partly substantiates his "active Gruul ally" status — he is rallying support again.)
