# Onyx

The person who **betrayed [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]]** at [[Strixhaven]], getting her captured and imprisoned. Named by Chandra in [[PP_03_081025_Thats_Why_No_One_Will_Remember_Your_Name|Transcript 03]] as the one she thought she could trust.

## In-Fiction Status (Transcript 03)
The party does **not** recognize the name "Onyx." The DM gated a recall check behind a **nat-20** (tied to an alternate "Strixane" timeline); the party rolled and missed it, so the identity remains hidden to them in-fiction.

> ⚠ **Archivist cross-reference (campaign-level, NOT party knowledge in T03):** Per the [[Campaign Dashboard]], **[[Liliana Vess]]** is later revealed to have operated under the alias **Professor Onyx**. Do not treat this connection as known to the party during Transcript 03.
