# Benji

A [[House Dimir]] agent and an old school friend of [[Varis Aestra|Varis]]. A recurring party ally and diplomatic contact.

## Campaign Role

Benji is the party's primary link to the unaligned [[House Dimir]]. In early sessions a Dimir insurgent (Benji) beckoned Varis after the party's arrest and provided him with items. Later he served as the party's messenger and diplomat, examined the [[Sole Integrated Storage]] (SIS) artifact (the DM noted part of it "belongs to the Dimir Guild"), and was sent — carrying Varis's amulet as proof of connection — to recruit guild support for the auction/fundraising effort (Transcript 10).
