# Benji

A [[House Dimir]] agent and an old school friend of [[Varis Aestra|Varis]]. A recurring party ally and diplomatic contact.

## Campaign Role

Benji is the party's primary link to the unaligned [[House Dimir]]. In early sessions a Dimir insurgent (Benji) beckoned Varis after the party's arrest and provided him with items. Later he served as the party's messenger and diplomat, examined the [[Sole Integrated Storage]] (SIS) artifact (the DM noted part of it "belongs to the Dimir Guild"), and was sent — carrying Varis's amulet as proof of connection — to recruit guild support for the auction/fundraising effort (Transcript 09).

## Key Events

### [[PP_01_071325_Into_the_Ghost_Mind|Transcript 01 — Into the Ghost Mind]] (07/13/2025)
- Present at the [[Ghost Mind]] exterior with [[Hikara]] while the party descended.

> ⚠ **Flag:** Benji's appearance in Transcript 01 is brief and given without further context. His identity here is consistent with the established Dimir-agent Benji, but the session notes flag it for confirmation.

### [[PP_10_121425_You_Cant_Just_Buy_the_Sun|PP 10 — You Can't Just Buy the Sun]] (12/14/25)
- Now in the **Dimir "Inner Circle"** (the guild is leaderless/chaotic since the party crashed its information network). Dropped the intel that **[[Krenko]]'s** crew (the goblin-faced blimp) blew up the [[Second Sun Labs|Simic lab]] and **took the [[Immortal Sun]].**
- **Gifted the party 5 basic Healing Potions** (2d4+2) and ferried **[[Vuthala]]** to the [[Selesnyan Conclave|Selesnya Conclave]] (completing the Vuthala handoff).
- Clocked [[Ogre]]'s **"Sis"/[[Leather Daddy Game Boy]]** (LDGB) as partly **Dimir property** (*"I'll need that at some point"*) — seeding future Dimir tension.
- Re-summoned (by saying his name thrice), he **agreed to canvass allies for auction funds (Plan A)** — but flagged that the [[Orzhov Syndicate|Orzhov]] banking system is dead, forcing the **[[First Church of Orzhov]]** bank-reopening workaround. Carries [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]'s Orzhov amulet as a token of trust.

### [[PP_14_022226_Reforging_the_Guildpact|PP 14 — Reforging the Guildpact]] (02/22/26)
- **The Dimir token, taken by force.** Examining the [[Guildpact]] shreds, [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]] found 9 of 10 guild insignias — the **[[House Dimir|Dimir]] missing.** The party summoned Benji (name said 3×); he came as the now-bureaucratic, council-run Dimir's representative but **refused to give a "legally binding" signature.** [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]] **yanked his Dimir lapel pin** (nat-20 Dex, 22) and shoved him off dragon-[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]; [[Ogre]] Feather-Fell him, and Benji **shadow-exited, resigned.** The pin became the **10th guild token** for the reforge.
- **Got his Orzhov amulet back** from [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]] (returned the [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] family amulet he'd been carrying as a trust token).

> ⚠ **Note:** the pin Orphie took is the in-fiction "Dimir representation"; it was later pinned through the [[Guildpact]] shreds on the [[Maze's End]] pedestal as the ritual's 10th token.

### [[PP_16_040226_The_Guildpact_Reborn|PP 16 — The Guildpact Reborn]] (04/02/26)
- **Present but pointedly not helping ("so Dimir").** As reborn [[Niv-Mizzet]] (the [[Living Guildpact]]) addressed the party at [[Maze's End]], Benji was **felt/sensed to be present** in the chamber — but **deliberately did not lend a hand** during the [[Augur of Bolas|Augur]] fight. Niv (and the party) noted the no-show as quintessentially Dimir. Off-screen, no dialogue.

### [[PP_17_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 17 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26)
- **At the grand council — and gestures Sanis to the Dimir die.** Benji attends the council at the surfaced [[Maze's End]] among the **[[House Dimir|Dimir]]** contingent ([[Hikara]], [[A Shady Individual|Penny]], Benji). When the guilds put their energies on the ritual die, **Benji gestures [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]** to the **Dimir die** — since Sanis carries **Lazav** (the original Dimir leader) inside him — and Sanis **does the honors** for the guild. (Sanis is now formally tied to the Dimir seat / Lazav.)
