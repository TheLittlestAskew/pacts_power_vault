# Rakdos

The **demon-god and parun of the [[Cult of Rakdos]]** — the guild of chaos, entertainment, and hedonistic destruction. A vast sleeping demon whose **caged, beating heart** the blood-witches of the Carnarium ritually woke during the war. He cannot resist a show.

## Campaign Role
The party's chaotic-but-friendly demon ally. His Cult's blood-pact contractor **[[A Shady Individual|Penny]]** (whose old Rakdos contract he once transferred) ties him to the party. After being roused in T07 ("The show shall begin"), Rakdos and his forces become an asset in the war against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] — and in the finale he can't resist joining the biggest show of all.

## Key Events

### [[PP_07_100525_The_Reylana_Infiltration|PP 7 — The Reylana Infiltration]] (10/05/25)
- **Roused.** In [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]]'s concurrent prophetic dream of the **Rakdos Carnarium under siege** by [[Eternals]], a coven of blood witches (defended by [[A Shady Individual|Penny]], with Rin's Wall of Fire) ritually woke his caged heart — *"The show shall begin."* The [[Liliana Vess|Liliana]]/[[Vraska]] intel later confirmed the **Rakdos invasion was thwarted** (the dream was real).

### [[PP_21_053126_I_Miss_You_Buddy|PP 21 — I Miss You, Buddy]] (05/31/26)
- ⭐ **Joins the final battle — for the show.** At the summit of [[Bolas's Citadel|Bolas's Citadel]], **Rakdos appears**, drops **[[A Shady Individual|Penny]] onto [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]'s shoulder** to hack at his scales, **leaves "his mark"** on the dragon, and departs — the partying god who couldn't miss the multiverse's biggest spectacle. His chip-damage is part of the collective effort that brings Bolas down.
- **Victory & peace:** with Bolas sealed, Rakdos and his Cult join the celebration across Ravnica as [[Niv-Mizzet]] declares a coming time of peace.

## Relationships
- [[A Shady Individual|Penny]] — His Cult's blood-pact contractor; he transferred her old contract and drops her onto Bolas in the finale.
- [[Cult of Rakdos]] — His guild; he is its parun/demon-god.
- [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]] — Helped wake him (T07 dream).
- [[Nicol Bolas]] — The enemy he helps bring down in the finale.
