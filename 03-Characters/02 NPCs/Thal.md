# Thal

The **leader of the Myconid colony** at the [[Bone Mine]] — the large Myconid the party recognized "from last time." **Named this session** ([[PP_05_090725_Do_You_Smell_That_Its_Breakfast_Time|Transcript 05]]). A calm, gnomic figure who communicates in spore-telepathy ("yes, that is one way to say hello").

## Description / Appearance
A large Myconid distinct from his five lesser colony-mates; the [[Bone Mine]]'s "mushroom king." (The Myconids arose when a chemical from the [[Bone Mine]]'s plant life mixed with the mushrooms in the soil — the same were-zombie "essence" that birthed [[Salena|Selena]].)

## Key Events

### [[PP_05_090725_Do_You_Smell_That_Its_Breakfast_Time|Transcript 05 — Do You Smell That? It's Breakfast Time]] (09/07/2025)
- His colony was attacked by [[Eternals]]; **[[Salena|Selena]] defended them**, slaying hundreds before she fell. ~20–30 Myconids died.
- To save the gravely injured Selena, Thal **used "the essence"** (the were-zombie pit liquid) — which inadvertently spread [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]'s corruption into her, as the essence pits themselves were being corrupted.
- Warned the party that **[[Tezzeret]]** (a "human machine, arms of metal") was at the marrow/essence pits **corrupting the colony's essence**, and pointed them down the tunnels to confront him.
- After the party purged the corruption and the pits collapsed, the party returned to find **all Myconids unconscious** — and an **ethereal white-eyed figure standing over Thal's body** (implied [[Salena|Selena]], ascended). Session-ending stinger.

## Relationships
- [[Salena|Selena]] — Saved her life with the essence; she submerged in his colony's pit and ascended.
- [[The Breakfast Club]] — Allied; guided them to Tezzeret.
- [[Tezzeret]] — Corrupted his colony's essence pits for [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]].
