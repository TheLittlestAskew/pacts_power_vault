# Aurelia

The angelic guildmaster of the **[[Boros Legion]]**, who attended the **grand council of all ten guilds** at the surfaced [[Maze's End]] with her commander [[Molly]] — putting the Boros's energies on the ritual die to formally unite the guilds against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]].

> **Spelling note (raw transcript):** rendered here as canonical **Aurelia** (the standard MTG Boros guildmaster); no spell-check pass per Taylor.

## Campaign Role
The Boros representative at the campaign's "everyone shows up" council. Stands alongside [[Molly]] (the party's familiar Boros commander) as the guild commits its forces to the coalition. A late-arc payoff: the militant Boros Legion, who have **HELD the Bulwark front line** for the party (see [[Molly]]/[[Eternals]]), now joins the formal alliance.

## Key Events

### [[PP_17_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 17 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26)
- Attended the **grand council** at the surfaced [[Maze's End]] convened by **[[Niv-Mizzet]]** (the [[Living Guildpact]]) — representing the **[[Boros Legion]]** alongside [[Molly]].
- Joined all ten guilds in **channeling their forces onto the ritual die** to formally unite against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]].
