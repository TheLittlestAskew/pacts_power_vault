# God-Eternal Oketra

A massive (≈60-ft) undead god-archer of [[Amonkhet]], reanimated as a God-Eternal in service of [[Nicol Bolas]] and the [[The Second Sun|Second Sun]]. Identified by [[Samut]], who warned that she "never misses a shot."

> ⚠ **Cross-session note:** Oketra first appears in [[PP_02_072725_I_Cant_Rebuild_You|Transcript 02]] (alive, attacking) and is killed by the party in [[PP_03_081025_Thats_Why_No_One_Will_Remember_Your_Name|Transcript 03]]. This page reflects both, dated per session.

## Description / Appearance
A 60-ft hulking figure wielding a longbow with white-fletched arrows. Wears heavy armor (which the party later exploits). Her arrows are devastatingly accurate and powerful.

## Abilities & Combat Notes
- **Never misses** (per [[Samut]]); unerring ranged attacks at extreme range (500–700+ ft, hitting the front lines)
- Arrow strike dealt **78 piercing** (Dex save for half) to [[Ogre's Traveling Tavern]] and its occupants
- Shifts aim to whoever draws her attention (e.g., [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]'s flame arrow → the Tavern)
- The Eternals under her wield spark-extracting **bidents** (transcribed "Biden") that pull planeswalker sparks toward the harvesting pyramid

## Relationships
- [[Nicol Bolas]] / [[The Second Sun]] — Serves Bolas's invasion.
- [[Samut]] — Knows her by name and reputation.
- [[The Breakfast Club]] — Destroyed the party's Tavern in T02; killed by the party in T03.
