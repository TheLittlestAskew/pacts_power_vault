# Narset

A Jeskai planeswalker ally — known for restoring Tarkir's dragons — spotted moving covertly through the [[Krenko Incorporated]] underground city during the lead-up to the [[Immortal Sun]] auction. Her purpose there is unstated.

> Spelling unconfirmed; canonical page form **Narset**.

## Campaign Role

A planeswalker ally converging (with others) on the [[Immortal Sun]] auction. [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]] glimpses her shimmering through the underground city beneath [[Krenko Incorporated]] — one of several mystery figures gathering as the auction nears. Her motive is unknown (a rival bidder? an ally angling for the same heist?).

## Key Events

### [[PP_11_121425_You_Cant_Just_Buy_the_Sun|PP 11 — You Can't Just Buy the Sun]] (12/14/25)
- **Spotted sneaking through the [[Krenko Incorporated]] underground city** by [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]] — shimmering into view. Present, motive unknown; logged among the mystery figures converging on the auction (alongside an unidentified shadowy infiltrator).

### [[PP_12_011126_The_Masquerade_Auction|PP 12 — The Masquerade Auction]] (01/11/26)
- **Motive revealed:** Narset is running her **own heist to steal the [[Immortal Sun]] and destroy it** — the same goal as the party. Her inside man is the Goliath guard **[[Kalmar]]** (a doppelganger), planted to **let her in after the auction.**
- **Cliffhanger:** as auction chaos broke, **[[Ogre]] (nat-20 Perception) spotted Narset creeping Spidey-style toward the covered, chain-hung cage** holding the [[Immortal Sun]] — the heist beginning as the session ends "to be continued."

### [[PP_13_012526_Fucking_Barbarians|PP 13 — Fucking Barbarians]] (01/25/26)
- **Succeeded — the campaign hinge.** During the [[Vraska]] fight she dropped to the caged **[[Immortal Sun]]**, planted an **Izzet apparatus bomb** ("hot potato"), cast Jump on [[Ogre]], and when it detonated **bagged the Sun and escaped backstage** with her crew.
- **Took the Sun off-plane**, which **restored planeswalking across Ravnica** — hundreds of trapped planeswalkers' souls/light-beams streaking the sky as they finally leave. (This also uncaged [[Nicol Bolas\|Bolas]]'s open destruction of the city.)
- Revealed to lead/run with **[[Ravnica Trading Incorporated]]** — **[[Shaylin]]'s** Tarkir company (crew: the doppelganger [[Kalmar]], and likely [[Rekka]] as the red-haired carriage driver glimpsed leaving). All confirmed **allies.**

### [[PP_14_021326_Hold_the_Wall|PP 14 — Hold the Wall]] (02/13/26)
- **Brought the dragon cavalry.** As the [[Boros Legion|Boros]]/[[Izzet League|Izzet]] wall and the [[Azorius Senate|Azorius]] front teetered, Narset **opened portals over the Azorius spilling dragons** that firebombed the Eternal army (contesting [[God-Eternal Rhonas|Rhonas]]'s assault) — the off-plane reinforcement counterpart to [[Kwatli Pakal|Jareth]]'s Ixalan armies. Both fronts **stabilized.**
- The fruit of her own heist: taking the [[Immortal Sun]] off-plane restored planeswalking, so **off-plane allies (dragons, Ixalan armies, planeswalkers) can now reach Ravnica to fight** — the war is winnable.

### [[PP_21_053126_I_Miss_You_Buddy|PP 21 — I Miss You, Buddy]] (05/31/26)
- **Appears for the victory.** As [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] falls, **Narset and [[Vraska]] appear** at the battlefield with **the [[Immortal Sun]] gone, back on [[Ixalan]]** — her steal-and-destroy arc fully resolved. She is among the allies (Tarkir dragons, the Jeskai, Ixalan's forces, all ten guilds) celebrating Ravnica's salvation as [[Niv-Mizzet]] declares peace. *(Note: [[Vraska]] died PP 13 — the note lists her appearing here; logged as written, the reference is to the gathered allies/figures at the close.)*
