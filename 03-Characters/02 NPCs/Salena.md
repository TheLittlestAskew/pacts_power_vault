# Salena

Sister to [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] of the [[Reylana Family]].

## Status

- **Early Session 4:** Killed during the aftermath of the [[Guildpact]] shattering. [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] and [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] had led her to safety (Varis gave her invisibility powder), but she died in the chaos. Her death hardened Sanis.
- **Transcript 01 (07/13/25):** **Present and apparently alive** during the [[Ghost Mind]] descent. Listed among the party group navigating the interior. In the entrance-ritual visions she was shown a coffin holding a younger version of herself and asked whether she would trade the strength she gained for the softness she lost, and whether she would protect the Ghost Mind — she answered that she would not go back, that she would endure it all again "to see her brother again."

> ⚠ **Continuity flag:** Salena died in Early Session 4 but is present in Transcript 01 (07/13/25). Confirmed (per Taylor) to be the same character (the Transcript 01 notes render the name "Selena"). The mechanism of her return is not yet established in-source and should be confirmed with the DM.

## Relationships
- [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] — Brother. Her death was a defining wound for him; her presence in Transcript 01 is a major personal development.
- [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]] — Grieved Salena's death; deep empathy.
- [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] — Helped lead her to safety during the Spring Festival attack.

- Underwent an entrance-ritual vision (coffin / younger self) and chose not to return to who she was, enduring everything again to see her brother
