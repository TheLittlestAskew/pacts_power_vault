# Eddie

The **head priest** of the [[Myconids|Myconid]] colony that worships **[[Aphonexa]]** — the god the party themselves created. When [[Dalya Bara|Dahlia]]'s Drillinator bored straight through his colony, killing one myconid and injuring another, Eddie demanded restitution — *"a like for like"* — then, once [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]] revived the dead, knelt and hailed the party as Aphonexa's **"chosen."**

> **Naming note (raw transcript):** the colony's lesser myconids are nicknamed **"Mike / Mike-and-Ikes / clickers"** by the party; the head priest is distinguished as **"Eddie."** Spelling unconfirmed (no spell-check pass, per Taylor).

## Campaign Role
The voice of the wronged Myconid colony — and, after the restitution, a new guide and ally for the finale. His demand for "a like for like" is the moral hinge of the session: it forces the [[Orzhov Syndicate|Orzhov]] spell-loan ("Pacts and Power") that lets Rin cast Raise Dead. Once the wrong is righted, the colony recognizes the party as **the chosen/creators of [[Aphonexa]]** (their god), and Eddie volunteers to **guide them to the Rot Warren.**

## Key Events

### [[PP_18_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 18 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26)
- **The Myconid misunderstanding.** [[Dalya Bara|Dahlia]]'s Drillinator tunneled **straight through the Myconid colony** — the purple myconids who worship **[[Aphonexa]]** — **killing one and badly injuring another.** The colony attacked; Eddie, the head priest, demanded **"a like for like."**
- **The restitution.** [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]] stabilized the injured myconid, then revived the dead one with **Raise Dead** cast through [[Varis Aestra|Varis]]'s **Mizzium Gauntlet** (after a Cloudkill misfire — see [[The Rot Warren]]/session note). Eddie **knelt**; the colony **hailed the party as Aphonexa's "chosen"** (its creators).
- **Volunteered to guide** the party onward to the **[[The Rot Warren|Rot Warren]]** (the Golgari spawning grounds the Drillinator was tunneling toward). Quote: *"One of our kind has been killed. A like for like is required."*
