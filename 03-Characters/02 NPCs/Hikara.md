> ✅ **Name & affiliation resolved (DM #Lore Dump, full archive 06/02/26):** the DM's canonical NPC is **"Hekara, the Rakdos Harlequin"** (posted 08/20/23, alongside the Rakdos Bloodwitch **[[Exava]]**). "Hikara," "Hikaru," and "Hekara" are transcription variants of the same individual — **Hekara** is canonical, and she is **[[Cult of Rakdos|Rakdos]]**, not Dimir. (Her PP 18 grouping with the Dimir contingent is a raw-transcript attribution quirk, not a guild change.) File name kept as `Hikara` to preserve existing backlinks; alias **Hekara** added.
