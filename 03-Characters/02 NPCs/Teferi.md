# Teferi

A time-bending planeswalker — "the time guy" — long referenced in the campaign as one of the powers concerned with the multiverse's repair. He is **glimpsed**, not met, in PP 17: a shadow resembling Teferi appears through a **self-righting crack in the Blind Eternities** as [[Niv-Mizzet]] reforges the [[Guildpact]].

## Campaign Role
A distant, off-screen power. His cameo signals that the **planar damage caused by the war** (the unraveling [[Leylines]], the rents in the Blind Eternities) is being **addressed by forces beyond the party** — the "self-righting" crack reads as Teferi's chronomancy at work. Flagged as a possible future ally or plot beat. ([[Nissa]] noted in T03 that there was no sign of Teferi at the [[Selesnya Conclave]]; this is his first on-page appearance.)

**Guest-player note:** Teferi is **Dan's** PC at the table (this is his **2nd table appearance** — first as a guest, after the S17 cameo). Time wizard / chronomancer.

## Key Events

### [[PP_17_040226_The_Guildpact_Reborn|PP 17 — The Guildpact Reborn]] (04/02/26)
- **Cameo through the Blind Eternities.** As reborn [[Niv-Mizzet]] (the [[Living Guildpact]]) stabilized the rogue red [[Leylines|Leyline]] and addressed the planar damage, a **shadow resembling Teferi** — "the time guy" — was glimpsed through a **self-righting crack in the Blind Eternities.** The crack mending itself implied an outside power (likely Teferi's time magic) is repairing the planar wounds.
- Raw-transcript spellings in play: "the time guy" / "Tafari" / "the fairy." Left as-is (no spell-check, per Taylor).

### [[PP_18_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 18 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26)
- **Arrives in force.** At the **grand council** at the surfaced [[Maze's End]], Teferi **tears open a crack in reality** and steps through with a **[[Zhalfir army]]** — the forces of his recovered home plane, **[[Zhalfir]]** — volunteered to aid Ravnica's war against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]. *"It's very odd as a time wizard to be late to the party."*
- **The lore-keeper.** Acts as the party's knowledgeable guide, narrating MTG/multiverse lore. Delivers the **Gatewatch status update**: **Ajani** alive; **Gideon** missing (went after [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]] to [[Strixhaven]], unseen since the Beacon); **[[Liliana Vess|Liliana]]** working for [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] (necromancy + the **[[The Chain Veil|Chain Veil]]**, possibly animating the Eternals) "but also not"; **[[Nissa]]** present; **[[Kaya Cassir|Kaya]]** and the "ghost assassin" dead. Notes **[[God-Eternal Kefnet|Kefnet]]** battling Tarkir dragons overhead and **3 Eternal Gods** loose in Ravnica.
- **The build & the T-Rex.** In Dahlia's workshop he cast a **minor illusion of "Isaiah's face"** on the [[The Drillinator|Drillinator]]'s bit to motivate [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]]'s hammering. At the [[The Rot Warren|Rot Warren]], he **polymorphed Orphie into a T-Rex** (her second time — *"I am a T-Rex forever"*; the warm reveal that **he's the one who first made her a T-Rex** earns her forgiveness). His **Fire Bolt (22)** shattered the final pylon.
- **Departs.** Called to the **[[Zhalfir]] front lines**, Teferi leaves at session's end — reachable via *Sending*. (Table note: he may not return before the campaign ends — getting married ~6 weeks out.)
- Raw-transcript names: "the time fairy / the fairy / Tafari" = Teferi.

### [[PP_21_053126_I_Miss_You_Buddy|PP 21 — I Miss You, Buddy]] (05/31/26)
- **Returns for the final battle.** Teferi (Dan, guest) **steps back through a portal** to join the Citadel-summit fight against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] — answering [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]]'s running "rewind time so we can't lose" bit one last time.
- **Epilogue — back to the Gatewatch.** With Bolas sealed, Teferi **returns to the Gatewatch**, off to **[[Dominaria]] to help Ajani.** As a parting gift he leaves [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] **two Polymorph scrolls** (for her beloved T-Rex form).

## Relationships

- [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] — Polymorphed her into a T-Rex (twice); revealed he's the one who first did it — warm reunion/forgiveness (PP 18).
- [[Zhalfir]] / [[Zhalfir army]] — His recovered home plane and the army he brings to Ravnica.
