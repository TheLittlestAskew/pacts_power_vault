# Lord Aestra

**[[Varis Aestra|Varis]]'s** father — a displaced **[[Orzhov Syndicate|Orzhov]]** noble now sheltering, with **[[Lady Aestra]]**, in the Orzhov quarter of the [[Selesnyan Conclave|Selesnya Conclave]] refugee camp. A key pillar of the plan to reopen the First Church of Orzhov bank.

> Spelling unconfirmed (Aestra/Aesthra); canonical page form **Lord Aestra**.

## Campaign Role

Part of the Aestra parents who anchor the party's **Plan A** (buy the [[Immortal Sun]]). As Orzhov refugees with standing, they agree to **rally surviving tellers and liquidity** to reopen the **[[First Church of Orzhov]]** bank once the party scouts it — the workaround for the collapsed Orzhov banking system.

## Key Events

### [[PP_10_121425_You_Cant_Just_Buy_the_Sun|PP 10 — You Can't Just Buy the Sun]] (12/14/25)
- Reunited warmly with his son [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] at the [[Selesnyan Conclave|Conclave]] refugee camp (Orzhov quarter).
- With [[Lady Aestra]], agreed to **rally Orzhov tellers and liquidity to reopen the [[First Church of Orzhov]] bank** once the party scouts the deserted church — enabling the guild fundraising for the auction.
- Gave the party an Aestra **sending stone** to coordinate the bank reopening.

### [[PP_12_012526_Fucking_Barbarians|PP 12 — Fucking Barbarians]] (01/25/26)
- **Installed as the Orzhov bank manager.** At the [[Selesnyan Conclave]], [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] (now holding [[Orzhov Syndicate\|Orzhov]] guild leadership via the reopened [[First Church of Orzhov]] bank) "blesses" his father as **bank manager** — a clumsy ritual (rolled a 3) that produces a manager's sash anyway.
- With [[Lady Aestra]], will **lead the refugees back to reopen the [[Orzhov Syndicate\|Orzhov]]** using the ~100k gp [[Pecunia]] reserve. (Varis intends to appoint a permanent guild leader later; keeps the diamond.)

### [[PP_14_022226_Reforging_the_Guildpact|PP 14 — Reforging the Guildpact]] (02/22/26)
- **Orzhov front-line commander.** Holding the [[Orzhov Syndicate\|Orzhov]]/[[First Church of Orzhov|First Church]] front with a **ragtag defense** against the [[Eternals]]. The party's **dragon-back drive-by** (killing ~100 Eternals) **bought him time** before they used the church's basement portal to descend into the [[Implicit Maze]].
