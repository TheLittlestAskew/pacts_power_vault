# Krenko

A goblin mob boss from the party's old [[Tin Street]] neighborhood who, in the years since they ran errands for him, has built himself into the king of a hidden underground metropolis and the CEO of **[[Krenko Incorporated]]**. As of PP 10 he holds the **[[Immortal Sun]]** and intends to auction it to the highest bidder.

> Spelling unconfirmed — the DM's renders drift across "Cranko," "Krinko," and "Crinkle." Canonical page form **Krenko**.

## Campaign Role

The party's new obstacle to the [[Immortal Sun]]. Five years ago Krenko was a [[Tin Street]] mob boss who sent the party after the **[[Sword of Feast and Famine]]**; now risen to a corporate crime-king, he runs Krenko Incorporated — a 20–25-story tower over a sprawling underground city. His crew's **goblin-faced blimp** stole the Immortal Sun from the drowned Simic lab. He refuses to parlay or meet, routing the party to his assistant [[Kiku]], and plans to sell the Sun (alongside a Temporal Spear, a Hell Spear, an Izzet signet, and more) at a **single auction tonight to the highest bidder.** He still wants the [[Sword of Feast and Famine]] hilt back.

## Key Events

### [[PP_10_121425_You_Cant_Just_Buy_the_Sun|PP 10 — You Can't Just Buy the Sun]] (12/14/25)
- His crew's **goblin-faced, cannon-armed blimp** is seen flying off from the wreck of the drowned [[Second Sun Labs|Simic lab]] with the **[[Immortal Sun]]**; [[Benji]] IDs the blimp as Krenko's, and the party deduces **Krenko's crew blew up the lab and took the Sun.**
- Traced to **[[Krenko Incorporated]]** on [[Tin Street]] — the party's old neighborhood, where they attended Tin Street / 10th Street High and once ran the [[Sword of Feast and Famine]] job for him.
- On [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]]'s night watch, a goblin messenger delivers a **letter from Krenko**: *"Come talk before I sell the sun to a dragoner…"*
- Refuses to meet in person ("a very, very busy man"); routes the party to his assistant **[[Kiku]]**, who reveals the **Immortal Sun will be auctioned tonight to the highest bidder.** The letter is ruled a **parlay request, not a binding contract** ([[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]]'s persuasion to argue otherwise fails — nat 1).
- Still wants the **[[Sword of Feast and Famine]] hilt** back; the party declines to surrender it without the Sun. Sets up the buy-vs-steal auction clock.

### [[PP_11_011126_The_Masquerade_Auction|PP 11 — The Masquerade Auction]] (01/11/26)
- Hosted the masked auction as **emcee "Franco"** — a masquerade ballroom into an amphitheater auction (3 items, 1 minute each, highest bidder). *"Don't blow your load on the first item. Highest bidder wins."*
- **Anti-tamper barrier:** called out [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]]'s stealth Thaumaturgy to dim the display (it fizzled on his anti-tamper ward): *"You think that you can try and kill me or manipulate the stage? No, it will not work."*
- Took the party's **41,000 gp** winning bid for **Item 1, "Ogre's Tavern 2."** The covered, chain-hung cage holding the **[[Immortal Sun]]** swayed above as the final lot — the heist breaking open at the cliffhanger.

### [[PP_12_012526_Fucking_Barbarians|PP 12 — Fucking Barbarians]] (01/25/26)
- **Humiliated and stripped of everything.** As the auction erupted into the [[Vraska]] fight (and [[Ava]]'s diversion fire), Krenko **panicked and called the guards.**
- **Bonked prone** when [[Ogre]] dropped the **[[Sword of Feast and Famine]] hilt** on him (10 dmg), then **Hold-Personed by [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]]**, who **stole the entire display case of [[Guildpact]] shreds** off him (+ psychic damage + a fire-safety rant: *"You really need to get some fire safety put in place…"*).
- **Lost the [[Immortal Sun]]** ([[Narset]] bagged it), the **Guildpact shreds** (Rin), and **"Ogre's Tavern 2"** (recovered by [[Ava]]). On the way out, dragon-[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] **Force-Breathed Krenko's goblin-blimp to shreds.** Survives, but utterly emptied out.
