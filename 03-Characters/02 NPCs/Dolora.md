# Dolora

The **patron deity of [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]]** — an [[Aasimar]] Cleric of the **Light Domain**. The source of Rin's celestial/radiant power. Rin invokes her constantly ("praying to Dolora") and channeled her blessing into key moments of [[PP_05_090725_Do_You_Smell_That_Its_Breakfast_Time|Transcript 05]].

> **Spelling: Dolora (confirmed per Taylor).** The transcript renders the name variously as "Dol Ara," "Dora," and "Dolores"; **Dolora** is canonical and is recorded in the campaign spelling dictionary (§7 Campaign NPCs).

## What's Known
- Rin's divine source as a **Light Domain Cleric**; her radiant spells and **Channel Divinity** draw on Dolora.
- In [[PP_05_090725_Do_You_Smell_That_Its_Breakfast_Time|Transcript 05]], the **celestial radiant power** that had infused Rin's old syringe (a swirl of black [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] ichor and white radiance) came from a past Channel Divinity — i.e., **Dolora's blessing** — and became one of the three energies (with [[Ugin]]'s essence and Bolas's ichor) used to **cure [[Salena|Selena]]**.
- Rin also called on Dolora for **Divine Intervention** during the marrow-pits collapse (it failed; d100 = 65).

## Relationships
- [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]] — Devout cleric; channels Dolora's light.
