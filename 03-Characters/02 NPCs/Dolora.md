# Dol Arrah

The **patron deity of [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]]** — an [[Aasimar]] Cleric of the **Light Domain**. A goddess of light, honor, and the sun; the source of Rin's celestial/radiant power. Rin invokes her constantly ("praying to Dol Arrah") and channels her blessing into key moments.

> **Naming:** the canonical name is **Dol Arrah** (confirmed in play by S15 — *"I'm the freaking champion of Dol Arrah"*). Earlier sessions and the raw transcripts rendered the name variously as **Dolora**, "Dol Ara," "Dora," and "Dolores." This page keeps the filename **`Dolora.md`** to preserve existing backlinks; **Dolora** is retained as an alias.

## What's Known
- Rin's divine source as a **Light Domain Cleric**; her radiant spells and **Channel Divinity** draw on Dol Arrah.
- In [[PP_05_090725_Do_You_Smell_That_Its_Breakfast_Time|Transcript 05]], the **celestial radiant power** that had infused Rin's old syringe (a swirl of black [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] ichor and white radiance) came from a past Channel Divinity — i.e., **Dol Arrah's blessing** — and became one of the three energies (with [[Ugin]]'s essence and Bolas's ichor) used to **cure [[Salena|Selena]]**.
- Rin also called on Dol Arrah for **Divine Intervention** during the marrow-pits collapse (it failed; d100 = 65).

## Key Events

### [[PP_15_031326_A_Scared_Child|PP 15 — A Scared Child]] (03/13/26)
- **Rin's catharsis — the turning point of the boss fight.** Terrified at [[Maze's End]] by the **shadow-echo of her childhood bully** (raised by the [[Augur of Bolas|"Ogre of Bolas"]]), [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]] remembered who she is — *"Wait — I'm the freaking champion of Dol Arrah"* — and slammed the ground with **Channel Divinity** (30-ft radiant AoE, **28 dmg**).
- The burst of **Dol Arrah's light evaporated every shadow-echo and dispelled the black smog** cloaking the Augur (radiant light routing the dark), exposing the boss and turning the fight. The session establishes Rin's relationship to her god as the source of her power to face her own ghost rather than flee it. **This session canonizes the name "Dol Arrah" in play.**

### [[PP_20_053126_I_Miss_You_Buddy|PP 20 — I Miss You, Buddy]] (05/31/26) — ⭐ FINALLY ANSWERS (AS A PUNCHLINE)
- **The running gag pays off — too late.** [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]]'s **Divine Intervention fails the entire final battle** (d100s 97/67/53/63…, [[Dol Arrah]] silent through the whole campaign-deciding fight — *"my god really does fucking suck"*).
- **Then, after the danger has passed**, in the epilogue Rin **finally rolls under 12 (a 2)** at the victory party — and **Dol Arrah finally answers**: *"We're having a party now… No, I couldn't [come sooner]."* The campaign-long unanswered-prayer arc resolves as a **punchline** — the goddess shows up only once it's safe. Rin's faith, tested to the breaking point, is answered as a joke (and she pivots to *building* instead — her Azorius/Boros safety-inspection agency).
