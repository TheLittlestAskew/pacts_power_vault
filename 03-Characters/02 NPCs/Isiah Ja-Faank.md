# Isiah Ja-Faank

A theatrical, grandstanding ally first met by the party in the [[Eighth Precinct Jail]] (Early Session 3). He fights with a **flaming greatsword**, speaks in flourishes, and treats even a battlefield like a stage. As of [[PP_04_082625_The_Spark_and_the_Swarm|Transcript 04]] he has **joined [[The Breakfast Club]]** for the journey to the [[Bone Mine]].

> Transcripts render his name "Isaiah"; canonical spelling is **Isiah Ja-Faank** (Lore Dump, 06/18/2023).

## Description / Appearance
Wields a **fiery greatsword** that he swings with flourish, beheading [[Eternals]] one after another. Flamboyant and showy in battle.

## Personality
Grandiose, cheerful, and theatrical — greets a desperate fight as "the fanfare." Reads as helpful and competent: per [[Rin]], he has good intentions and "executes pretty well," and has pulled the party out of tight spots before. Whether this is genuine heroism or self-aggrandizing showmanship is exactly what divides the party about him.

## The Party's Divided Opinion (T04)
- **[[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]]** — thinks he's a jackass but **not evil**; she's long distrusted him on instinct. (His persona once "wooed" her back in the [[Eighth Precinct Jail]].)
- **[[Rin]]** — broadly **positive/neutral**: he's been helpful, well-intentioned, and effective.
- **[[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]** — **actively hostile**: blames Isiah for [[Salena|Selena]]'s death ("He got my sister killed. He's the reason she died").
- **[[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]]** — owes him her life; he is the one who **broke her out of prison** (her [[Strixhaven]] captivity). She leaps to fight at his side without hesitation.

## Key Events

### Early Session 3 — [[Eighth Precinct Jail]]
- First met by the party while jailed; his persona "wooed" [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]].

### [[PP_04_082625_The_Spark_and_the_Swarm|Transcript 04 — The Spark and the Swarm]] (08/26/2025)
- Found **alone atop [[Niv-Mizzet]]'s corpse**, fending a horde of [[Eternals]] off the Firemind's body with his fiery greatsword
- Recognized by [[Chandra Nalaar\|Chandra]] as the man who **broke her out of prison**; she dropped in to fight beside him
- After [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] cleared a path with a breath weapon, fought back-to-back with Chandra, beheading Eternals
- Asked [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] whether he had **"the box"** (the Dragon Spirit Box) and urged him to use it on Niv-Mizzet's remains
- Once Niv's essence was absorbed, congratulated Chandra and **joined the party**, asking where they were headed next — answer: "the Bone Zone" ([[Bone Mine]])

### [[PP_05_090725_Do_You_Smell_That_Its_Breakfast_Time|Transcript 05 — Do You Smell That? It's Breakfast Time]] (09/07/2025)
- Flew with the party toward the [[Bone Mine]], swapping war stories — a planeswalking traveler ([[Ixalan]], [[Tarkir]], [[Phyrexia]], [[Strixhaven]]); confirmed he was an **undercover "professor" at [[Strixhaven]]** (taught "Adventuring 101" and a class on fighting the undead), and that [[Chandra Nalaar\|Chandra]] attended his classes
- Revealed that after Chandra's capture, **[[Onyx\|Professor Onyx]] snitched the [[Flameseekers of Truth]]' identities to [[Nicol Bolas\|Bolas]]** — none of them survived
- **Knocked unconscious the instant he touched the [[Leather Daddy Game Boy]]** (overwhelmed by its god-souls); [[Chandra Nalaar\|Chandra]] doodled a monocle/mustache/unibrow on his face while he was out; he woke later muttering "cheesecake"
- In the [[Tezzeret]] fight, cleaved corrupted Myconids apart with his fiery longsword ("that is how you grill mushrooms"), suffering exhaustion
- Stayed "in solidarity" as the marrow pits collapsed (produced an umbrella)

### [[PP_06_091725_Soul_Integrated_Soul_Storage_Get_It_Right|Transcript 06 — Soul Integrated Soul Storage, Get It Right]] (09/17/2025)
- Urged the party to **hear [[Liliana Vess\|Liliana]] out** during the confrontation.
- **Tackled away from the fight** with [[Chandra Nalaar\|Chandra]]; Liliana **dimension-doored** them both to the Great Tree of [[Vitu-Ghazi]], **tasking Isiah to care for the unconscious Chandra** and to take [[Nissa]] to the [[Simic Combine]] (where surviving [[Flameseekers of Truth]] are experimented on). Separated from the main party.
