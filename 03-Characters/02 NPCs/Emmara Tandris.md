# Emmara Tandris

A leader of the **[[Selesnya Conclave]]** overseeing the swelling refugee camp at the [[Selesnyan Conclave|Conclave]]. She pledges Selesnya funds to the party's auction effort once a banking system is back online.

> Spelling unconfirmed; the Dashboard Guild Tracker previously listed a garbled "Amara Tangers" — resolved here to canonical **Emmara Tandris** (kept as an alias).

## Campaign Role

The Selesnya representative for the party's **Plan A** fundraising. With the Orzhov banking system dead, she pledges the Conclave's liquidity to bid on the [[Immortal Sun]] **once the [[First Church of Orzhov]] bank is reopened** so the guilds can pool money. A candidate Selesnya token/representative for the [[Niv-Mizzet]] revival ritual.

## Key Events

### [[PP_10_121425_You_Cant_Just_Buy_the_Sun|PP 10 — You Can't Just Buy the Sun]] (12/14/25)
- At the [[Selesnyan Conclave|Conclave]] refugee camp (now a vast camp for Gruul, Orzhov, and Simic refugees), **pledged Selesnya funds** toward buying the [[Immortal Sun]] at auction — contingent on the party getting the **[[First Church of Orzhov]]** bank running so the guilds can pool coin.

### [[PP_17_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 17 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26)
- **At the grand council** of all ten guilds (surfaced [[Maze's End]]), Amara Tandris represents the **[[Selesnya Conclave|Selesnya]]** alongside [[Nissa]] (riding the animate [[Vitu-Ghazi]]) as the guilds put their forces on the ritual die to formally unite against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]].
