# Kwatli Pakal

An **[[Ixalan]] ruler** attending the [[Krenko Incorporated]] masquerade auction in disguise as **[[Isiah Ja-Faank|Isaiah]]**'s companion, under the alias **"Jareth Wig."**

> The session note links "Jareth Wig" and "Kwatli Pakal" as the same figure; canonical page form **Kwatli Pakal** (alias **Jareth Wig**). Spelling unconfirmed.

## Campaign Role

A masked-auction attendee revealed (Rin, Insight 28, IDing the pair) to be an Ixalan ruler traveling incognito alongside [[Isiah Ja-Faank|Isaiah]] (who is himself going by "Johnny"). One of several mystery figures gathering as the [[Immortal Sun]] heist breaks open; his purpose at the auction is unstated.

## Key Events

### [[PP_11_011126_The_Masquerade_Auction|PP 11 — The Masquerade Auction]] (01/11/26)
- Present at the **[[Krenko Incorporated]] masquerade auction** as [[Isiah Ja-Faank|Isaiah]]'s companion, going by the alias **"Jareth Wig."**
- **Revealed as [[Kwatli Pakal]], an Ixalan ruler** in disguise.

### [[PP_13_021326_Hold_the_Wall|PP 13 — Hold the Wall]] (02/13/26)
- **Turned the tide.** As the [[Boros Legion|Boros]]/[[Izzet League|Izzet]] wall was about to collapse, Jareth **opened a portal disgorging hundreds of [[Ixalan]] pirates, merfolk, and priests** (incl. Admiral [[Beckett Brass]] and Captain [[Lannery Storm]]) to **plug the breach** — a payoff of the restored planeswalking ([[Immortal Sun]] off-plane).
- His read on the war: *"Immortal Sun gone, planeswalkers off Ravnica, Bolas throwing a hissy fit — everything's going as planned."* (Also: *"Could always use some more friends, right?"*)
- Now an **openly co-belligerent ally** — the off-plane-reinforcement counterpart to [[Narset]] (who brought dragons over the Azorius).
