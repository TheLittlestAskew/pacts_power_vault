# Nicol Bolas

An ancient Elder Dragon planeswalker and the overarching antagonist of the campaign. He is collecting Power on an interplanar scale — the campaign's central threat. His undead army, the [[Eternals]] (black-plated, blue-glowing soldiers from Amonkhet's necropolis), serves as his military force, and [[Liliana Vess]] works with/for him.

## Campaign Role

Bolas is the figure behind [[The Second Sun]] faction. The party's overarching goal is to oppose him. The [[Immortal Sun]] (which traps planeswalkers on a plane) and the gathering of planeswalkers on [[Ravnica]] are suspected to serve his plans.

## Key Events

### [[PP_01_071325_Into_the_Ghost_Mind|Transcript 01 — Into the Ghost Mind]] (07/13/2025)
- **DM stinger (not party knowledge):** An interplanar bridge opened in [[Golgari Swarm|Golgari]] territory. Rows of [[Eternals]] and god-sized coffins emerged from Amonkhet's necropolis — the first physical appearance of Bolas's invasion force on Ravnica.
- The [[Immortal Sun]]'s activation (trapping all planeswalkers on Ravnica) is suspected to be connected to Bolas / [[The Second Sun]].

### [[PP_03_081025_Thats_Why_No_One_Will_Remember_Your_Name|Transcript 03 — That's Why No One Will Remember Your Name]] (08/10/2025)
- **First in-person appearance.** Rose from [[Bolas's Citadel]] (a black pyramid in the [[Golgari Swarm|Golgari]] district) and proclaimed his judgment over [[Ravnica]]: *"It is time for my judgment for the plane of Ravnica. I am here and I will be the decision maker."*
- **Killed [[Niv-Mizzet]]** in a dragon duel atop the citadel — gazed into the Fire-Mind's soul, conjured a vast dark sphere (*"This is the end for you, Fire-Mind"*), and disintegrated them to bone.
- Afterward retreated to a meditative pose atop the citadel — "aura farming" — visibly **absorbing harvested planeswalker sparks** (orbs rising from across the [[Tenth District]]). [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]] confirmed (Arcana) the sparks are taken from planeswalkers killed via the **[[Elder Spell]]** + **[[Spark-Harvester Trident]]**.
- Did not engage the party directly; they stood down from a rescue of Niv-Mizzet per Niv's own telepathic instruction.

### [[PP_13_012526_Fucking_Barbarians|PP 13 — Fucking Barbarians]] (01/25/26)
- **Escalated to overt genocide.** The instant the **[[Immortal Sun]]** left Ravnica (taken off-plane by [[Narset]]) and **planeswalking was restored** — trapped planeswalkers' light-beams streaking the sky — Bolas (dragon form) **screamed "NO!"** loud enough to crack the city.
- Climbed his **[[Bolas's Citadel\|Citadel]]** in the [[Golgari Swarm\|Golgari]] District and **fired a beam of black energy across the [[Azorius Senate\|Azorius]] District**, **disintegrating swaths of it and cutting the Azorius HQ in half** (thoughts of [[Lavinia]]). The session ends on Bolas wreaking city-wide destruction — the war now openly apocalyptic.

### [[PP_14_021326_Hold_the_Wall|PP 14 — Hold the Wall]] (02/13/26)
- **The ultimatum.** Atop his [[Bolas's Citadel|Citadel]], Bolas continues **razing the [[Azorius Senate|Azorius]] District** with spark-fueled black energy and broadcasts his terms: *if he can't have the planeswalkers to ascend to godhood, he'll lay waste to all Ravnica.* (*"Enough games, enough is enough — it is time to be done with this!"*)
- **Deployed his God-Eternals** — **[[God-Eternal Bontu|Bontu]]** & **[[God-Eternal Kefnet|Kefnet]]** + the bulk of the Eternal horde to the [[Boros Legion|Boros]]/[[Izzet League|Izzet]] front, and **[[God-Eternal Rhonas|Rhonas]]** + an army to the [[Azorius Senate|Azorius]].
- **Millions of Eternals** poured from **portal-gates at his Citadel**; Bolas **raked a beam of black energy across the field**, vaporizing **thousands of his own Eternals** and a stretch of battlements.
- His **Citadel is tapping the [[Leylines]]** — a major contributor to their catastrophic unraveling (with [[Jace]]'s death and war stress) that now threatens to shatter the plane. A raw Leyline crater opens in the Azorius.
- Did not engage the party directly; remains atop the Citadel as the front line **holds** against him.

### [[PP_16_031326_A_Scared_Child|PP 16 — A Scared Child]] (03/13/26)
- **The war is now inside the party's heads.** Though Bolas does not appear in person, the **[[Augur of Bolas|"Ogre of Bolas"]]** — his **hatred-construct**, born of the corrupted black [[Leylines]] (poisoned by his miasma, rooted in [[Bolas's Citadel]]) — fights the party at [[Maze's End]], weaponizing **each PC's worst memory** (Bolas's own face is the echo it raises for [[Ogre]]).
- The construct's **energy-absorption** and the persistence of the corruption reinforce that the **long-term cure is destroying his [[Bolas's Citadel|Citadel]]** (the root of the Leyline corruption). The fight is **unresolved** at the session's end.

### [[PP_19_041926_Hi_Im_Ogre|PP 19 — Hi, I'm Ogre]] (04/19/26)
- **Untouchable on his throne — the finale's boss, undefeated.** Atop the **400-ft black pyramid** ([[Bolas's Citadel]]), Bolas sits beneath a blinding-white **ball of harvested sparks**, **siphoning** it into himself — *"drinking down everybody's stolen souls like it was nothing."* While he siphons he is **outlined/empowered by the spark energy and effectively invulnerable**: [[Varis Aestra|Varis]]'s direct **nat-20 Scorching Ray dissipated** against him.
- **[[Liliana Vess]]** (the [[The Chain Veil|Chain Veil]] over her face) **marionettes his 3 remaining God-Eternals** ([[God-Eternal Bontu|Bontu]], [[God-Eternal Rhonas|Rhonas]], + a third whose name is unconfirmed in the transcript; [[God-Eternal Oketra|Oketra]] already downed) from beside him.
- His corruption had **nearly disintegrated the halfling death-god [[Ur-Ogre-lan]] to ash** (only the god's face left) — but [[Ogre]] **plunged into the spark-ball, absorbed its power, and reunited with the god**, returning **whole with Bolas's corruption purged.** The sparks themselves **remain with Bolas.**
- **Finale deferred:** the session **ends mid-assault** with Bolas still siphoning, still invulnerable, and **undefeated** — the kill is the **finale (05/31)**. (The spark-ball likely must be disrupted to make him vulnerable.) *Do not import any Bolas-defeat / finale outcome here.*

### [[PP_20_051426_I_Just_Need_Your_Soul|PP 20 — I Just Need Your Soul]] (05/14/26)
- **Still siphoning, still invulnerable — but his army is taken from him.** Throughout the summit battle Bolas keeps **siphoning the orb of harvested sparks** (~50 ft above the platform), **untouched and invulnerable** while he feeds. He does not engage the party directly.
- **⭐ His God-Eternals flipped against him.** The party freed his puppeteer **[[Liliana Vess|Liliana]]** — [[Ogre]] siphoned her soul to break the [[The Chain Veil|Chain Veil]]'s control, and [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] (Orzhov Guildmaster) **rewrote her contract to [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]]**. Freed, Liliana **whips her hand and turns [[God-Eternal Bontu|Bontu]], [[God-Eternal Rhonas|Rhonas]], and [[God-Eternal Kefnet|Kefnet]] against Bolas** — they "crack back to life and stomp toward the orb." **Bolas's own army is now the party's.**
- **Finale setup:** the session **ends mid-encounter** with Bolas **still siphoning and undefeated**, his God-Eternals turning on him and the spark-orb **slowly shrinking** as he absorbs it. The kill is the **finale (05/31)** — likely requires disrupting the orb. *Do not import the finale outcome here.*

### [[PP_21_053126_I_Miss_You_Buddy|PP 21 — I Miss You, Buddy]] (05/31/26) — ⭐ THE FINALE: DEFEATED & CONTAINED
- **The summit war of attrition.** Bolas opens by shrugging off the God-Eternals' strike and unleashing **AoE breath** (fire/necrotic/force) + a **necrotic blast that blinds** much of the party; as a **Dragon-God (spell save DC 22)** he is **near-untouchable while siphoning the spark-orb.** It takes *everyone* — [[Liliana Vess|Liliana]]'s flipped God-Eternals draining his power, **[[Rakdos]] dropping [[A Shady Individual|Penny]]** onto his shoulder to hack his scales, **[[Molly]]** dueling a God-Eternal, dragon-form **[[Varis Aestra|Varis]]** (up to 65 Force), **[[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]'s** Ava's-Longbow support, **[[Teferi]]** returning through a portal, and **[[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] caving him in with [[HellSmasher]]** (incl. a gleeful **66-damage pinky-toe smash**).
- **⭐ Choking the siphon.** **[[Ogre]]** (now **[[Ur-Ogre-lan]]**) realizes he can't move Bolas, so he uses **Mage Hand + Eldritch Blast** to **push the orb of sparks away** from him, foot by foot, **starving the siphon** ([[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]]'s Arcana confirms it's working).
- **⭐ The fall.** **[[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] lands the decisive blow** — a **Hail of Thorns + Ava's Longbow** shot to Bolas's exposed solar plexus. He drops to a knee; when he tries to yank the spark-orb back, **the tether frays and snaps.** He coughs black blood — *"No, no, this cannot be."* — and collapses.
- **The harvest & the seal.** Down but **not dead**, Bolas has his spark **absorbed by [[Liliana Vess|Liliana]]'s Lazotep-plated God-Eternals.** **[[Ogre]] tries to siphon his soul with Duskblade and FAILS** — Bolas is an elder dragon, *part of what made existence.* **[[Ugin]] appears, fully corporeal**, and counsels that Bolas **cannot be destroyed, only contained.** Bolas's soul is **sealed into the [[Dragonhedron]]** ([[Varis Aestra|Varis]]'s Dragon Spirit Box), and **Ugin sentences himself to imprison Bolas** in his own realm — bowing, thanking the party for ending a multi-millennia family feud, and vanishing with the box.
- **Outcome: DEFEATED & CONTAINED.** The shadow over the entire campaign is **sealed away, not destroyed** — *"some evils you can only hold at bay."* [[Niv-Mizzet]] declares peace for Ravnica; the multiverse is saved. The kill belonged to no one PC — *"It was all of us."*
