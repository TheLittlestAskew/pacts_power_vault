# Kalmar

A Goliath guard at **[[Krenko Incorporated]]**'s masquerade auction — revealed as the **doppelganger** the party met in a past one-shot. Beneath the badge, he is part of **[[Narset]]'s** crew, planted to let her in after the auction so she can steal and destroy the **[[Immortal Sun]]**.

> Spelling unconfirmed; canonical page form **Kalmar**. (The transcript also tags the DM as "Kalmar" in places — speaker attribution around the auction is occasionally ambiguous; this page is the NPC.)

## Campaign Role

An inside man for the Sun heist. [[Ogre]] befriends him on a chandelier perch at the masquerade ballroom and reads him as "a friend" (Insight nat-20). Kalmar reveals he serves **[[Narset]]**, whose plan — to **take the Immortal Sun and destroy it** — happens to match the party's own goal; his job is to **let Narset in after the auction**. One of the web of friendly-but-secret co-conspirators (with [[Rekka]]) converging on the Sun.
