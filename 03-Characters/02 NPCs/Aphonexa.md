# Aphonexa

The **goddess of rebirth** — the ascended form of **[[Salena|Selena]]**, [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]'s sister, who rose from the [[Bone Mine]] essence pit. Patron deity of the [[Bone Mine]] Myconids. **Named by the party** in [[PP_06_091725_Soul_Integrated_Soul_Storage_Get_It_Right|Transcript 06]] (she chose "Aphonexa" from their suggestions).

## Description / Nature
An ethereal, white-eyed projection of immense power. [[Rin]] (Arcana 26) reads the visible figure as a *projection* — her true self remains in the essence pit; she **cannot leave the [[Bone Mine]] physically**, but her **blessing travels with the party**. Time runs strangely for her ("it's been quite some time since I've been called that"). Her domain is **rebirth / raising the dead**.

## Backstory
Killed in Early Session 4; reborn as a **were-zombie** via the [[Bone Mine]] essence pits; corrupted by [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]'s ichor and cured by the party (T05); submerged in the essence pit and **ascended** (T06). See [[Salena]] for her full mortal history.

## Abilities (observed)
- **Mass revival** — raised the entire Myconid colony, including ones dead before the party arrived (incl. [[Thal]]).
- **Blessing-spore** — bestowed a boon on [[Orphie]], [[Ogre]], [[Rin]], and [[Varis Aestra|Varis]].
- **"Seed of renewal"** — a one-time free Revivify-type cast granted to [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]].

## Key Events

### [[PP_07_100525_The_Reylana_Infiltration|PP 7 — The Reylana Infiltration]] (10/05/25)
- Her **heightened senses** (since ascending) let her send [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] a **prophetic vision** during the long rest at the [[Bone Mine]]: the **[[Reylana mansion|Reylana Estate]]** in [[Wayport]] surrounded by a Devkarin/[[Lotleth]] army awaiting orders. Sanis forced himself awake to warn the party.

### [[PP_10_113025_What_Are_You_Waiting_For_Sanis|PP 10 — What Are You Waiting For, Sanis?]] (11/30/25)
- **Actively intervened in a mortal fight for the first time.** As their mother [[Lady Reylana|Lady Hyena]] lunged a final time for [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]], Aphonexa **reached down from godhood to grant Sanis advantage** on the killing blow — *"It seems like you need some help,"* *"we're not supposed to interfere, but this is important."* Sanis ran their mother through. A profound beat: the ascended sister steadies her brother's hand to kill the parent who murdered her.
- ⚠ **Rules/lore flag:** this is the first time Aphonexa **intervenes in mortal affairs** despite being bound to the [[Bone Mine]] (previously only her blessing traveled). The mechanics of her reach/intervention are **unclear** — flagged.

### [[PP_18_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 18 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26)
- **A second worshipping colony — the party are her "chosen."** A colony of **purple [[Myconids]]** in the tunnels between the surface and the [[The Rot Warren|Rot Warren]] **worships Aphonexa** (the god the party created). When [[Dalya Bara|Dahlia]]'s [[The Drillinator|Drillinator]] bored through their home — killing one myconid — their head priest **[[Eddie]]** demanded *"a like for like."* After [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]] **revived the slain myconid** (Raise Dead via [[Varis Aestra|Varis]]'s Orzhov gauntlet — the "Pacts and Power" loan), the colony **hailed the party as Aphonexa's "chosen"/creators** and joined them as allies. A new Aphonexa-faction asset heading into the finale. (Aphonexa herself does not appear; her worship reaches beyond the [[Bone Mine]].)
