# Vuthala

A **merfolk** known to the party from their **[[Ixalan]]** arc, found imprisoned as a captive **[[Zombie-Simic mutants|zombie-Simic specimen]]** in a tube inside the [[Second Sun Labs]] inner office.

> Spelling unconfirmed (transcript renders variants "Vthala / Futhala / Muzala / Luthala"); canonical page form **Vuthala** per the dedup table.

## Campaign Role

An old [[Ixalan]] acquaintance discovered in captivity — a rescue hook and a likely ally-to-be. One of the fully-formed zombie-Simic mutants the facility manufactures.

## Key Events

### [[PP_08_101925_Blood_Runs_Thicker_Than_Water|PP 8 — Blood Runs Thicker Than Water]] (10/19/25)
- Found inside the inner office, among tubes of fully-formed [[Zombie-Simic mutants]], when [[Prime Speaker Megandar]] opened the door.
- **Recognized by the party** from their time on [[Ixalan]] — now a captive zombie-Simic specimen. Establishes a captive-rescue thread (alongside the begging **crab-humanoid** captive).

### [[PP_10_113025_What_Are_You_Waiting_For_Sanis|PP 10 — What Are You Waiting For, Sanis?]] (11/30/25)
- During the drowning-lab fight, **[[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] identified his tube** (Insight 24) and **smashed it open** with [[Blazebringer]], freeing him.
- **Turned to the party's side** after Orphie's appeal/intimidation (6 → 14 on the second attempt): *"I want you to remember that you're more scared of me than you are of them."* As a flesh-golem were-zombie, he **wrist-locked one of the new were-zombies** for the party.
- Reduced and knocked out by fight's end; **reverted to his merfolk form** (shrunk back) and was swept out through the cracked ceiling by dragon-[[Varis Aestra|Varis]] alongside Sanis, Rin, and Orphie. **Unconscious, in the party's care** at the surface of the Simic-district lake. The broader "Selena sisters"/captive-specimens thread remains open (only Vuthala and [[Brula]] freed so far).

### [[PP_11_121425_You_Cant_Just_Buy_the_Sun|PP 11 — You Can't Just Buy the Sun]] (12/14/25)
- **Handed off to [[Benji]]**, who ferried him to the **[[Selesnyan Conclave|Selesnya Conclave]]** to recover — completing the party's ally-delivery thread. Now recovering off-screen at the Conclave.
