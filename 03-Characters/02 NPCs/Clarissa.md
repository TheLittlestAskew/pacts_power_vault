# Clarissa

A civilian friend and roommate of [[Rin]] and [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]], sharing their apartment in Sunhome. A bartender by trade. Long-standing friend of Orphie's (gave Orphie her [[Stapler]] back in high school) and a confidante the party shares "the tea" with.

## Description / Appearance
Down-to-earth civilian; works at a bar in Sunhome whose name keeps changing under new management — currently "the Redeemed Angel" (a running joke, as the party can never remember it).

## Personality
Easygoing, social, gossip-loving, devoted to her job. Refuses to stay home from work even during a crisis ("I work at a bar, I literally cannot do that"). Wants to be a manager, not an assistant manager.

## Relationships
- [[Rin]] & [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] — Roommates and close friends.
- [[Ogre]] — Friend; enjoys her stories.
- [[Salena|Selena]] — Knows her; aware she was expected to stop by.
