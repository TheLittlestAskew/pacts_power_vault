# Lavinia

A leader of the **[[Azorius Senate]]** who attended the **grand council** at the surfaced [[Maze's End]] with the Azorius sphinxes — and notably **alongside [[Varis Aestra|Varis]]'s father** (the Aestras' Azorius connection). Her fate had been an open worry since [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] **cut the Azorius HQ in half** (PP 13) and the district was nearly lost (PP 14); the council confirms she **survived.**

> **Spelling note (raw transcript):** rendered here as canonical **Lavinia** (the standard MTG Azorius lieutenant-arrester); no spell-check pass per Taylor.

## Campaign Role
The Azorius representative at the campaign's grand-council coalition. Her on-page appearance resolves the long-standing "Lavinia in peril" flag from the razing of the Azorius District — she is alive and brings the [[Azorius Senate|Azorius]] (sphinxes in tow) into the formal alliance against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]. Her arrival with **Varis's father** reinforces the Aestra family's Azorius ties.

## Key Events

### [[PP_18_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 18 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26)
- Attended the **grand council** at the surfaced [[Maze's End]] representing the **[[Azorius Senate]]** — arriving with **Azorius sphinxes** and **[[Varis Aestra|Varis]]'s father.**
- Joined all ten guilds in **channeling their forces onto the ritual die** to formally unite against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] — confirming she survived the destruction of the Azorius HQ.
