# Lady Reylana

The matriarch of the **Reylana Family** and **[[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]'s mother**, seated beside **[[Lord Reylana]]** at the heart of the [[Second Sun]]'s underwater [[Simic Combine|Simic]] lab as the party confronts them in PP 8.

## Campaign Role

Co-architect (with Lord Reylana) of the Reylana-blooded were-zombie weapon program and a major antagonist in [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]'s personal arc. She is implicated in [[Salena|Selena]]'s weaponization alongside her husband.

## Key Events

### [[PP_08_101925_Blood_Runs_Thicker_Than_Water|PP 8 — Blood Runs Thicker Than Water]] (10/19/25)
- Found seated with [[Lord Reylana]] on the dry platform at the inner office of the underwater [[Second Sun Labs]], overseeing the [[Zombie-Simic mutants]] program.
- Present through the confrontation in which Lord Reylana confessed to murdering [[Salena|Selena]] by design and [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] renounced the family.
- **Combat triggered, initiative rolled — deferred to the next session (cliffhanger).**

### [[PP_10_113025_What_Are_You_Waiting_For_Sanis|PP 10 — What Are You Waiting For, Sanis?]] (11/30/25)
- **Waltzed with [[Lord Reylana|Lord Modest]] on the dais** through the fight as an **invisible/teleporting dual-dagger striker** (poison, crit-machine) who twice nearly killed [[Ogre]] — both lethal crits negated by [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]]'s **Warding Flares**. In the flashback she is shown slipping young Sanis a **piece of chocolate** after his father forced him to kill the servant.
- Healed her husband to sustain his were-zombie regeneration; aimed a powerful spell at Sanis that **[[Ogre]] Counterspelled** — as it failed she mouthed *"I'm sorry."*
- Taunted Sanis in turn (*"I always knew you had good aim, son"*).
- **Killed by [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]:** as she lunged a final time for him, his ascended sister **[[Aphonexa]] reached down from godhood to grant him advantage** (*"we're not supposed to interfere, but this is important"*), and Sanis **ran her through** with a fencer's parry. She also **died saying *"I'm sorry,"*** sinking into the flood. Her repeated apologies and the Counterspelled-spell beat imply she was **conflicted/coerced** (logged as implied, not confirmed).
