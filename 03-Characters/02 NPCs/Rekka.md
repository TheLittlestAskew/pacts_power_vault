# Rekka

A guard the party recognizes from the **[[Bone Mine]]**, now working **[[Krenko Incorporated]]** HQ security. Speaks in an "Australian-Scottish" cadence and greets the party with a knowing wink — a quietly friendly face inside an unfriendly building.

> Spelling unconfirmed; canonical page form **Rekka**.

## Campaign Role

A covert friendly inside Krenko's tower. Recognized from the [[Bone Mine]], Rekka greets and routes the party (to [[Kiku]]) and winks — signaling she may be a possible ally for the looming heist/auction.

## Key Events

### [[PP_11_121425_You_Cant_Just_Buy_the_Sun|PP 11 — You Can't Just Buy the Sun]] (12/14/25)
- Met the party at the door of [[Krenko Incorporated]] — recognized from the [[Bone Mine]] — and **winked** at them before routing them in to [[Kiku]]. Read as friendly-ish; a potential inside contact for Plan B.

### [[PP_12_011126_The_Masquerade_Auction|PP 12 — The Masquerade Auction]] (01/11/26)
- **Confirmed as a covert ally** at the masquerade: slipped the party an **earpiece**, winks, and a **"tap-it-if-things-go-down" token for [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]]**, and warned that **"something will go down tonight; the Sun is the last item."**
- Guarded the stage-left door near where [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]] & [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] posted up — one of the inside helpers (with [[Kalmar]]) on the heist.

### [[PP_13_012526_Fucking_Barbarians|PP 13 — Fucking Barbarians]] (01/25/26)
- Tied to **[[Narset]]**'s crew / **[[Ravnica Trading Incorporated]]** ([[Shaylin]]'s Tarkir company): the **red-haired driver** of the RTI carriage glimpsed leaving the burning auction is **presumed to be Rekka** (unconfirmed). Escaped with the crew. **Ally.**
