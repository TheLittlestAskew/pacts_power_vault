# Niv-Mizzet

[[Parun]] and guildmaster of the [[Izzet League]] — the dragon-planeswalker known as **the Fire-Mind**. Uses they/them. A beloved leader to the party (and a far better one, in their eyes, than [[Ral Zarek|Ral]]).

## Description

A massive dragon wreathed in crackling electricity, capable of lightning magic and hurling chunks of skyscraper with their tail. Andro / they-them.

## Death — The Duel with Nicol Bolas (Transcript 03)

During the siege of the [[Tenth District]], Niv-Mizzet flew across the battlefield to engage **[[Nicol Bolas]]** atop [[Bolas's Citadel]], declaring *"This is my plane and you will not meddle in our affairs so easily."* The dragon duel went well at first — lightning lashes and thrown buildings — until Bolas gazed into Niv-Mizzet's soul, conjured a vast dark sphere (*"This is the end for you, Fire-Mind"*), and **disintegrated them**, leaving only a skeleton that crumpled to the ground. The body was too destroyed to revivify.

Before dying, Niv-Mizzet sent **[[Varis Aestra|Varis]]** a telepathic message: *"you'll know what to do — let this happen, and wait,"* and winked/nodded at him in passing. At session's end, Varis felt **"the box"** among his belongings grow warm — later understood to hold Niv-Mizzet's preserved spirit (the **Dragon Spirit Box**).

## Aftermath — Essence Absorbed (Transcript 04, 08/26/25)

En route to the [[Bone Mine]], the party found Niv-Mizzet's skeletal corpse swarmed by [[Eternals]] and defended by [[Isiah Ja-Faank]]. After clearing the horde, Isiah urged Varis to use "the box." With **[[Varis Aestra|Varis]]'s Arcana (22)** and **[[Ogre]] acting as the conduit** (a connect-the-dots vision; Wisdom 9, Strength 14 to pull the tether), the remains glowed red-blue-purple and **Niv-Mizzet's essence was drawn into the Dragon Spirit Box** in a tornado of energy. A shockwave knocked back the surrounding Eternals. The Firemind's spirit is now physically secured in the box — though Varis reports **no felt change in power** yet. This is a key step toward the **Revive Niv-Mizzet** quest.

## Revival — Maze's End (PP 15, 02/22/26)

At **[[Maze's End]]** the party completed the reforge ritual ([[Ogre]]'s [[Black Lotus]] wish + the **[[Firemind Vessel]]** holding Niv's soul + the 10 guild tokens). The Vessel rose, opened, and **formed a portal in the ceiling** at the Leyline confluence. **[[Ogre]] jumped through into darkness** and met Niv-Mizzet in a **6-ft, red-and-blue-haired humanoid form** — who revealed **only Ogre could be there** (*"something special about you"*) and asked Ogre to **pull them back through.** Ogre dragged Niv-Mizzet out by the hand — but Niv began emerging in **full dragon form** (a massive claw/talon descending through the widening portal), not the humanoid the party expected. **Niv-Mizzet is being revived** (mid-emergence at session's end, gated by the [[Augur of Bolas|"Ogre of Bolas"]] boss fight).

> ⚠ **Flag:** Niv-Mizzet's emerged **form is a full dragon** (the party met them as a humanoid through the portal, then they came out as a dragon). The revival is **in progress** — not yet complete.

## Rebirth — The Living Guildpact (PP 17, 04/02/26)

**Niv-Mizzet is fully reborn — and reborn as the [[Living Guildpact]].** The mid-emergence ends when [[Ogre]] **feeds a shrinking mushroom to the half-stuck dragon**: Niv instantly compresses from **gargantuan to Large**, pops free of the [[Firemind Vessel]] ceiling portal, and — in a **high-pitched helium voice** — thanks Ogre for saving "about 18 seconds." Free at last, Niv reveals a **decagon crest** on their chest bearing **all ten guilds' colors** (the embodiment of the reforged [[Guildpact]]; the DM corrected "dodecagon/dodecahedron" live to **decagon** — 10 sides for 10 guilds). With **all five colors of mana compressed in one claw**, Niv **shears off half the [[Augur of Bolas|Augur]]'s form**, then **collapses it into the floor** on initiative — *"the Ogre is no more"* — absorbing the black mana into the crest. Combat ends.

Standing wreathed in a **white aura** (all colors at peace), Niv addresses the party as **peers**:
- **Endorses the party's leadership pick** — validates replacing [[Ral Zarek|Ral]] with [[Dalya Bara|Dahlia]] as Izzet leader.
- **Stabilizes the rogue red [[Leylines|Leyline]]** (the one that disintegrated the Tarkir dragons/Eternals near [[Azorius Senate|Azorius]]) — *stemming the bleeding* but unable to undo damage already done: *"There is no undo button… we technically do have one, but I don't think we want to use it."*
- **Praises [[Ogre]]'s aptitude** ("Another dragon on my plane?" to [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]]); notes [[Benji]] is present but pointedly **not helping** ("so Dimir").
- **Gathers all ten Leylines to recharge**, then **glyphs the ritual die/platform** and **teleports the party** (briefly through the Blind Eternities) up to the **surface central plaza** (the spot where [[Jace Beleren|Jace]] gave his speech — full circle).

> **Note on [[Ogre]]'s wish:** Ogre's [[Black Lotus]] wish was *"make me a new Guildpact so I could be one too."* **Niv is the Guildpact — Ogre is not** (the crest is Niv's). Ogre's Leyline-grabbing afterward reads as him still reaching for that unfulfilled wish — flagged as an open thread.

## The Grand Council (PP 18, 04/05/26)

**Niv-Mizzet convenes the whole world.** Back to **gargantuan** (after planeswalking) and standing in the surface **central plaza** (the surfaced [[Maze's End]]), Niv places a claw on the **central ritual die**; the **ten guild seats** channel a **prismatic beacon** to the sky — like [[Ral Zarek|Ral]]'s [[Interplanar Beacon]], but calling **only Ravnica's guild-aligned forces.** Every standing guild's representatives converge: **Gruul** ([[Sharp Dog]], [[Borborygmos]]), **Boros** ([[Aurelia]], [[Molly]]), **Izzet** ([[Dalya Bara|Dahlia]], in a mech), **Azorius** ([[Lavinia]] + sphinxes; [[Varis Aestra|Varis]]'s father), **Dimir** ([[Hikara]], [[A Shady Individual|Penny]], [[Benji]]), **Selesnya** ([[Emmara Tandris|Amara Tandris]] + [[Nissa]] riding the animate [[Vitu-Ghazi]]), **Golgari** ([[Yarod Votsavo]] atop [[Steve]] the Rotworm), **Orzhov** ([[Varis Aestra|Varis]] as leader), plus [[Beckett Brass]], [[Lannery Storm]], and **[[Carl]]** (the lone Simic — *"if he's here, he's the guild leader"*). **[[Teferi]]** arrives with the **[[Zhalfir army]].** All guilds **put their energies on the die to formally unite their forces against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]**; **[[Benji]] gestures [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]** to the **Dimir** die (Sanis carries Lazav, the original Dimir leader, within him) and Sanis does the honors.

**Niv's directive:** confront [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] *directly* at his **[[Bolas's Citadel|Citadel]]** — but the army can't punch through in time, so the party needs **another path in** (→ the [[The Drillinator|Drillinator]] + the freed Rotworms tunnel to the Citadel).

- **Convened the grand council of all ten guilds** at the surfaced [[Maze's End]] (gargantuan again), lighting the **prismatic guild-beacon** that summoned Ravnica's guild forces to formally **unite against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]].**
- Set the **directive to confront Bolas directly at the Citadel** — and, since the army can't punch through, sent the party to find **another path in** (the Drillinator/Rotworm tunnel route).

## Legacy & Quests

- The party's standing quest to **Revive Niv-Mizzet** (see [[Campaign Dashboard]]) centers on the spirit preserved in Varis's Dragon Spirit Box, requiring a representative/artifact from every guild and a ritual at the [[Implicit Maze]].
- With Niv-Mizzet dead, **[[Ral Zarek|Ral]]** becomes Izzet guildmaster — a development the party resents, fueling their grudge against Ral.
