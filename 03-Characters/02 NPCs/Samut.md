# Samut

## Description / Appearance
A human warrior from the plane of [[Amonkhet]]. An ally who previously helped the party during the trials on Amonkhet (a prior one-shot, established above-table).

## Backstory
Samut aided the party during their Amonkhet trials. With the planeswalkers drawn to [[Ravnica]] and the [[Eternals]] invading, she is now fighting on the allied front lines and recognizes the Amonkhet [[Eternals]] and their God-Eternal commanders.

## Personality
Direct, urgent, knowledgeable about Amonkhet's gods and Eternals. Warns allies of lethal threats without hesitation.

## Relationships
- [[Amonkhet]] — Home plane.
- [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] — Recognized and warned him directly during the battle.
- [[God-Eternal Oketra]] — Knows her by name and reputation ("she never misses a shot").
