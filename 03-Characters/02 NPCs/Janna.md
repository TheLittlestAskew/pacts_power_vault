# Janna

A boisterous **halfling girl** from the **[[Blistercoils|Blister Coils]]** who crashed [[Krenko]]'s masquerade auction (perched on the chandelier, bidding on the [[Guildpact]] shreds) and then tagged along with the party through the chaos. Loud, destructive, and obsessed with a game of tag.

> Spelling unconfirmed (raw transcript); canonical page form **Janna**. The "Blister Coils" she names is the [[Blistercoils]] district.

## Campaign Role

A stray-child hook acquired during the burning-auction escape. Janna refuses to go home, plays tag along dragon-[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]'s spine during the flight out, and is ultimately parked in the **[[Selesnyan Conclave]] medicine workshop**, where her destructive energy ("Janna smash!") is redirected into grinding herbs. She mentions a friend who "stands on corners and sells to people" — blue skin, white hair, trench coat — which the party recognizes as **[[Lance|Lance Filo]]**, revealing Orphie's long-lost companion is alive on Ravnica.

## Key Events

### [[PP_12_012526_Fucking_Barbarians|PP 12 — Fucking Barbarians]] (01/25/26)
- Crashed the [[Krenko Incorporated]] auction from the **chandelier**, bidding against the party (and the masked pair) on the **shreds of the First [[Guildpact]]**.
- **Tagged along** through the burning amphitheater and onto dragon-[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]'s back during the escape, playing tag on his spine.
