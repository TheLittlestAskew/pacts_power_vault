# Lady Aestra

**[[Varis Aestra|Varis]]'s** mother — a displaced **[[Orzhov Syndicate|Orzhov]]** noble sheltering, with **[[Lord Aestra]]**, in the Orzhov quarter of the [[Selesnyan Conclave|Selesnya Conclave]] refugee camp. A key pillar of the plan to reopen the First Church of Orzhov bank.

> Spelling unconfirmed (Aestra/Aesthra); canonical page form **Lady Aestra**.

## Campaign Role

One of the Aestra parents anchoring the party's **Plan A** (buy the [[Immortal Sun]]). As Orzhov refugees with standing, she and [[Lord Aestra]] agree to **rally tellers and liquidity** to reopen the **[[First Church of Orzhov]]** bank once the party scouts it.

## Key Events

### [[PP_11_121425_You_Cant_Just_Buy_the_Sun|PP 11 — You Can't Just Buy the Sun]] (12/14/25)
- Reunited with her son [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] at the [[Selesnyan Conclave|Conclave]] refugee camp.
- With [[Lord Aestra]], agreed to **rally Orzhov tellers and liquidity to reopen the [[First Church of Orzhov]] bank** for the auction fundraising.
- The Aestras provided **5 Superior Healing Potions** to the party via the family's "premium tier insurance" — redeemed by [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]] at the [[Selesnyan Conclave|Conclave]] doctor.

### [[PP_13_012526_Fucking_Barbarians|PP 13 — Fucking Barbarians]] (01/25/26)
- With [[Lord Aestra]] (now installed by [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] as **Orzhov bank manager**), will **lead the Orzhov refugees back to reopen the [[Orzhov Syndicate\|Orzhov]]** using the ~100k gp [[Pecunia]] reserve via the reopened [[First Church of Orzhov]] bank.
