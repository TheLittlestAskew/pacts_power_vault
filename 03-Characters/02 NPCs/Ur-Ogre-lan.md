# Ur-Ogre-lan

The **halfling god of death** — and the revealed **true, whole self of [[Ogre]].** Eons ago the god cut a part of himself loose — **his heart**, the icon of love and passion — and sent it out into the multiverse to live mortal lives. That heart is [[Ogre]]. In **[[PP_19_041926_Hi_Im_Ogre|PP 19]]** the two are made one again.

> **Spelling note (raw transcript, no spell-check per Taylor):** rendered variously **"Ur-Ogre-lan / Arugalon / Aroglon / Zaddy Daddy."** Kept as **Ur-Ogre-lan** here. ⚠ **Distinct from [[Argo Lot]]** (Ogre's father-essence / [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]'s pact-shard, also nicknamed "Zaddy Daddy") — same affectionate nickname, **different entity.**

## Campaign Role
The lore linchpin that recontextualizes [[Ogre]] entirely: the party's chaos-goblin mascot is the **severed heart of a death god**, sent out to *live, love, and feel* among mortals. His soul/death magic, his god-absorptions, and the S16 *"he is me and me is him"* foreshadowing all retroactively trace to this. By [[PP_19_041926_Hi_Im_Ogre|PP 19]] the god has been **nearly disintegrated to ash by [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]'s corruption** — only his face remains — and is made whole when Ogre returns to him.

## Key Events
### [[PP_19_041926_Hi_Im_Ogre|PP 19 — Hi, I'm Ogre]] (04/19/26)
- During the assault on [[Bolas's Citadel]], [[Ogre]] plunged his blade **Dusk** into the harvested spark-ball to absorb its power, took **65 + 74 radiant**, and **fell unconscious / "died."**
- Ogre woke in a memory (the forest, picking mushrooms with his adoptive father [[Rod]]), then **planeswalked (NAT 20)** — not to the Blind Eternities but to the **seats of the halfling pantheon.**
- There Ur-Ogre-lan, **nearly disintegrated to ash by Bolas's corruption (only his face left)**, revealed the truth: ***"You are me and I am you."*** Eons ago the god removed his **heart** (love/passion) and splayed it into the world to live mortal lives **as Ogre.**
- Ogre **planeswalked *into* Ur-Ogre-lan's body** (occupying the same space — the table likened it to *"Steven Universe putting his heart back in"*). The **souls Ogre had absorbed reconstituted the god's body**; memories flooded in (the pantheon council, ferrying the dead, his own birth).
- **Ogre and Ur-Ogre-lan became one** — Ogre is now **whole: the halfling god of death, Bolas's corruption purged.** He planeswalked back beside [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] **older, bearded, wise, and calm** (no longer chaotic): ***"Hi, I'm Ogre."***
