# Scarab God

An **Amonkhet death-deity** referenced in the DM's **#Lore Dump** (12/07/24) — the god associated with raising the **[[Eternals]]** (the lazotep-plated, god-metal undead Bolas mass-produces).

## Campaign Role
Lore backdrop for the campaign's central undead threat: the **[[Eternals]]** and **God-Eternals** ([[God-Eternal Oketra|Oketra]], [[God-Eternal Bontu|Bontu]], [[God-Eternal Rhonas|Rhonas]], [[God-Eternal Kefnet|Kefnet]]) that [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] turns loose on [[Ravnica]] from [[Bolas's Citadel]]. The Scarab God sits behind that Amonkhet eternalization machinery.
