# Lance

An **Air Genasi rogue/thief** and **former member of [[The Breakfast Club]]** (player: Tenthebrutal) — long inactive, having left the party to care for his father. In **PP 13** he is **revealed alive on Ravnica**, apparently working as a **street-corner dealer** (blue skin, white hair, trench coat), known to the halfling girl **[[Janna]]**.

> Canonical page form **Lance** (alias **Lance Filo**); the [[Campaign Dashboard]] roster lists him as an inactive PC. Created in NPCs to track his in-fiction reappearance without altering the PC roster.

## Campaign Role

A beloved old companion of [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]]'s resurfacing as a major personal hook. His whereabouts had been an open question since he left the party; PP 13 places him on Ravnica as a dealer "who stands on corners and sells to people," a lead the party intends to chase.

## Key Events

### [[PP_13_012526_Fucking_Barbarians|PP 13 — Fucking Barbarians]] (01/25/26)
- **Revealed alive on Ravnica** through [[Janna]]'s description of her friend — an Air Genasi (blue skin, white hair, trench coat) who "stands on corners and sells to people," recognized as **Lance Filo**, Orphie's long-lost former companion (apparently a dealer now).
