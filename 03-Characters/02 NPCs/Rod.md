# Rod

[[Ogre]]'s **adoptive father**, glimpsed only in a memory/vision during Ogre's apotheosis. (Stub — appears solely in [[PP_19_041926_Hi_Im_Ogre|PP 19]] as a remembered figure.)

## Campaign Role
The human (presumed halfling) anchor of [[Ogre]]'s mortal life — the warm, ordinary home the god's severed heart was sent out to live. When Ogre "died" plunging into the spark-ball, the first place he woke was the **forest of his childhood, picking mushrooms with Rod** — the threshold memory through which he then planeswalked to the halfling pantheon and [[Ur-Ogre-lan]].

## Key Events
### [[PP_19_041926_Hi_Im_Ogre|PP 19 — Hi, I'm Ogre]] (04/19/26)
- Appeared in [[Ogre]]'s near-death **memory/vision**: the forest, a basket of mushrooms, his **adoptive father Rod** beside him — the gentle mortal life that grounded Ogre before he reunited with [[Ur-Ogre-lan]].

## Relationships
- [[Ogre]] — His **adoptive son** (the death god's heart, raised as a mortal halfling).
- [[Ur-Ogre-lan]] — The death-god whole-self Ogre is — the life Rod raised was the heart sent out to live.
