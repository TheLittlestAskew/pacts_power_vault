# A Shady Individual

The party's nickname **"Penny"** — full name **Ena "Penny" Virmys** (per the DM #Lore Dump, 06/18/23) — for a Rakdos-aligned contractor first met as "a shady individual." She once held a **demon contract** that [[Rakdos]] later **transferred** — a contract repeatedly contrasted (T06) with [[Liliana Vess|Liliana]]'s far stricter [[The Chain Veil|chain-veil]] pact to [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]].

## Campaign Role
A Rakdos contact and a candidate **Rakdos representative/token** for the [[Niv-Mizzet]] revival ritual. Defends the Cult of Rakdos's interests and the rousing of their sleeping demon-god.

## Key Events

### [[PP_07_100525_The_Reylana_Infiltration|PP 7 — The Reylana Infiltration]] (10/05/25)
- Appeared in [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]]'s concurrent prophetic dream of the **Rakdos Carnarium under siege** by [[Eternals]].
- **Defended a coven of blood witches** ritually trying to wake **[[Rakdos]]** (his giant caged heart) while Rin's Wall of Fire incinerated 100+ Eternals.
- The effort **woke Rakdos** — *"The show shall begin."* The eavesdropped [[Liliana Vess|Liliana]]/[[Vraska]] conversation later confirmed the **Rakdos invasion was thwarted** with help from "an Aasimar ghost conjuring a wall of flame" (Rin's dream was real) — making Penny a confirmed Rakdos asset.

### [[PP_17_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 17 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26)
- **At the grand council** of all ten guilds (surfaced [[Maze's End]]), Penny appears in the contingent the session note groups with **[[House Dimir|Dimir]]** ([[Hikara]], Penny, [[Benji]]) as the guilds channel their forces onto the ritual die to unite against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]. (⚠ Note: Penny is the party's **[[Cult of Rakdos|Rakdos]]** contact elsewhere in the vault; her grouping with the Dimir reps this session may be a raw-transcript attribution quirk — flagged, not resolved.)

### [[PP_18_041926_Hi_Im_Ogre|PP 18 — Hi, I'm Ogre]] (04/19/26)
- **Summoned for the Citadel climb — [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]]'s pick.** When [[Niv-Mizzet]] grants the party a pick of personal allies to help them ascend [[Bolas's Citadel]], **Rin chooses Penny** — explicitly **to close her [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]-tied arc** (Penny's old [[Rakdos]] contract is the weaker counterpart to the Bolas/[[The Chain Veil|Chain Veil]] pacts the party now faces at the summit). Penny is **en route / supporting** the ascent as the session ends mid-assault.

### [[PP_20_053126_I_Miss_You_Buddy|PP 20 — I Miss You, Buddy]] (05/31/26)
- ⭐ **Rides the demon-god onto Bolas.** In the finale **[[Rakdos]] appears and drops Penny onto [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]'s shoulder** to **hack at his scales** — both Rakdos and Penny chip the dragon's armor as part of the collective takedown, before Rakdos leaves "his mark" and departs. Penny's Bolas-tied arc closes alongside the whole campaign's.
