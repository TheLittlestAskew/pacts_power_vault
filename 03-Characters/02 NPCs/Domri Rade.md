# Domri Rade

A planeswalker and the **new leader of the [[Gruul Clans]]**, having displaced [[Borborygmos]]. Originally a Bolas-aligned contender whose faction the party once helped remove; by T07 he holds Gruul leadership but is opposed by the old guard.

## Campaign Role
The disputed face of Gruul leadership during the war. Reported overthrown of [[Borborygmos]] (per [[Ral Zarek]], T01); confirmed in power in T07 but with shaky support — "promising but opposed by the old guard." His standing matters for the [[Niv-Mizzet]] revival ritual, since the party needs a Gruul representative.

## Key Events

### [[PP_07_100525_The_Reylana_Infiltration|PP 7 — The Reylana Infiltration]] (10/05/25)
- Appeared in [[Ogre]]'s concurrent prophetic dream of the Gruul red wastes ([[The Red Wastes]]), where he had smashed the deposed [[Borborygmos]]'s "cup."
- **Ogre punched him in the crotch** (Eldritch Blast 22 to hit; 9 uppercut) to restore Borborygmos's confidence and rally support against him.
- The eavesdropped [[Liliana Vess|Liliana]]/[[Vraska]] conversation later confirmed the dream was **real**: Borborygmos has roused, the Gruul front is losing momentum for Bolas, and **Domri is opposed by the old guard**.
