## Backstory

Watched over as a lost child by **Charmalaine** (halfling god of luck), who imbued him with luck. Befriended orcs who were keeping him captive as a gladiator; faced every challenge with a smile. When an actual ogre attacked the camp, he aided the chief and was given the name "Ogre," along with the greataxe he still uses. Over years he taught the orcs kindness over raiding. Later met **Urogalan** (halfling god of death), and **Dawn** — a sentient divine sword whose soul bonded to his.

He speaks, reads, and writes only his own unintelligible language; his in-world journal is written in it (readable via Comprehend Languages). He misunderstands most events in some way.

- **Allies:** Charmalaine (adoptive mother figure), Urogalan (adoptive father figure), Dawn (sentient sword)
- **Organizations:** Yondalla's Children (Halfling Pantheon)
