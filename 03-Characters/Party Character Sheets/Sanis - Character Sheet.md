## Backstory

A young drow from a family that barely acknowledges his existence — parents who threw money at him instead of attention. His father is an advisor to the Lord presiding over their district; his mother makes elegant gowns for the district's ladies. He grew up feeling unwanted and learned to manipulate his parents. On his 16th birthday his father gifted him the **Reylana family longbow**, the one moment his father truly saw him; afterward his father returned to ignoring him, and Sanis practiced relentlessly with the bow. He later chose to attend **Strixhaven University**, where instructors recognized his quiet intelligence. After refusing to answer in class and earning detention, he fell in with a group of fellow outcasts. *(Sheet text cuts off here.)*
