## Appearance

Orphie is a tiefling with red skin and a tail. She has horns that wrap back around her ears like a ram. Her eyes are normally brown, but when she rages, her eyes and the tribal tattoos on her arms, back, and face light up in the color of hot lava. She is medium height with long, black dreadlocks. She usually wears a grey hoodie.

## Personality

Fiercely loyal to [[The Breakfast Club]]. Sarcastic, blunt, brave. Solves problems with fists, not finesse. Sensitive about her past; tough exterior hides deep emotional scars. Quick to anger, quicker to defend a friend. Doesn't care about expectations; lives life on her own terms. Has a tough, sarcastic exterior but secretly craves connection.

**Ideals:** Loyalty (friends are family; betray them and you answer to her fists) and Freedom (no one gets to tell her who to be).

**Bonds:** Her friends in [[The Breakfast Club]] are everything. She still keeps an old stapler, dented and battered, as a trophy from defending [[Clarissa]] against bullies.

**Flaws:** Quick to violence even when diplomacy might work.

## Backstory

Orphie was born to two powerful magic-wielding tieflings, celebrated in their circles for their arcane prowess. Her parents had grand dreams for their only child, visions of Orphie mastering spellbooks and carrying on the family legacy of magic. Instead, Orphie learned to punch.

From the time she could walk, it was clear she was different. Where her parents whispered spells, Orphie threw fists. Where they crafted delicate rituals, Orphie climbed trees, broke things, and solved disputes on the playground with a solid right hook. Her parents' disappointment was cold and sharp. They weren't angry because she fought; they were angry because she didn't fight *their* way.

At her middle school graduation, after a rare moment of personal pride, Orphie saw her father leave early without a word. She waited for him to come back. He never did.

School wasn't much better until [[Clarissa]]. When bullies cornered Clarissa one day after school, Orphie didn't hesitate. She grabbed the heaviest thing within reach, a thick metal stapler, and beat the bullies bloody. From that day on, Orphie learned a truth she never forgot: the world didn't care about your intentions; it respected results.

High school brought [[The Breakfast Club]]: a ragtag group of misfits who didn't mind that she couldn't cast a spell to save her life. Together, they bought a traveling tavern that rolled from town to town, gathering stories, gold, and trouble. In the tavern, Orphie found her calling: the bouncer.

## Ancestry & Lineage

Orphie's ancestor is [[Athena]], who was also a planeswalker. During the campaign, Athena appears in history as Orphie discovers herself. Athena's adventures spanned [[Ixalan]], [[Tarkir]], [[Dominaria]], and other planes. [[Blazebringer]] was originally Athena's weapon.

In Session 18 (Early Sessions), Orphie encountered a spell-woven room in the tunnels beneath [[New Prahv]] and met one of her ancestors, a tiefling barbarian who previously visited [[Ravnica]]. Her Sword of Fire and Ice hummed and melded with a greataxe on a pedestal; Orphie chose Fire, and the weapon became [[Blazebringer]].

In Session 7 (Transcript 07), Orphie met [[Athena]] in a dream sequence. Athena appeared in Orphie's apartment (shifted to an older style), poured her a pint from a kegerator, and shared stories of her own adventures. Athena warned Orphie about [[Phyrexians]] and their oil, mentioned that [[Blazebringer]] contains Amenkheti metal from [[Amonkhet]], and expressed pride in Orphie's path.
