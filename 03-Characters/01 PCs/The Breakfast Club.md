# The Breakfast Club

*Ravnica's Most Dysfunctional Family Unit*

## Overview

The Breakfast Club is a found family of adventurers who met in high school, became lifelong friends after defeating a [[Hag]] in the restricted section of the [[Tin Street High School Library]], and now operate a traveling tavern pulled by a mechanical Izzet horse named [[Scrappy]].

The party is defined by deep emotional bonds, high chaos energy, and a firm belief in doing the right thing, but not always the right way. They function like a real family, with love, bickering, grief, loyalty, and way too much violence for a breakfast-themed team.

## Ogre's Traveling Tavern

The party's mobile headquarters: a portable Airstream-style bar, kitchen, and living quarters pulled by [[Scrappy]], a mechanical Izzet horse. Used to fund travels and gather intel. Constantly upgraded with loot and weird acquisitions (Jungle Juice, Drawer of Holding, sausages...).

**Tavern Roles:**
- [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] — Cook
- [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]] — Bouncer / Security
- [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]] — Finances
- [[Ogre]] — Namesake / Chaos

## Living Arrangements

[[Orphie]], [[Rin]], and [[Clarissa]] share an apartment in the [[Boros Legion]] district.
- Orphie's side: grunge apartment, comfy cottage core
- Rin's side: bookshelves everywhere

## Shared Trauma

All have lost or been betrayed by family or institutions. The shattering of the [[Guildpact]] was a breaking point: [[Jace]]'s death, [[Salena]]'s death, the [planeswalker] trap. Everyone is grappling with guilt, rage, or both.

## Group Dynamics

- **Orphie & Ogre:** Emotional heart of the party; balance rage and weirdness
- **Rin & Sanis:** Internalized grief; healing vs. vengeance
- **Varis & Lance:** Strategists and escape artists; magic vs. mischief
- **Everyone vs. Ral Zarek:** Unanimous hostility
