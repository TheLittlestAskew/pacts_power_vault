## Personality

Giddy, genuine, childlike curiosity meets eldritch trauma. Ogre is the chaos engine of [[The Breakfast Club]], driven by impulse and a surprising emotional depth beneath the antics. He has a deep bond with his companions and an extraordinary talent for accidentally solving problems.
