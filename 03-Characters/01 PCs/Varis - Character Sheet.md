## Backstory

Left as a newborn on the doorstep of a church belonging to an elf family — no record of where he came from or why. Raised by that elf family and given the elven name **Varis**. His adoptive father, **Molander Aestra**, is a priest; his adoptive mother, **Emmara Aestra**, is a Life Domain cleric of Mishakal. He became a draconic bloodline sorcerer through his ancestral ties to **Ugin**.

## Lore & Connections (from sheet notes)

- Draconic ancestor: **Ugin**, who gave Varis the last of his essence before being killed by **Nicol Bolas** ("the Second Son").
- **Nicol Bolas** — brother of Ugin · **Merrevia Sal** — sister of Ugin.
- Dragonborn ancestor **Uni**, known to [[Ogre]], pictured in a tapestry.
