## Personality

- **Personality traits:** Tolerant of other faiths and respectful of the worship of other gods. Has spent so long in the temple and hidden from bullies that she has little practical experience dealing with the outside world. Very encouraging of others.
- **Ideals:** Charity. I always try to help those in need, no matter what the personal cost. (Good)
- **Bonds:** To uphold the teachings of Dol Arrah and protect the helpless from evil and darkness.
- **Flaws:** Very quiet, reserved, not outgoing; extremely insecure about living up to her deity's expectations.
