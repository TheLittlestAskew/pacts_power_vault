🥞 The Breakfast Club – Ravnica’s Most Dysfunctional Family Unit
________________________________________
🍳 Party Overview:
The Breakfast Club is a found family of adventurers who met in high school detention, became lifelong friends after defeating a hag in the restricted section of the library, and now operate a traveling tavern pulled by a mechanical Izzet horse named Scrappy.
The party is defined by:
•	Deep emotional bonds
•	High chaos energy
•	A firm belief in doing the right thing, but not always the right way
They function like a real family—with love, bickering, grief, loyalty, and way too much violence for a breakfast-themed team.
