Pacts & Power · Session 01

We went down into the dark on purpose this time. No chains, no one dragging us — just five of us and whatever the city kept hidden under its own skull. I have learned not to trust quiet, and the Ghost Mind was the loudest quiet I ever stood inside.

They wanted to weigh us. Urns and choirs and a staircase made of lies you could fall through. I am not built for riddles — I am built for the thing that comes after the riddle — so I let the clever ones talk and I kept the rest of us breathing.
