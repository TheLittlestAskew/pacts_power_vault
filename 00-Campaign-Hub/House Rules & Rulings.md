# House Rules & Rulings

DM rulings and table conventions, logged by session. These are Chris's in-the-moment rulings (indisputable per source authority), recorded for continuity — not general D&D rules.

---

## Transcript 01 (07/13/25) — [[PP_01_071325_Into_the_Ghost_Mind|Into the Ghost Mind]]

| Ruling | Detail |
|---|---|
| Magical darkness vs. light | The [[Ghost Mind]]'s magical darkness nullified all light sources, including magical light. Torches and ordinary light did nothing. ("The darkness doesn't care about your torch.") |
| Devil's Sight / Warlock sight | [[Ogre]] could see normally through the magical darkness due to his warlock sight; the rest of the party was effectively blind. |
| Dispel Magic ceiling | [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]]'s Dispel Magic (rolled 18) failed against the darkness — Chris ruled the effect was "higher than 3rd level," so a 3rd-level Dispel could not end it. |
| Puzzle-failure damage | Wrong answers on the Glass Staircase of Truth and failed urn Constitution saves dealt psychic damage (5 on a pass, 10 on a fail). Brute-forcing the ghost choir also dealt 10 psychic damage. No initiative; non-combat puzzle damage. |

## Transcript 02 (07/27/25) — [[PP_02_072725_I_Cant_Rebuild_You|I Can't Rebuild You]]

| Ruling | Detail |
|---|---|
| Ever-smoking bottle in motion | Chris ruled that when the bottle is moved, the cloud dissipates where it was and follows as a trailing smoke cloud (rather than a static sphere), as long as the holder keeps the bottle. Heavily obscured — the party inside was effectively blind beyond the cloud. |
| Sight-based healing while blind | Inside the smoke, [[Rin]]'s sight-dependent healing spells (Healing Word, Mass Cure Wounds) could not target allies she couldn't see. [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]]'s touch-range Cure Wounds still worked ("you can only heal what you see… not what you touch" — i.e., touch spells are the exception). |
| Enlarge on Steve underground | [[Ogre]]'s enlarge mushroom doubled [[Steve]] while he was still partly in his tunnel; Chris acknowledged possible underground infrastructure damage but did not mechanize it. |
| Dragon-form skill expertise | [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] does **not** retain his Arcana expertise while transformed into a dragon (unlike Druid Wild Shape, which retains the caster's stats). Out of dragon form he rolls Arcana at +3 (Int), not +12. |
| Enlarge sizing | Chris ruled the enlarged dragon's space at 30×30 ft (gargantuan, doubled from huge) for the body-slam. |
| Cinematic mass combat | The Part 2 battle was run cinematically without tracked initiative or per-Eternal HP — single rolls resolved sweeping outcomes (e.g., breath weapon "incinerates 14," nat-20 body slam "flattens 30"). |
| Oketra's arrow | God-Eternal [[Oketra]]'s hit dealt 78 piercing (Dex save for half); she "never misses." Targeting shifted to whoever drew her attention ([[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]]'s shot → the Tavern). |

## Transcript 03 (08/10/25) — [[PP_03_081025_Thats_Why_No_One_Will_Remember_Your_Name|That's Why No One Will Remember Your Name]]

| Ruling | Detail |
|---|---|
| Loose / informal initiative | The mass battle continued in "loose / informal initiative" — turns cycled by group (ground trio → front line → Chandra → Oketra → end-of-round cinematics) rather than by rolled order. |
| Enlarge persists through dragon-form drop | Chris ruled [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] would remain enlarged even if he dropped out of dragon form; reiterated that Enlarge grants no extra durability (and lowers Dex/AC, making him an easier target). |
| Universal grapple | Any creature may attempt a grapple as an action via an Athletics check even if it isn't on the stat block — dragon-[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] grappled [[Oketra]] (Athletics 25 vs DC 21). |
| Grappled → Restrained upgrade | With dragon-Varis pinning the shoulders **and** [[Steve]] coiling the legs, Chris upgraded [[Oketra]] from grappled to **restrained** (disadvantage on Dex saves). |
| Prone-adjacent roll cancellation | Attacking a prone target within 5 ft grants advantage, but being within 5 ft also imposes disadvantage on a ranged attack — Chris ruled the two cancel to a straight roll ([[Rin]]'s Scorching Ray). |
| Shatter vs. worn armor | Inanimate objects normally auto-fail saves, but armor being worn gets a save (the wearer's Con/DC); Chris rolled for [[Oketra]]'s armor against [[Ogre]]'s Shatter. |
| Armor sabotage effects | [[Ogre]] shrinking into a crevice of Oketra's armor lowered its AC by 2 (Con save DC 16 to endure the pressure); [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] popping off the shoulder pauldron made the god easier to hit. |
| Twin Spell eligibility | Chris confirmed Fireball can't be Twin-Spelled (the spell must be incapable of targeting more than one creature) when [[Chandra Nalaar\|Chandra]] tried it. |
| Revivify limits (dragons) | Revivify could work on a dragon in principle, but **not** on [[Niv-Mizzet]] — disintegrated to bone, no intact body to restore. |
| Nat-20-gated recall | The identity of "[[Onyx]]" (tied to an alternate "Strixane" timeline) was gated behind a nat-20 recall check; the party missed it, so the information stayed hidden. |

## Transcript 04 (08/26/25) — [[PP_04_082625_The_Spark_and_the_Swarm|The Spark and the Swarm]]

| Ruling | Detail |
|---|---|
| Ever-smoking bottle duration | Chris confirmed the endless smoke bottle's cloud is **infinite "until you cork it."** Re-corking it (Rin still held the cork) immediately cleared the smoke from the wreck. |
| Help action, narrated | Chris had both the searcher ([[Ogre]]) and the helper ([[Chandra Nalaar\|Chandra]]) describe in-fiction how they searched/helped before granting the Help bonus (advantage) on the investigation checks. |
| Immovable rod retrieval | Recovering the rod "parking brake" from the wreck was a flat check vs **DC 18**; [[Ogre]] hit 17 (with help) and **failed** — Chris ruled the immovable rod stays exactly where it is. |
| Breath weapon, mass save | [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]'s Force Breath (60-ft cone, 18d6 = 65 force) used **one Dex save for the whole Eternal group** (Chris rolled a 7); ruled to **evaporate 18 [[Eternals]]** at once. Varis angled it from the air to avoid allies. |
| Spirit-absorption ritual | Pulling [[Niv-Mizzet]]'s essence into the Dragon Spirit Box was a **multi-check ritual**: [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] Arcana (22) to initiate, then [[Ogre]] (tethered to the box) Wisdom (9, connect-the-dots vision) and Strength (14) to pull the tether. |
| Dragon-form stats exception | Continuing the T02/T03 ruling: out of/around dragon form Varis lacks his Arcana expertise, but for the absorption Chris made an **exception** and let him roll with his normal [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] stat block and proficiencies. |
| Eternal "armor" (lore ruling) | On inspection, an [[Eternals\|Eternal]]'s plating is **metal painted directly onto the skin**, not removable armor — still has thickness/material (not naked), but cannot be looted as armor. |
