## Transcript 01 (07/13/25) — [[PP_01_071325_Into_the_Ghost_Mind|Into the Ghost Mind]]

| Ruling | Detail |
|---|---|
| Magical darkness vs. light | The [[Ghost Mind]]'s magical darkness nullified all light sources, including magical light. Torches and ordinary light did nothing. ("The darkness doesn't care about your torch.") |
| Devil's Sight / Warlock sight | [[Ogre]] could see normally through the magical darkness due to his warlock sight; the rest of the party was effectively blind. |
| Dispel Magic ceiling | [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]]'s Dispel Magic (rolled 18) failed against the darkness — Chris ruled the effect was "higher than 3rd level," so a 3rd-level Dispel could not end it. |
| Puzzle-failure damage | Wrong answers on the Glass Staircase of Truth and failed urn Constitution saves dealt psychic damage (5 on a pass, 10 on a fail). Brute-forcing the ghost choir also dealt 10 psychic damage. No initiative; non-combat puzzle damage. |
