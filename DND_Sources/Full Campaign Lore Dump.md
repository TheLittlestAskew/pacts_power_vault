## 🏛️ Guilds & Factions

### The Ten Guilds of Ravnica

- **Selesnya** (Nature / Community) — Emmara Tandris is High Priestess
- **Golgari** (Death / Cycle) — Vraska is Guild Leader; Myra Deathbloom is NPC
- **Izzet** (Invention / Magic) — Ral Zarek is Chief Engineer; Dalya Bara is Researcher; Niv Mizzet is the Dragon
- **Gruul** (Wild / Chaos) — Domri Rade was Guildleader; Borborygmos leads the Borborygmos Clan
- **Azorius** (Law / Order) — Isperia is Supreme Judge/Guildmaster; Lavinia is a key NPC
- **Rakdos** (Chaos / Performance) — Exava is Bloodwitch; Hekara is Harlequin
- **Orzhov** (Wealth / Religion) — Obzedat is referenced; Cassir opposes them
- **Dimir** — (referenced in campaign)
- **Boros** — Aurelia is associated
- **Simic** — (referenced in campaign)

### Other Factions
- **Cassir's Crusaders** — Anti-Obzedat rebel group
  - Poster Text: *"No Taxation Without Representation! Out with the Obzedat! Join Cassir's Crusaders today!"*
- **Family vod Savo** — Referenced on a Golgari Elf's robe pin; members include Myra Deathbloom and Saskia vod Savo
- **Mardu** (former) — Led by Dragonlord Kolaghan; referenced in statue passwords

## 🪄 Magic Items

### Mizzium Cloaker *(Dec 15, 2024)*
**Wondrous Item, rare**

- Must roll above a 5 to use; success roll increases by 5 per use; resets to 5 when repaired by an Izzet artificer.
- **Effect:** Invisible for 1 hour. Damage taken, attacks, or spells cast will break invisibility. Certain ambient energy sources may cause malfunction.
- **Failure:** On failed roll, user takes 2d10 force damage and is blinded for 1 minute.
- *Notes: Set: Buff, Jewelry*

### Grixis's Lament — Ogre *(Feb 23, 2025)*
**Armor (light), Weight: 1**

Arcing inscribed runes trace erratic paths across bracers, crackling with residual energy. Surface ripples between deep crimson and electric blue. Metal pulses with black glow in dim light.

- **Passive:** +2 bonus to AC if wearing no armor and using no shield.
- **Tip the Scales (Bonus Action):** Wearer can redistribute weapon bonus between AC, Attack/Damage, and Magic bonus (+3 currently) for 60 seconds.
- **Last Grasp:** ??? Ogre will find out.

### Infernal Embrace — Orphie *(Feb 23, 2025)*
**Armor (light)**  
AC: 13 + Dex Modifier | Stealth: Disadvantage | Weight: 8

A form-fitting light armor adorned with Hazoretium, radiating oppressive heat. When the wearer rages, the gold veins ignite and run like liquid fire.

- **Shake it Off:** While raging and under half maximum HP, at the start of the wearer's turn, gain 1d12 + half barbarian level (rounded up) as temporary hitpoints. Effect refreshes only when previous temp HP from this effect is exhausted.

---

### Favor of the Fervent — Rin *(Feb 23, 2025)*
**Armor (medium)**  
AC: 15 + Dex modifier (max 2) | Stealth: Disadvantage | Weight: 18

Scarlet and gold armor with pauldrons shaped like stylized wings of flame. When the wearer leaps into the air, the armor shimmers with holy energy.

- **Zealous Devotion:** While airborne after healing another target, the wearer may cast Sacred Flame as a free action.
