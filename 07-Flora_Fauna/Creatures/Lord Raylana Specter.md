# Lord Raylana Specter

The **reaped soul of [[Lord Reylana|Lord Modest Raylana]]**, bound by [[Ogre]] into a **guardian Specter** and commanded to **protect [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]** — the very son the man tortured. A dark-justice asset: the abuser's spirit enslaved to guard his victim.

## Description / Nature
- Manifests as a **younger form** of Lord Raylana's ghost.
- Created via [[Ogre]]'s **Accursed Specter** soul-reaping: when Ogre landed the killing blow on Lord Raylana, he drew the soul **through the Witch-blade into the [[Leather Daddy Game Boy]]**, then **expelled it back out as a bound Specter** under his command.
- **Standing order:** *"Protect your son from your wife."* (i.e., shield Sanis from [[Lady Reylana|Lady Hyena]]).

## Campaign Role
A new bound combat asset for [[Ogre]] (alongside [[Witch]] and the [[Leather Daddy Game Boy|LDGB]]'s accumulated spirits) and a thematically loaded one — the father's soul now serves the family he abused. Escaped the collapsing lab alongside Ogre, who **surfed out on Lord Raylana's corpse** with the Specter at his side.
