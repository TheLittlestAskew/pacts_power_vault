# Myconids

The fungal humanoid colony native to the [[Bone Mine]], led by **[[Thal]]**. They communicate via spore-telepathy and tend the **marrow / essence pits** where the concentrated soul-essence of ancient dead is distilled.

## Origin
The Myconids **arose when a chemical from the Bone Mine's plant life mixed with the mushrooms in the soil** — the same were-zombie "essence" that births beings like [[Salena|Selena]]. They are bound to the [[Bone Mine]]'s essence pits.

## Description / Appearance
- Normal Myconids: fungal humanoids; one larger leader ([[Thal]]) among several lesser colony-mates. (One has bark-like and human-skeleton features visible on close inspection.)
- **Corrupted Myconids (T05):** huge, **black-and-purple** with **bulbous recesses and dripping black tendrils**, surrounded by a **necrotic miasma aura** (Con save vs necrotic to be near/move through). Created when [[Tezzeret]]'s [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]-corruption pumped into the essence pits.

## Behavior
- The normal colony is **neutral/allied** — they sheltered and tried to heal [[Salena|Selena]].
- Corrupted Myconids are **hostile**, mindless guardians of the corrupted pits.

## Key Events

### [[PP_05_090725_Do_You_Smell_That_Its_Breakfast_Time|Transcript 05 — Do You Smell That? It's Breakfast Time]] (09/07/2025)
- The colony was attacked by [[Eternals]]; **[[Salena|Selena]] defended them** (hundreds of Eternals slain), but ~20–30 Myconids died.
- [[Thal]] used "the essence" to save the injured Selena (inadvertently passing on Bolas's corruption).
- **Three corrupted giant Myconids** guarded [[Tezzeret]] at the marrow pits and fought the party (necrotic miasma; tough but felled by Orphie, Varis's bite/claws, Ogre's Witch, Rin's Fireball, and Isiah).
- After the pits were purged and collapsed, the **entire colony was left unconscious**, with an ethereal white-eyed figure (Selena, ascended) over [[Thal]].

### [[PP_06_091725_Soul_Integrated_Soul_Storage_Get_It_Right|Transcript 06 — Soul Integrated Soul Storage, Get It Right]] (09/17/2025)
- **The colony is revived** by the ascended [[Salena\|Selena]] ([[Aphonexa]]) — including members dead before the party arrived. They rise and **kneel**; [[Thal]] (Thal Oram) proclaims her *"goddess of rebirth."*
- The Myconids become **[[Aphonexa]]'s worshippers**, with her as their patron deity bound to the [[Bone Mine]].
