# Zombie-Simic mutants

The **Eternal-plated monstrosities** manufactured at the [[Second Sun]]'s underwater [[Simic Combine|Simic]] lab ([[Second Sun Labs]]) for [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]: **half-finished hybrid people** — octopus-, crab-, and shark-armed/mouthed Simic biomancy creations — patched with **undead flesh** (the [[Eternals|Eternal]]-plating the S07 intel described).

## Description / Nature
- Living Simic hybrids (crab-armed, shark-mouthed, octopus-limbed) **stitched with dead flesh** — "dead flesh on the living."
- Held in **specimen tubes** in the facility's vivarium and inner office, in stages from half-finished to fully-formed.
- Several are **sapient and aware** — a captive **crab-humanoid** pounds the glass mouthing *"help me,"* and the captive merfolk **[[Vuthala]]** (known from [[Ixalan]]) is among the fully-formed specimens.

## Campaign Role
The confirmed product of the Bolas/[[Second Sun]] monstrosity factory; a captive-rescue thread for the party. Their manufacture parallels [[Salena|Selena]]'s were-zombie weaponization (Reylana-blooded), the lab's "perfect weapon" program.

## Key Events

### [[PP_08_101925_Blood_Runs_Thicker_Than_Water|PP 8 — Blood Runs Thicker Than Water]] (10/19/25)
- Encountered in the vivarium (half-finished hybrids) and the inner office (fully-formed), confirming the lab as Bolas's monstrosity factory.
- A captive **crab-humanoid** begged for help through the glass; the merfolk **[[Vuthala]]** was recognized among the captives — establishing the rescue thread.
