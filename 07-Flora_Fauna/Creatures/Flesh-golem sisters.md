# Flesh-golem sisters

A line of **flesh-golem girls** — "soul sisters" — created in the necromantic lab beneath the [[Reylana mansion|Reylana Estate]]. Each looks like [[Salena|Selena]] (and like [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]), and their **limbs detach and regrow**. They appear to be Selena's / Sanis's blood relations, their "father" being the doctor the party previously killed; they have **no mother**.

## Description / Nature
- Resemble [[Salena|Selena]] / [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]; roughly Selena's age.
- **A limb comes off in your hand and regrows** (the diagnostic tell of the line; [[Brula Galadora]]'s arm fell off and regrew when freed).
- Their transformation/powers are **necromantically suppressed** by an arcane shell (Arcana 17); [[Ogre]]'s Dispel Magic (L3, 15) failed to break it.
- One of a **whole line of stolen sisters** — ~5 were seen being marched around the war camp.

## Campaign Role
A rescue / clone-lab thread tied to Sanis's desecrated childhood home. **[[Brula Galadora]]** is the first freed (sent to stealth out to the [[Selesnya Conclave]]); the others remain captive in the Golgari war camp.

## Key Events

### [[PP_07_100525_The_Reylana_Infiltration|PP 7 — The Reylana Infiltration]] (10/05/25)
- The party spotted **~5 girls Selena's age** being marched around the [[Reylana mansion|Reylana Estate]] (now a Golgari war camp).
- In the cell corridor (a former ritual hall), they found and **freed [[Brula Galadora]]** — beaten, powers suppressed; [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] kicked the door in (Athletics 22). Her arm fell off and regrew, revealing the flesh-golem nature ([[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]/[[Rin]] Insight 21).
- A **necromantic arcane shell** blocked her transforming; [[Ogre]]'s Dispel Magic **failed**. She was sent to stealth out to the [[Selesnya Conclave]].
