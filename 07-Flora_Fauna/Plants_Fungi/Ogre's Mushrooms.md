# Ogre's Mushrooms (Embiggening & Shrinking)

A set of magical mushrooms [[Ogre]] carries and consumes (or feeds to allies) to change size.

## Properties
- **Embiggening ("enlarge") mushroom:** Functions as the **Enlarge** spell — doubles the target's size for ~1 hour, with no way to end the effect early. Used on [[Steve]] (doubling the rotworm) and on [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] in dragon form.
- **Shrinking ("reduce") mushroom:** Shrinks the consumer (Ogre went to ~6–8 inches). Used in [[PP_03_081025_Thats_Why_No_One_Will_Remember_Your_Name|Transcript 03]] to **infiltrate [[God-Eternal Oketra|Oketra]]'s armor** and sabotage the god from inside.

## DM Rulings
- The mushrooms function as the Enlarge/Reduce spell; enlarge grants **no extra durability** (and lowers Dex/AC). Enlarging [[Steve]] underground may cause infrastructure damage (not mechanized).
- Squeezing into Oketra's armor crevice required a Constitution save (DC 16) against the pressure.

## Supply
~1 enlarge mushroom remaining after Transcript 02; at least one shrinking mushroom used in Transcript 03.
