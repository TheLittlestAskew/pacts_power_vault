# Purple Bone Mushrooms

Purple mushrooms growing out of a **bone**, given to [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] by [[Brula]] in [[PP_03_081025_Thats_Why_No_One_Will_Remember_Your_Name|Transcript 03]] as a token from his sister [[Salena|Selena]].

## Description
A bone sprouting purple mushrooms. Associated with the **[[Bone Mine]]** and its Myconids.

## Properties / Magic
- [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]] (Arcana) deduced the magic on the mushroom-bone **resembles the magic seen at [[Strixhaven]]** — related to, but **not**, the [[Elder Spell]]. It "piqued her curiosity."
- Significance not yet established; likely a hook tied to Selena's situation at the [[Bone Mine]].
