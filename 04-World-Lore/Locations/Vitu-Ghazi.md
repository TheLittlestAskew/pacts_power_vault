# Vitu-Ghazi

The **Great Tree** at the heart of the [[Selesnya Conclave]] — a colossal living tree that serves as the Conclave's seat and, during the war, as the party's safe zone and **field hospital**.

## Description
A massive tree with interior rooms (including private care rooms). During the siege the open ground before it is staged with **white intake tents** for wounded soldiers and patients, run by the Selesnya under [[Nissa]], Myra, and Saskia.

## Notable Events
- **Transcript 03 (08/10/25):** The party flew the rescued [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]] here for care. [[Nissa]] intook Chandra in a private room inside the tree; Chandra revealed her [[Strixhaven]] backstory and the [[Elder Spell]]. [[Brula]] arrived here to deliver the mushroom-bone and news of [[Salena|Selena]] at the [[Bone Mine]].
- **Transcript 06 (09/17/25):** Closing stinger — [[Liliana Vess]] dimension-doors here with an unconscious [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]] and [[Isiah Ja-Faank]], deposits them at the tree, and tasks Isiah with caring for Chandra (and taking [[Nissa]] to the [[Simic Combine]]).
- **[[PP_13_021326_Hold_the_Wall|PP 13 — Hold the Wall]] (02/13/26):** **The Great Tree awakens.** As Ravnica's [[Leylines]] unravel, a **Leyline surge pulses across the [[Selesnya Conclave|Conclave]] and strikes the Great Tree** — which **uproots itself and stands up** (cliffhanger; intent unknown). See the active-entity page **[[The Great Tree of Vitu-Ghazi]]**. [[Ogre]]: *"Glad I made friends with that tree."*
- **[[PP_14_022226_Reforging_the_Guildpact|PP 14 — Reforging the Guildpact]] (02/22/26):** **Rampaging (off-screen).** The awakened Great Tree is now **fully animate and stomping refugee tents at the [[Selesnya Conclave|Conclave]]** — Boros Angels attack it while Selesnya priests try to protect their holy tree. The party only glimpsed this from the air before flying off to the [[First Church of Orzhov]]; the rampage continues unresolved off-screen. See **[[The Great Tree of Vitu-Ghazi]]**.

- **[[PP_16_040226_The_Guildpact_Reborn|PP 16 — The Guildpact Reborn]] (04/02/26):** **Ridden / being tamed.** From the surface central plaza (post-teleport), the party sees the awakened Great Tree **still thrashing but now ridden by a small female figure** with a **harness/reins** — someone is bringing the rampaging Tree under control. The rider is **unconfirmed** (the transcript's "Nessa"); the most likely candidate is **[[Nissa]]** (the Conclave's nature-aligned planeswalker — "Tree Lady"), but identity is **not confirmed in-session** (flag for next session). The S13 "awakened Great Tree" mystery shifts from *threat* toward *tamed asset.* See **[[The Great Tree of Vitu-Ghazi]]**.

- **[[PP_17_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 17 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26):** **Tamed asset — [[Nissa]] rides it to the council.** The S16 "Nessa rider" pays off: the **animate Great Tree** now serves the alliance, **ridden by [[Nissa]]**, who brings it to the **grand council of all ten guilds** at the surfaced [[Maze's End]] as part of the **[[Selesnya Conclave|Selesnya]]** contingent ([[Emmara Tandris|Amara Tandris]] + Nissa). The awakened Tree is firmly a **coalition asset**, no longer a rampaging threat. See **[[The Great Tree of Vitu-Ghazi]]**.

- **[[PP_20_053126_I_Miss_You_Buddy|PP 20 — I Miss You, Buddy]] (05/31/26):** **Marches to the victory.** In the finale, as [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] falls at the [[Bolas's Citadel|Citadel]], the Great Tree **marches up to the battlefield** with [[Nissa]] (steering it), **Myra Deathbloom**, and **Saskia Vostavo** — the Selesnya arriving for the close of the war. The awakened-tree arc resolves as a triumphant coalition asset at the campaign's end.
