# Tin Street

A 10th-District marketplace street and the party's **old neighborhood** — they attended **Tin Street / 10th Street High** and ran errands for the local goblin mob boss **[[Krenko]]** five years ago (the [[Sword of Feast and Famine]] job). It is now dominated by **[[Krenko Incorporated]]**, Krenko's tower over a hidden underground city.

> Spelling/garble flagged in-source ("Tit Street"/"10th Street"); canonical page form **Tin Street**. Distinct from the existing [[Tin Street High School library]] page (a specific room from the party's school days).

## Description

A bustling 10th-District market street with deep significance to the party's backstory. The leering goblin-faced tower of [[Krenko Incorporated]] now rises over it (20–25 stories) atop a sprawling underground metropolis. The party long-rested on a rooftop here while casing Krenko's HQ.

## Key Events

### [[PP_10_121425_You_Cant_Just_Buy_the_Sun|PP 10 — You Can't Just Buy the Sun]] (12/14/25)
- The party **chased [[Krenko]]'s goblin-faced blimp** here after it stole the [[Immortal Sun]] — landing back in their old neighborhood.
- Established as the site of **[[Krenko Incorporated]]** (Krenko's tower + underground city) and the **[[Immortal Sun]] auction**; the party long-rested on a Tin Street rooftop to case the HQ.

### [[PP_11_011126_The_Masquerade_Auction|PP 11 — The Masquerade Auction]] (01/11/26)
- The **Tin Street sewers** are revealed as the **lair of [[Ava]]**, a copper dragon (in child form) hoarding gold; the party descended to recruit her for the [[Immortal Sun]] heist.
- The **masquerade auction** at [[Krenko Incorporated]] (ballroom → amphitheater) played out here; the party won "Ogre's Tavern 2" for 41,000 gp as the Sun heist began.
