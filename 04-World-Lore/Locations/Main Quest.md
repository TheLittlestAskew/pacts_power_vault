**As [[The Breakfast Club]] prepares for their quest, they have been given a glimpse of the adventure to come:** _In the infinite space of the multiverse, Power is king. Power dictates the flow of magic and as a tool, allows the bending of fate itself. With enough Power, even time and the threads of reality are but clay, to be formed in the hands of the beholder. Luckily, in most cases, this Power is confined to the very essence of planes themselves. On the plane of [[Planes of The Multiverse/Arcavios]], this Power manifests itself in the form of [[Five Founding Dragons]], children of the snarl. [[Strixhaven]] students studying magic channel the magic of these [[dragons]], and consequently, the very heart of the plane. On the plane of [[Ravnica]], this Power manifests itself in intricate leylines sprawled across the globe, lending to its apt name, the [[Implicit Maze]]. Those who would use Power for good would strike a chord with the first ten [[Guildleaders]] of Ravnica. As founders of the [[Guildpact]], the [[Guildleaders]] forged a Pact that bound the plane to the principles of peace and prosperity. Now, while some may view peace and prosperity as “good”, there are those that view these ideals as “weak” and “defective”. These ideals, while perfectly acceptable to possess, are dangerous in the wrong hands, but especially in the hands of #planeswalkers. Namely, a very ancient and especially wicked planeswalker. What happens when a [[planeswalker]] collects Power on a planar scale? What happens when a planeswalker collects Power on an interplanar scale? Such tales have not been told since the Times of Legends, a time when all planeswalkers beheld such power. This time was a time of great peace, prosperity, and technological innovation. Yet, as is common with sentient life and possession of Overwhelming Power, this Time culminated in the Dark Age and utter loss. Now, almost ten thousand years later, we may once again see the pages of time tell the same story._
