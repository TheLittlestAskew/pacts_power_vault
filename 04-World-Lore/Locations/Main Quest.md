**As [[The Breakfast Club]] prepares for their quest, they have been given a glimpse of the adventure to come:** _In the infinite space of the multiverse, Power is king. Power dictates the flow of magic and as a tool, allows the bending of fate itself. With enough Power, even time and the threads of reality are but clay, to be formed in the hands of the beholder. Luckily, in most cases, this Power is confined to the very essence of planes themselves. On the plane of [[Planes of The Multiverse/Arcavios]], this Power manifests itself in the form of [[Five Founding Dragons]], children of the snarl. [[Strixhaven]] students studying magic channel the magic of these [[dragons]], and consequently, the very heart of the plane. On the plane of [[Ravnica]], this Power manifests itself in intricate leylines sprawled across the globe, lending to its apt name, the [[Implicit Maze]]. Those who would use Power for good would strike a chord with the first ten [[Guildleaders]] of Ravnica. As founders of the [[Guildpact]], the [[Guildleaders]] forged a Pact that bound the plane to the principles of peace and prosperity. Now, while some may view peace and prosperity as “good”, there are those that view these ideals as “weak” and “defective”. These ideals, while perfectly acceptable to possess, are dangerous in the wrong hands, but especially in the hands of #planeswalkers. Namely, a very ancient and especially wicked planeswalker. What happens when a [[planeswalker]] collects Power on a planar scale? What happens when a planeswalker collects Power on an interplanar scale? Such tales have not been told since the Times of Legends, a time when all planeswalkers beheld such power. This time was a time of great peace, prosperity, and technological innovation. Yet, as is common with sentient life and possession of Overwhelming Power, this Time culminated in the Dark Age and utter loss. Now, almost ten thousand years later, we may once again see the pages of time tell the same story._

## Campaign Updates

### [[PP_13_012526_Fucking_Barbarians|PP 13 — Fucking Barbarians]] (01/25/26) — Planeswalking Restored
The central "trapped on Ravnica" premise is **broken.** [[Narset]] (with [[Ravnica Trading Incorporated]]) took the **[[Immortal Sun]] off-plane**, lifting the lockdown that had bound everyone to Ravnica since [[Ral Zarek\|Ral]]'s [[Interplanar Beacon]]. **Planeswalking is restored** — hundreds of trapped planeswalkers stream off the plane in beams of light. The same instant, **[[Nicol Bolas\|Bolas]] went overtly genocidal**, razing swaths of the [[Azorius Senate\|Azorius]] District from his [[Bolas's Citadel\|Citadel]]. The endgame is now a direct city-wide war. The party also secured the **shreds of the First [[Guildpact]]** (a converging tool, with the [[Niv-Mizzet]] box and the reopened [[Orzhov Syndicate\|Orzhov]] bank, toward reforging the Guildpact at the [[Implicit Maze]] Nexus).

### [[PP_14_021326_Hold_the_Wall|PP 14 — Hold the Wall]] (02/13/26) — The Leylines Are Failing
The stakes jump to **plane survival.** Defending the [[Boros Legion\|Boros]]/[[Izzet League\|Izzet]] front line against [[Nicol Bolas\|Bolas]]'s Eternal horde and God-Eternals ([[God-Eternal Bontu\|Bontu]], [[God-Eternal Kefnet\|Kefnet]], [[God-Eternal Rhonas\|Rhonas]]), the party discovers Ravnica's **[[Leylines]] are catastrophically unraveling** — frayed by [[Jace\|Jace]]'s death, **tapped by [[Bolas's Citadel\|Bolas's Citadel]]**, and stressed by cumulative war — threatening to **shatter the plane.** A raw **Leyline crater** opens in the [[Azorius Senate\|Azorius]] District. The party **confirms reforging the [[Guildpact]] would help stabilize the Leylines**, so the Main Quest is now **doubly urgent** (it saves the world *and* wins the war). Meanwhile, the **payoff of removing the [[Immortal Sun]]** is military: off-plane **dragons ([[Narset]]) and [[Ixalan]] armies ([[Kwatli Pakal\|Jareth]])** now reinforce Ravnica — both fronts **held.** **Cliffhanger:** a Leyline surge **awakens the Great Tree of [[Vitu-Ghazi]]** ([[The Great Tree of Vitu-Ghazi]]), which uproots itself and stands up (intent unknown).

### [[PP_15_022226_Reforging_the_Guildpact|PP 15 — Reforging the Guildpact]] (02/22/26) — The Central Quest Pays Off
The campaign's central quest **fires live.** The party secured the **10th guild token** ([[Benji]]'s [[House Dimir|Dimir]] pin, taken by force), descended through the recalibrated [[First Church of Orzhov]] portal into the [[Implicit Maze]], and cleared the **five color-trials** (Black/Hatred, Red/Haste, White/Order, Blue/Wits, Green/Endurance) across the Leyline moat. At **[[Maze's End]]** they **reforged the [[Guildpact]]** — [[Ogre]] held up the **[[Black Lotus]]** and **wished a new pact into being** (the long-mysterious condition was being "powerful enough"; petals disintegrated), while the **[[Firemind Vessel]]** opened a ceiling portal and **[[Niv-Mizzet]] began reviving as a full dragon.** But [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]] sensed the **black [[Leylines|Leyline]] is corrupted by [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]'s miasma** (rooted in [[Bolas's Citadel]]) — adding a **new objective: destroy the Citadel** as the true cure. From the corruption + the hatreds the party shed in the Black trial, the **[[Augur of Bolas|"Ogre of Bolas"]]** boss coalesced and attacked while Niv is still mid-emergence (**cliffhanger**; saves deferred to S16). **The Guildpact reforge and the 10-token gather are complete; Niv's revival and the boss fight are in progress.**
