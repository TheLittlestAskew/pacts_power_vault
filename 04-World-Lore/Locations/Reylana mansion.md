# Reylana mansion

- Located in [[Wayport]]

[[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]'s childhood home — the **Reylana Estate** — now desecrated into a **[[Golgari Swarm|Golgari]] war camp** with the family name still on the gate. Beneath it lies the necromantic lab that produces the **[[Flesh-golem sisters]]** ("soul sisters" of [[Salena|Selena]] and Sanis).

## Layout (Transcript 07)
- **Grounds / war camp:** Devkarin/[[Lotleth]] army quartered here awaiting orders; ~5 flesh-golem girls (Selena's age) marched around the yard.
- **Basement:** Sanis's boyhood hiding route gives covert access.
- **Hidden door:** opened by Sanis's **Reylana family necklace**; inscribed *"That which is dead may never die, but those who seek life shall live eternal."*
- **Cell corridor:** a former ritual hall now lined with **jail cells** (held [[Brula|Brula Galadora]] and a benefactor "picnic-basket" cell with a [[Potion of Command Undead]]).
- **Ritual room:** now empty; contains a **vent** the party used to eavesdrop.
- **Lord Reylana's study:** holds the Golgari **battle plans**; where the party hid before the cliffhanger.

## Key Events

### [[PP_07_100525_The_Reylana_Infiltration|PP 7 — The Reylana Infiltration]] (10/05/25)
- [[Aphonexa]] sent [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] a prophetic vision of the estate **surrounded by a Devkarin/[[Lotleth]] army**; the party infiltrated via Pass Without Trace (avg stealth ~26) through the civilian-empty [[Golgari Swarm|Golgari]] district.
- **[[Vraska]]** ran the offensive from here; **[[Liliana Vess|Liliana]]** arrived by portal and the two went inside.
- The party entered the **basement** via Sanis's boyhood route; his **Reylana necklace** unlocked a hidden door (inscription above) into a corridor of **jail cells**.
- One cell held a benefactor gift (the "Time Lord" picnic basket + note *"thought y'all might need this"* + a **Potion of Command Undead** to [[Ogre]]). Another held **[[Brula|Brula Galadora]]** — freed, revealed as a **[[Flesh-golem sisters|flesh-golem sister]]**, sent to the [[Selesnya Conclave]].
- Through a **vent** in the empty ritual room, the party **eavesdropped on Liliana & Vraska** (Liliana winked — she knew): Rakdos invasion thwarted, [[Borborygmos]] roused, [[Simic Combine|Simic]] loyal with the [[Immortal Sun]], [[Tezzeret]] dead, [[Kaya Cassir|Kaya]] dealt with, and the **Golgari plan to flank the Alliance by marching through the hollowed-out [[Orzhov Syndicate|Orzhov]] district.**
- In **Lord Reylana's study**, the party salvaged the **Golgari march route through Orzhov** (memorizing the full plans failed, Int nat-1). Sanis recovered the buried memory that the **[[Yarod Votsavo|Golgari Lich]]** implanted [[Argo Lot]] into him and pacted him to **kill Vraska.**
- Vraska got word of the basement, called the guards; [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] failed to slip past (Acrobatics 8) and was **stabbed** (Con save 9 — made a sound) → **combat triggered, initiative rolled, deferred to S08 (cliffhanger).**

### [[PP_08_101925_Blood_Runs_Thicker_Than_Water|PP 8 — Blood Runs Thicker Than Water]] (10/19/25)
- ⚠ **Off-transcript transit:** the S07 study-guard cliffhanger here is **not depicted or resolved**, and the bridge from the estate to the underwater [[Second Sun Labs]] is not shown — S08 opens mid-tour inside the Simic facility.
- The estate's overseers **[[Lord Reylana]] & [[Lady Reylana]]** appear in person at the [[Second Sun Labs]], linking the [[Golgari Swarm|Golgari]] estate to the Simic monstrosity factory. Lord Reylana confessed he **murdered [[Salena|Selena]] by design** to forge a Reylana-blooded were-zombie weapon — recontextualizing the estate's clone-lab ([[Flesh-golem sisters]]) as part of the same program.

### [[PP_09_113025_What_Are_You_Waiting_For_Sanis|PP 09 — What Are You Waiting For, Sanis?]] (11/30/25)
- **Featured in [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]'s childhood flashback** (~years prior, at the estate): a **five-year-old Sanis** was handed a too-large bow by **[[Lord Reylana|Lord Modest]]** and ordered to **execute a blindfolded, hooded captive** — a **beloved family servant** (the man who cooked for and fed young Sanis), coerced by [[House Dimir|Dimir]] (who held the servant's family hostage) into leaking Reylana secrets. Sanis killed him to win his father's approval; Lord Raylana disintegrated the body and praised him, and **[[Lady Reylana|Lady Hyena]] slipped Sanis a piece of chocolate** afterward. The origin scene of Sanis's core trauma, set at the family home.
