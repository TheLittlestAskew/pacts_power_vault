# Maze's End

The heart of the [[Implicit Maze]] — the **Nexus** where Ravnica's [[Leylines]] converge and where the original [[Guildpact]] was forged. Reached after clearing the five color-trials, it is the **site of the Guildpact's reforging and [[Niv-Mizzet]]'s revival** (PP 15).

## Campaign Role
The campaign's destination, defined back in T07 ([[Ugin]]) as the place to bring a token of power from each of the 10 guilds to revive [[Niv-Mizzet]] and remake the [[Guildpact]]. In PP 15 the party finally reached it and performed the ritual.

## Description
A stone ring containing **ten guild seats** around a central **pedestal**. The ceiling above shows the **five Leyline colors swirling** (the WUBRG confluence), with a **barren circle** above the pedestal that needs a *vessel* to complete. During the ritual the swirling colors **filter into 10 distinct pairs** and beam into each guild seat; a **portal opens in the ceiling** at the Leyline confluence.

## Key Events

### [[PP_15_022226_Reforging_the_Guildpact|PP 15 — Reforging the Guildpact]] (02/22/26)
- **Reached** after the party crossed the [[Implicit Maze]]'s Leyline moat and cleared all five color-trials.
- **The reforge ritual.** The party laid on the pedestal the **tattered [[Guildpact]] (9 seals)** with [[Benji]]'s **Dimir pin** pinned through it (the 10th token), plus **[[Jace Beleren|Jace]]'s old speech/list paper** ([[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] had carried it since Session 1). [[Ogre]] held up the **[[Black Lotus]]** and **wished for a new pact** — this time the petals **unfurled and disintegrated** and the wish took hold (he was finally "powerful enough").
- The **[[Firemind Vessel]]** ([[Niv-Mizzet]]'s soul, carried by [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]) **rose, opened, and formed a portal** in the ceiling at the Leyline confluence; the colors filtered into 10 pairs and beamed into the guild seats.
- **[[Ogre]] jumped through the ceiling portal** and met [[Niv-Mizzet]] in darkness, then **pulled them back** — Niv began emerging as a **full dragon** (a massive claw descending through the widening portal).
- **The corruption & the boss.** [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]] sensed the **black [[Leylines|Leyline]] is corrupted** by [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]'s miasma (rooted in [[Bolas's Citadel]]). From the corrupted Leyline + the party's shed hatreds, the **[[Augur of Bolas|"Ogre of Bolas"]]** coalesced in the entryway and attacked while Niv was still mid-emergence. **Cliffhanger** (saves deferred to S16).

### [[PP_16_031326_A_Scared_Child|PP 16 — A Scared Child]] (03/13/26)
- **The whole session is this chamber.** The party fights the **[[Augur of Bolas|"Ogre of Bolas"]]** here while [[Ogre]] keeps pulling [[Niv-Mizzet]] through the ceiling portal (the dragon's claw, then arm, slowly emerging). The smog-cloaked boss raises **shadow-echoes** from each PC's shadow and **drains max HP** (see [[Augur of Bolas]]).
- **New environmental hazard — falling stalactites.** When [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]]'s **Channel Divinity** radiant AoE dispels the boss's smog, the Augur **shrieks**, and the shriek **cracks the chamber's stalactites** loose — a **ceiling collapse** looms next round, on top of the still-active Leyline confluence overhead.
- The chamber ends the session as a live battlefield: **boss hurt but standing, Niv mid-revival, stalactites about to fall.** (Outcomes belong to the next session — not imported here.)

### [[PP_17_040226_The_Guildpact_Reborn|PP 17 — The Guildpact Reborn]] (04/02/26)
- **The climax plays out here.** The [[Augur of Bolas]] is **destroyed** in this chamber (Niv's all-five-color claw), and **[[Niv-Mizzet]] is reborn as the [[Living Guildpact]]** — Large, white-aura'd, decagon-crested — after [[Ogre]]'s shrinking mushroom freed the stuck dragon from the ceiling portal.
- **The Augur's shriek** yanked the chamber's **stalactites** down (Dex save DC 18, 4d4 piercing) — the S16 collapse hazard finally fired, but the party weathered it ([[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]]'s Mass Cure Wounds).
- **The Leyline grab.** Reborn Niv gathered all ten [[Leylines]] to recharge; the party grabbed on too (Con saves) — [[Ogre]], [[Varis Aestra|Varis]], [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]], [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] benefited (restored slots/rage); **[[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] failed** (3 exhaustion + lost top slots).
- **Exit.** Niv **glyphed the ritual die/platform** and **teleported the party** (through the Blind Eternities) to the **surface central plaza.** The party leaves Maze's End — quest complete.

### [[PP_18_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 18 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26)
- **The surfaced Maze's End hosts the grand council.** The ten-guild **ritual die/seats** are now at the **surface central plaza** (the spot where [[Jace Beleren|Jace]] gave his Session-1 speech — full circle). **[[Niv-Mizzet]]** (gargantuan again) places a claw on the central die and the ten guild seats channel a **prismatic guild-beacon**; every standing guild's representatives converge and **put their forces' energies on the die to formally unite against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]** — **[[Benji]] gestures [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]** to the **Dimir** die (he carries Lazav within). **[[Teferi]]** arrives here with the **[[Zhalfir army]].**
- Niv's directive from this seat: confront [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] **directly at his [[Bolas's Citadel|Citadel]]** — the party departs to find a tunnel route in (the [[The Drillinator|Drillinator]] / freed Rotworms).

### [[PP_19_041926_Hi_Im_Ogre|PP 19 — Hi, I'm Ogre]] (04/19/26)
- **The regroup point before the final climb.** A comedy of planeswalking lands the party back at Maze's End to **[[Niv-Mizzet]]** — only [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] succeeds first (nat 20); the others follow his etheric trail (with advantage). Here Niv warns this is *"a fight for the future of this plane,"* grants the party a pick of **personal allies** ([[A Shady Individual|Penny]], [[Molly]], [[Nissa]]) for the ascent, and relays Dimir intel (Bolas's forces dwindling, God-Eternals pulled back, [[Liliana Vess|Liliana]] atop the Citadel). From here the party heads **up the Rotworm tunnel to [[Bolas's Citadel]]** for the final assault.
