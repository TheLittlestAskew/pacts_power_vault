# Fortress of Nivix

The headquarters of the [[Izzet League]] on [[Ravnica]] — a towering structure topped by Izzet machinery. Its summit houses the [[Interplanar Beacon]].

## Campaign Role

The Fortress is [[Ral Zarek]]'s base of operations. From its top, Ral had been attempting daily to activate the [[Interplanar Beacon]], thwarted until now by the [[Ghost Mind]]'s interference.

## Key Events

### [[PP_01_071325_Into_the_Ghost_Mind|Transcript 01 — Into the Ghost Mind]] (07/13/2025)
- The party was involuntarily planeswalked from [[House Dimir|Dimir]] territory to the top of the Fortress immediately after the Ghost Mind collapsed.
- [[Ral Zarek]] was present and successfully activated the [[Interplanar Beacon]] now that the Ghost Mind's interference was gone.
- Ral briefed the party on the [[Immortal Sun]] lockdown, the integrity of the [[Guildpact]] ley lines, and guild alignment (Golgari, Simic, and Gruul unaligned; [[Borborygmos]] reportedly overthrown). He also revealed his marriage to [[Tomik]] and his planeswalker-detecting glasses.
- Ral departed on a lightning disc to gather allies; the party headed downstairs to find Dahlia.
