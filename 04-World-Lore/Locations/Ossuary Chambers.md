# Ossuary Chambers

A bone-vault location in the **[[Golgari Swarm|Golgari]]** undercity, posted to the DM's **#Lore Dump** (01/12/25) alongside the **[[Marrow Pits]]**. An ossuary is a chamber for the dead — fitting the Golgari death-and-decay domain.

## Campaign Role
Part of the subterranean **[[Bone Mine]] / [[Marrow Pits]]** complex of bone-and-essence chambers where the Golgari (and the Myconid colony under **[[Aphonexa]]**) cycle the dead. Likely one of the chambers tied to the were-zombie "essence pits" the party encountered around the Bone Mine (T05–T06).
