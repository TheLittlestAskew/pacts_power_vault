# Bolas's Citadel

A massive **black pyramid** that rose out of the [[Golgari Swarm|Golgari]] district during the siege of the [[Tenth District]] — the seat from which [[Nicol Bolas]] commands his invasion.

## Description
A towering black pyramid. A circular aperture opens at its apex, through which [[Nicol Bolas]] first emerged onto [[Ravnica]]. From across the district, harvested **planeswalker sparks** (small orbs of light) can be seen rising and flowing into the citadel, where Bolas absorbs them.

## Notable Events
- **Transcript 03 (08/10/25):** Rose in the Golgari district; [[Nicol Bolas]] emerged and proclaimed his "judgment" over Ravnica. Atop the citadel, Bolas dueled and **killed [[Niv-Mizzet]]**, then settled into a meditative pose to "aura farm," absorbing sparks harvested via the [[Elder Spell]] + [[Spark-Harvester Trident]]. [[Steve]] rushed off toward the citadel at session's end.
- **[[PP_12_012526_Fucking_Barbarians|PP 12 — Fucking Barbarians]] (01/25/26):** When **planeswalking was restored** (the [[Immortal Sun]] taken off-plane by [[Narset]]), [[Nicol Bolas]] (dragon form) **climbed his Citadel** in the Golgari District, screamed "NO!", and **fired a beam of black energy across the [[Azorius Senate\|Azorius]] District** — disintegrating swaths of it and **cutting the Azorius HQ in half.** The Citadel is the source of his open assault on the city.
- **[[PP_13_021326_Hold_the_Wall|PP 13 — Hold the Wall]] (02/13/26):** **Eternal spawn-engine + Leyline drain.** **Millions of [[Eternals]]** pour from **portal-gates at the Citadel**, fed to the [[Boros Legion\|Boros]]/[[Izzet League\|Izzet]] and [[Azorius Senate\|Azorius]] fronts; from here [[Nicol Bolas\|Bolas]] **rakes a beam of black energy across the field** (vaporizing thousands of his own Eternals). Crucially, the Citadel is **tapping Ravnica's [[Leylines]]** — a major driver of their catastrophic unraveling that threatens to shatter the plane.
- **[[PP_14_022226_Reforging_the_Guildpact|PP 14 — Reforging the Guildpact]] (02/22/26):** **Confirmed root of the Leyline corruption — a new objective.** Deep in the [[Implicit Maze]], [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]] senses the **black [[Leylines\|Leyline]] is corrupted by [[Nicol Bolas\|Bolas]]'s miasma**, traced to the **Citadel rising from the [[Golgari Swarm\|Golgari]] Swarm and tapping the Leylines.** Even a reforged [[Guildpact]] may not fully heal the lines until the **Citadel is destroyed** — the true long-term cure (likely the finale objective). The corruption also birthed the **[[Augur of Bolas|"Ogre of Bolas"]]** boss.

- **[[PP_16_040226_The_Guildpact_Reborn|PP 16 — The Guildpact Reborn]] (04/02/26):** **Seen from the surface plaza — the tide turning.** After [[Niv-Mizzet]]'s rebirth, the party teleports up to the central plaza and sees the **Citadel risen at the [[Golgari Swarm|Golgari]]**, still **spitting out the dead** ([[Eternals]]) — but now the **Golgari finally bite back**: **overgrown buildings and hulking beasts pour out to reap the Eternals**, turning the surface battle toward the allies. [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] feels his **[[Yarod Votsavo|Golgari-Lich]] pact coming to fruition** as he watches the Golgari rise. The Citadel itself **still stands** — destroying it (the root of the Leyline corruption) remains the standing long-term objective (see [[Campaign Dashboard]] Quest #22), even with the [[Guildpact]] reforged.

- **[[PP_17_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 17 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26):** **A path in is opened.** [[Niv-Mizzet]]'s council directive is to confront [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] *directly* at the Citadel — but the army can't punch through in time, so the party builds [[Dalya Bara|Dahlia]]'s **[[The Drillinator|Drillinator]]** and frees the ancient Rotworms at the **[[The Rot Warren|Rot Warren]]**. With the Rotworms freed, **[[Steve]] + [[Yarod Votsavo]] can now tunnel a straight path to the Citadel** — setting up the final assault. The Citadel still stands; the session ends poised to drill in.

- **[[PP_18_041926_Hi_Im_Ogre|PP 18 — Hi, I'm Ogre]] (04/19/26):** **Breached — the party reaches the summit.** The Citadel is detailed here as a **400-ft tiered black pyramid** with **8–10-ft steps**, [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] on his throne at the apex beneath a blinding-white **ball of harvested sparks** he is **siphoning** (it outlines/empowers him, making him **invulnerable** while he feeds — [[Varis Aestra|Varis]]'s nat-20 Scorching Ray dissipated). **[[Liliana Vess|Liliana]]** stands beside him with the **[[The Chain Veil|Chain Veil]]**, marionetting the **3 remaining God-Eternals** ([[God-Eternal Bontu|Bontu]]/[[God-Eternal Rhonas|Rhonas]]/+1). The coalition holds a **beachhead** at the tunnel mouth ([[Niv-Mizzet]]'s distraction, Golgari Rotworms, Izzet mech-suits, [[Myconids|myconid]] spore-clouds, Rakdos strafing, [[Dalya Bara|Dahlia]] + myconids) while the party **Pass-Without-a-Trace climbs**, throws Eternals off the steps, and rides **[[Ugin]] ("Eugene")** up. Atop it, **[[Ogre]] plunged into the spark-ball → his apotheosis** (reunion with [[Ur-Ogre-lan]]). **Summit breached, but the Citadel/Bolas stand** — the session ends mid-assault; the kill is the **finale (05/31)**. *(Do not import a finale outcome here.)*

- **[[PP_20_053126_I_Miss_You_Buddy|PP 20 — I Miss You, Buddy]] (05/31/26): ⭐ THE FINALE — Bolas falls at the summit.** The campaign's last battle plays out atop the Citadel: [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] (Dragon-God, near-untouchable while siphoning) is worn down by the whole coalition — [[Liliana Vess|Liliana]]'s flipped God-Eternals draining him, **[[Ogre]] (now [[Ur-Ogre-lan]]) pushing the spark-orb away to choke the siphon**, [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]]'s **[[HellSmasher]]**, dragon-[[Varis Aestra|Varis]], [[Teferi]], [[Rakdos]]/[[A Shady Individual|Penny]], and [[Molly]]. **[[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]'s Hail of Thorns + Ava's Longbow breaks the spark-tether**; the orb destabilizes and **[[Aphonexa]] sacrifices herself** to release it safely. Bolas's spark is harvested by the God-Eternals and **[[Ugin]] seals his soul into the [[Dragonhedron]]**, self-exiling as warden. The party **climbs down into a city full of cheering and lanterns** — Bolas defeated, the Citadel's reign over. The epilogue moves to **[[Ogre's Traveling Tavern|Ogre's Traveling Tavern]]** in the Rakdos district. ✅ **Bolas defeated & contained. Multiverse saved. No PC deaths.**
