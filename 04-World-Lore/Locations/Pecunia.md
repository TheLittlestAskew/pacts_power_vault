# Pecunia

**Ravnica's second moon** and the seat of the **Orzhov Grand Vault** — the central treasury of the **[[Orzhov Syndicate]]**, reached through a portal in the basement of the **[[First Church of Orzhov]]**.

> Spelling unconfirmed; canonical page form **Pecunia**.

## Description

The Orzhov's off-world strongroom, accessed by recalibrating the First Church basement portal's coordinates. As of PP 11 the vault is **nearly drained** — a single remaining pile of roughly **100,000 gp**, down from a far larger hoard, the rest **consumed by [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]'s [[Eternals]]** when [[Kaya Cassir|Kaya]] unsealed the vaults. An Orzhov insignia on the vault floor serves as the focus for the bank-reinstatement ritual.

## Key Events

### [[PP_11_011126_The_Masquerade_Auction|PP 11 — The Masquerade Auction]] (01/11/26)
- **[[Varis Aestra|Varis]]** recalibrated the [[First Church of Orzhov]] basement portal to Pecunia's coordinates and found the Grand Vault **nearly empty** (~100k gp, the rest eaten by the [[Eternals]]).
- Standing on the Orzhov insignia, Varis recited the ledger-ritual (*"By ledger written in bone and gold…"*) and **re-knit the leyline lattice, reopening the First Bank of [[Orzhov Syndicate|Orzhov]]** — he can now **conjure gold and issue loans at will**, snapping coins from the vault into his hand.
- The vault's reserve funded the **41,000 gp** winning bid on **"Ogre's Tavern 2"** at the auction (leaving ~59k — not enough to outbid for the [[Immortal Sun]], forcing Plan B).
