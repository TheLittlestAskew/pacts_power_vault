# Implicit Maze

The plane-wide network of **ley lines** sprawled across [[Ravnica]] — the manifestation of Power on this plane, lending Ravnica its name. The ley lines converge at a central **Nexus** at the **center of the maze**, the site where the [[Guildpact]] was forged and the destination for the [[Niv-Mizzet]] revival ritual.

## Campaign Role
The **endgame location.** Per [[Ugin]] (T07; and earlier via [[Liliana Vess|Liliana]]'s relayed vision in T06), the party must bring a representative or token of power from each of the **10 guilds** to the Nexus to revive [[Niv-Mizzet]] and re-establish the [[Guildpact]]. "Sis" (formerly [[Pippi]]) was once a **portal to the Nexus** and can guide the party there; the route runs through the **First Church of [[Orzhov Syndicate|Orzhov]]**, whose portal "Sis" can recalibrate to the maze's entrance.

## Key Events

### [[PP_07_100525_The_Reylana_Infiltration|PP 7 — The Reylana Infiltration]] (10/05/25)
- [[Ugin]] defined the **Nexus = the center of the implicit maze** as where Ravnica's ley lines converge and where the [[Guildpact]] was forged.
- Confirmed the revival ritual: bring **a token/representative from each of the 10 guilds** to the Nexus to revive [[Niv-Mizzet]] and remake the Guildpact.
- Route step established: reach the **First Church of [[Orzhov Syndicate|Orzhov]]** so "Sis" can **recalibrate its portal to the maze's entrance**.
