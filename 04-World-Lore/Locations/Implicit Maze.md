# Implicit Maze

The plane-wide network of **ley lines** sprawled across [[Ravnica]] — the manifestation of Power on this plane, lending Ravnica its name. The ley lines converge at a central **Nexus** at the **center of the maze**, the site where the [[Guildpact]] was forged and the destination for the [[Niv-Mizzet]] revival ritual.

## Campaign Role
The **endgame location.** Per [[Ugin]] (T07; and earlier via [[Liliana Vess|Liliana]]'s relayed vision in T06), the party must bring a representative or token of power from each of the **10 guilds** to the Nexus to revive [[Niv-Mizzet]] and re-establish the [[Guildpact]]. "Sis" (formerly [[Pippi]]) was once a **portal to the Nexus** and can guide the party there; the route runs through the **First Church of [[Orzhov Syndicate|Orzhov]]**, whose portal "Sis" can recalibrate to the maze's entrance.

## Internal Structure (PP 15)
Reached via the basement portal at the [[First Church of Orzhov]] (recalibrated by "Sis"/[[Pippi]]), the maze is a vast underground cavern holding a **golden labyrinth** streaked gray/purple. Its structure, from outer to inner:
- **The Leyline moat** — a ring of **raw [[Leylines|Leyline]] energy** around the labyrinth; anything that touches it **disintegrates** ([[Ogre]]'s test Mage Hand dissolved on contact).
- **The five color-trials** (the five primordial colors / guild mana — the WUBRG "Wuborg"):
  1. **Black — Hatred.** A ledger-stone: each PC must name what they despise most to raise a stepping-stone (5 total). Crossing **lifts the hatred from each of them** (it seeps into the Leyline — feeding the [[Augur of Bolas|"Ogre of Bolas"]] boss).
  2. **Red — Haste.** A chasm with three timed stone-buttons summoning disappearing platforms (Dex checks); [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] jammed hers so it stuck, locking the platforms.
  3. **White — Order.** Word-buttons; press only those that *"bind without breaking"* (Consent, Balance, Harmony, Structure — rejecting Control, Obedience, Dominion, Law).
  4. **Blue — Wits.** A riddle (*"From sea to stone, from stone to gold…"*); the true answer is **magic** (what runs through the land via the Leylines). Orphie's clever **"letter E"** was a bonus answer granting **+5 temp HP**.
  5. **Green — Endurance.** A sealed room with a **descending crushing ceiling** the party must hold up (Con saves vs **exhaustion** — Orphie and [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]] each took **2 levels**).
- **[[Maze's End]]** — the central Nexus: a stone ring of **10 guild seats** around a **pedestal**, the site of the Guildpact reforging and [[Niv-Mizzet]]'s revival.

## Key Events

### [[PP_07_100525_The_Reylana_Infiltration|PP 7 — The Reylana Infiltration]] (10/05/25)
- [[Ugin]] defined the **Nexus = the center of the implicit maze** as where Ravnica's ley lines converge and where the [[Guildpact]] was forged.
- Confirmed the revival ritual: bring **a token/representative from each of the 10 guilds** to the Nexus to revive [[Niv-Mizzet]] and remake the Guildpact.
- Route step established: reach the **First Church of [[Orzhov Syndicate|Orzhov]]** so "Sis" can **recalibrate its portal to the maze's entrance**.

### [[PP_15_022226_Reforging_the_Guildpact|PP 15 — Reforging the Guildpact]] (02/22/26)
- **The party finally descended into the maze.** [[Pippi|Sis]] recalibrated the [[First Church of Orzhov]] basement portal (from [[Pecunia]] to the maze); through it they reached the underground cavern, the **golden labyrinth** ringed by the **Leyline moat** (Ogre's test Mage Hand disintegrated on contact).
- **Cleared all five color-trials** — Black/Hatred, Red/Haste, White/Order, Blue/Wits, Green/Endurance (see Internal Structure above). The Black trial **lifted each PC's core hatred into the Leyline**, and an amorphous form began to rise from the moat behind them.
- At **[[Maze's End]]** they **reforged the [[Guildpact]]** ([[Black Lotus]] wish + the [[Firemind Vessel]] + the 10 tokens) and began **reviving [[Niv-Mizzet]]** — then the corrupted black Leyline + the shed hatreds birthed the **[[Augur of Bolas|"Ogre of Bolas"]]** boss (cliffhanger).

### [[PP_16_031326_A_Scared_Child|PP 16 — A Scared Child]] (03/13/26)
- **The boss fight continues at [[Maze's End]].** The entire session takes place in this single chamber — the **[[Augur of Bolas|"Ogre of Bolas"]]** (the hatred-manifestation born of the Black/Hatred trial's shed hatreds + the corrupted black Leyline) battles the party while [[Ogre]] pulls [[Niv-Mizzet]] through the ceiling portal.
- **New hazard — falling stalactites.** When the Augur's smog is dispelled (by [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]]'s radiant AoE) it **shrieks**, and the shriek **shakes the chamber's stalactites** — threatening a **ceiling collapse** the following round. The Maze's End chamber is now an active environmental hazard.
- The Hatred-trial mechanic pays off as designed: the hatreds each PC **shed** to cross the Black trial are now **weaponized back at them** as shadow-echoes (see [[Augur of Bolas]]). Encounter **unresolved** at the session's cut.

### [[PP_17_040226_The_Guildpact_Reborn|PP 17 — The Guildpact Reborn]] (04/02/26)
- **The maze arc closes.** The [[Augur of Bolas]] is **destroyed** at [[Maze's End]] and **[[Niv-Mizzet]] is reborn as the [[Living Guildpact]]** (decagon crest, white aura). After recharging via the ten [[Leylines]], reborn Niv **glyphs the ritual platform and teleports the party** (briefly through the Blind Eternities) **up to the surface central plaza** — the end of the party's time inside the Implicit Maze for this arc. The Nexus has served its purpose: the Guildpact is reforged and its embodiment restored.

> With the Guildpact reborn, the campaign's board **resets from the maze to the surface war** — [[Bolas's Citadel]], the [[Eternals]], and the contested guilds at [[Vitu-Ghazi]] / the [[Golgari Swarm]].
