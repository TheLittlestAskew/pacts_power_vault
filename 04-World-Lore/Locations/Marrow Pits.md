# Marrow Pits

The **marrow pits** of the **[[Bone Mine]]** — Golgari essence/were-zombie pits posted to the DM's **#Lore Dump** (01/12/25) with the **[[Ossuary Chambers]]**.

## Campaign Role
These are the **essence pits** at the heart of the [[Bone Mine]] where the dead are reworked into were-zombies and where **[[Salena]]/[[Aphonexa]]** submerged to purge [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]'s corruption (T05) — *"the marrow pits collapsed"* as she descended. The detailed marrow-pit lore lives on the **[[Bone Mine]]** page; this entry resolves the standalone link.
