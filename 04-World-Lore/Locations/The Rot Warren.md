# The Rot Warren

The **[[Golgari Swarm|Golgari]] spawning grounds** deep beneath Ravnica — the breeding ground of the ancient **Rotworms** (Steve's kin). By PP 18 it had been **caged by [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]'s forces** inside a **force-field dome**, trapping the ancient Rotworms so they could not be rallied. The party tunnels here aboard the **[[The Drillinator|Drillinator]]** to free them and open a path to [[Bolas's Citadel]].

> **Nickname (raw transcript):** [[Dalya Bara|Dahlia]] calls it **"the ref farm."** No spell-check pass per Taylor.

## Description
A **bowl-shaped valley** sealed under a **force-field dome** powered by **6 pylons** (each built of **Lazotep + Mizzium**), with **2 [[Eternals]]** guarding each pylon. The dome caged the ancient Rotworms within. Reached via the tunnel the Drillinator bored — straight through the [[Myconids|Myconid]] colony (the misunderstanding that triggered the session's Raise Dead restitution).

## Key Events

### [[PP_18_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 18 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26)
- **Drilled into.** The party rode the **[[The Drillinator|Drillinator]]** (driven by [[Dalya Bara|Dahlia]]) down to the Rot Warren, after boring through the Myconid colony and righting the wrong (Rin's Raise Dead via the Orzhov gauntlet).
- **The force-field assault.** A **force-field dome** powered by **6 pylons** (Lazotep + Mizzium, **2 Eternals each**) caged the Rotworms. [[Ogre]] fumbled the drill controls (stalled it); **[[Teferi]] polymorphed [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] into a T-Rex** (Str 27, Int 2 — "the happiest T-Rex"), who bit and tail-swiped pylons. Combined **Fireballs** (Rin, Varis), **Eldritch Blast** (Ogre — a **nat-20 crit**), **Shatter** (Ogre), **Lightning Arrow** (Sanis, via [[Ava's Longbow of Power]]), the **T-Rex multiattack**, and **Teferi's Fire Bolt** demolished all **6 pylons + their Eternals.**
- **✅ Force field down; Rotworms freed.** **Steve** + **[[Yarod Votsavo]]** surfaced to rally the freed ancient Rotworms — now able to **tunnel a path to [[Bolas's Citadel]]** for the final assault. No PC downed.

### [[PP_19_041926_Hi_Im_Ogre|PP 19 — Hi, I'm Ogre]] (04/19/26)
- **The launch point of the final climb.** From the liberated Rot Warren, the freed ancient Rotworms' tunnel runs up to [[Bolas's Citadel]]. The party staged through here (regrouping via [[Maze's End]]) and then **ascended the tunnel to the foot of the 400-ft pyramid**, where the coalition holds the beachhead and the party makes its push to the summit (→ [[Ogre]]'s apotheosis).
