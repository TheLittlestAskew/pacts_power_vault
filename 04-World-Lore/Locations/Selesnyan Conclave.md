# Selesnyan Conclave

The territory of the [[Selesnya Conclave]] guild — a neutral safe zone and the party's base of operations during the war. Centered on the great tree [[The Great Tree of Vitu-Ghazi|Vitu-Ghazi]]. See the guild page [[Selesnya Conclave]] for the faction itself.

## Description
A green, communal district of clerics, medics, and nature-aligned Selesnyans. During the war it serves as a medical staging ground, with tents and healers prepared for casualties. A safe haven amid the [[Tenth District]] conflict.
