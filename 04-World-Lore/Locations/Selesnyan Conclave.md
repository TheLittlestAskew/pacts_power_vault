# Selesnyan Conclave

The territory of the [[Selesnya Conclave]] guild — a neutral safe zone and the party's base of operations during the war. Centered on the great tree [[The Great Tree of Vitu-Ghazi|Vitu-Ghazi]]. See the guild page [[Selesnya Conclave]] for the faction itself.

## Description
A green, communal district of clerics, medics, and nature-aligned Selesnyans. During the war it serves as a medical staging ground, with tents and healers prepared for casualties. A safe haven amid the [[Tenth District]] conflict.

### [[PP_11_121425_You_Cant_Just_Buy_the_Sun|PP 11 — You Can't Just Buy the Sun]] (12/14/2025)
- Now a **vast refugee camp** — sheltering [[Gruul Clans|Gruul]], [[Orzhov Syndicate|Orzhov]], and [[Simic Combine|Simic]] civilians fleeing the [[The Second Sun|Second Sun]]/Allies war. [[Saskia]] & [[Myra]] help run the [[Vitu-Ghazi]] **intake tents.**
- Hub for the party's auction prep: **[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]'s parents [[Lord Aestra]] & [[Lady Aestra]]** (Orzhov refugees) agree to rally tellers/liquidity to reopen the **[[First Church of Orzhov]]** bank; **[[Emmara Tandris]]** pledges Selesnya funds once banking is live; **[[Saskia]] & [[Myra]]** donate 200 gp.

### [[PP_13_012526_Fucking_Barbarians|PP 13 — Fucking Barbarians]] (01/25/26)
