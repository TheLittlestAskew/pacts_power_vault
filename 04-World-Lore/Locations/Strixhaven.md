# Strixhaven

A magic **school** (on the plane of [[Arcavios]]) entangled with the campaign's spark-harvesting plot. Referenced from the earliest sessions — the party was originally redirected to [[Ravnica]] by "[[dragon paragons]] of Strixhaven" — and detailed by [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]] in [[PP_03_081025_Thats_Why_No_One_Will_Remember_Your_Name|Transcript 03]].

## What the Party Knows (Transcript 03)
- [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]] infiltrated Strixhaven after sensing something wrong. The school's **students were being abducted and turned into undead** for their planeswalker sparks.
- Chandra founded a resistance, the **[[Flameseekers of Truth]]**, was betrayed by **[[Onyx]]**, captured, and imprisoned/tortured for years.
- The **undead sorcerers** among [[Nicol Bolas]]'s [[Eternals]] army on Ravnica were recognized as Strixhaven students.
- A "**Strixane**" alternate timeline is referenced by the DM (tied to the gated [[Onyx]] recall).
