# Ghost Mind

## Description
The Ghost Mind was a massive arcane construct located in [[House Dimir]] territory on [[Ravnica]]. It functioned as a cylindrical column housing centuries of accumulated ghostly intelligence—effectively the Dimir Guild's information network and archive. The construct had become sentient or semi-sentient, taking on [[Szadek]]'s form as an avatar.

## Structure (as navigated in Session 01)
1. **Entrance Staircase:** Descending staircase with magical darkness that nullifies all light sources and resists Dispel Magic
2. **Urn Chamber:** Four urns containing condemned spirits; automated judgment puzzle
3. **Ghost Choir Hall:** Four spectral singers performing Szadek's history
4. **Glass Staircase of Truth:** Ten transparent steps with true/false lore statements
5. **Throne Room:** The Ghost Mind entity seated on a throne in Szadek's form

## Notable Properties
- Generated magical darkness that blocked all light (including magical light)
- Interfered with the [[Interplanar Beacon]], preventing Ral Zarek from activating it
- Contained centuries of Dimir intelligence and ghostly spirits
- Housed a fragment of [[Szadek]] as its avatar/core entity

## Key Events

### [[PP_01_071325_Into_the_Ghost_Mind|Session 01 — Into the Ghost Mind]] (07/13/2025)
- Party navigated all four puzzle chambers
- [[Ogre]] used Witch to exorcise all wandering spirits (absorbed into [[Leather Daddy Ball]])
- [[Sanis]] absorbed the Szadek fragment
- The Ghost Mind collapsed
- Its collapse removed the interference blocking the [[Interplanar Beacon]]

## Current Status
**COLLAPSED** as of Session 01 (07/13/2025). All spirits have been absorbed into the [[Leather Daddy Ball]]. The Dimir Guild's intelligence network is effectively destroyed.
