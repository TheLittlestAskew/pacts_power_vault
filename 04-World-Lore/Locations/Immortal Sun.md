# Immortal Sun

## Description
A powerful artifact that prevents planeswalkers from leaving a plane when active. Common transcription errors: "Immortal Son," "Eternal Sun," "Eternal Son" (transcription often renders "Sun" as "Son").

## Properties
- Prevents all planeswalking off the plane where it is active
- Previously encountered by the party on Ixalan
- Ogre recognizes the feeling of hitting the planeswalking barrier

## Key Events

### [[PP_01_071325_Into_the_Ghost_Mind|Session 01 — Into the Ghost Mind]] (07/13/2025)
- Activated on Ravnica shortly after the [[Interplanar Beacon]] began calling planeswalkers
- All planeswalkers—including the party—are now trapped on Ravnica
- [[Ogre]] recognized the feeling from the party's prior encounter on Ixalan
- Combined with the Interplanar Beacon, this creates a trap: planeswalkers are called to Ravnica and then cannot leave
- Suspected to be connected to [[Nicol Bolas]] / [[The Second Sun]]'s plans

### [[PP_05_090725_Do_You_Smell_That_Its_Breakfast_Time|Transcript 05 — Do You Smell That? It's Breakfast Time]] (09/07/2025)
- The party **recalled that [[Tezzeret]] is the one who took the Immortal Sun** (tied to the crystal-tower events). With Tezzeret now **dead** (killed at the [[Bone Mine]]), the Sun's current whereabouts are an **open lead** — its bearer is gone, but the artifact itself was not recovered this session.

### [[PP_11_121425_You_Cant_Just_Buy_the_Sun|PP 11 — You Can't Just Buy the Sun]] (12/14/2025)
- **Whereabouts resolved:** a **goblin-faced, cannon-armed blimp** stole the Sun from the wreck of the drowned [[Second Sun Labs|Simic lab]]; [[Benji]] IDs the blimp as **[[Krenko]]'s**, and the party deduces Krenko's crew blew up the lab and **took the Immortal Sun.**
- Now in **[[Krenko]]'s possession** at **[[Krenko Incorporated]]** ([[Tin Street]]). [[Kiku]] reveals it will be **auctioned tonight to the highest bidder** (alongside a Temporal Spear, Hell Spear, an Izzet signet, etc.).
- Becomes the party's central objective: **acquire the Immortal Sun** at the auction — **Plan A** (buy it via reopening the [[First Church of Orzhov]] bank + guild fundraising) or **Plan B** (steal it via a [[Gruul Clans|Gruul]]/planeswalker strike). The Sun lifting the planeswalker lockdown remains the in-world prize.

### [[PP_12_011126_The_Masquerade_Auction|PP 12 — The Masquerade Auction]] (01/11/26)
- Confirmed as the **final auction lot** at [[Krenko Incorporated]] — held in a **covered, chain-hung cage** swaying above the amphitheater. [[Rekka]] warned "the Sun is the last item."
- **Plan A (buy) collapses:** after [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] spent **41,000 gp** of reopened Orzhov bank funds to win "Ogre's Tavern 2," only ~59k remained — **not enough to outbid for the Sun.** The party commits fully to **Plan B (the heist).**
- **Multi-party scramble:** the Sun is contested by the party (Plan B), **[[Narset]]** (steal-to-destroy, via her inside man [[Kalmar]]), [[Isiah Ja-Faank\|Isaiah]]/[[Kwatli Pakal]], the jet-black pair, and Krenko's security — all converging tonight.
- **Cliffhanger:** [[Ogre]] (nat-20 Perception) spots **[[Narset]] climbing Spidey-style toward the caged Sun** as chaos erupts — the heist beginning. "To be continued."

### [[PP_13_012526_Fucking_Barbarians|PP 13 — Fucking Barbarians]] (01/25/26)
- **REMOVED FROM RAVNICA — the campaign hinge.** During the [[Vraska]] fight, [[Narset]] planted an **Izzet apparatus bomb** ("hot potato") on the caged Sun; on detonation she **bagged the Sun and escaped backstage** with her crew (**[[Ravnica Trading Incorporated]]**), taking it **off-plane.**
- **Planeswalking is restored across Ravnica.** The "chains/shackles" binding everyone to the plane since [[Ral Zarek\|Ral]]'s [[Interplanar Beacon]] fall away (Rin/Varis Arcana 26); **hundreds of light-beams streak the sky as trapped planeswalkers leave the plane.** The central "trapped on Ravnica" premise is broken.
- Liberation at a cost: the same moment **uncaged [[Nicol Bolas\|Bolas]]'s open wrath** — he immediately razed swaths of the [[Azorius Senate\|Azorius]] District.
