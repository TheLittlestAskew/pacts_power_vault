# Living Guildpact

The **living embodiment of the [[Guildpact]]** — the magical office that binds all ten of [[Ravnica]]'s guilds to peace and anchors the plane's [[Leylines]]. The Guildpact is not just a document but a **role made flesh in a single being**: while a Living Guildpact stands, the Pact holds and the Leylines remain stable.

## History
- **[[Jace Beleren|Jace]]** was the original Living Guildpact — its physical embodiment. When Jace was assassinated by [[Vraska]] (Session 4), the **Guildpact shattered**, the Leylines began to fray, and Ravnica fell into the war that frames the campaign.
- The campaign's central quest (reforge the Guildpact + revive [[Niv-Mizzet]]) was, in effect, a quest to **re-create the Living Guildpact.**

## Reborn in Niv-Mizzet (PP 17, 04/02/26)
At **[[Maze's End]]**, with the [[Augur of Bolas]] destroyed and the reforge ritual ([[Ogre]]'s [[Black Lotus]] wish + the [[Firemind Vessel]] + the 10 guild tokens) complete, **[[Niv-Mizzet]] is reborn as the new Living Guildpact** — Large, wreathed in a **white aura** (all colors at peace), bearing a **decagon crest on their chest with all ten guilds' colors.** As the embodied Pact, Niv:
- **Obliterated the [[Augur of Bolas]]** with all five colors of mana in one claw, absorbing the black corruption into the crest.
- **Stabilized the rogue red [[Leylines|Leyline]]** (stemmed the bleeding; damage already done is permanent — *"there is no undo button"*).
- **Endorsed [[Dalya Bara|Dahlia]]** as [[Izzet League|Izzet]] leader (replacing [[Ral Zarek|Ral]]).
- **Gathered the ten Leylines to recharge**, then **teleported the party to the surface plaza.**

> **Decagon crest.** Niv's chest-crest — ten sides, all ten guild colors — is the visible mark of the embodied Guildpact. (The DM corrected "dodecagon/dodecahedron" live to **decagon**.) Folded into this concept rather than given its own page.

> **Open thread:** [[Ogre]]'s [[Black Lotus]] wish was *"make me a new Guildpact so I could be one too."* The Living Guildpact is **Niv, not Ogre** — Ogre bears no crest. His subsequent Leyline-grabbing is read as still reaching for that unfulfilled wish.

## Key Events

### [[PP_17_040226_The_Guildpact_Reborn|PP 17 — The Guildpact Reborn]] (04/02/26)
- **The Living Guildpact is restored** — embodied in reborn [[Niv-Mizzet]] with a **decagon crest** of all 10 guild colors. The maze arc closes; the board resets to the **surface war** (Bolas's Citadel, the Eternals, the contested guilds).
