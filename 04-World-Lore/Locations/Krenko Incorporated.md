# Krenko Incorporated

The power base of the goblin crime-king **[[Krenko]]** — a **20–25-story tower** on [[Tin Street]], crowned with a leering goblin face, sitting atop a **sprawling underground metropolis** (9+ levels) hidden beneath the 10th District. The party's old neighborhood mob boss has, in five years, scaled up from petty errands into "Krenko Incorporated."

## Description

A surface tower (with that same goblin grin from the cannon-armed blimp) over a vast **underground city** — at least nine levels deep, where the **goblin-faced blimp** is parked after the [[Immortal Sun]] heist. Ground-floor security is run by **[[Rekka]]** (ex-[[Bone Mine]] guard); the front office is gatekept by **[[Kiku]]** atop a comically huge desk. Mystery figures (a shadowy infiltrator; the planeswalker [[Narset]]) were seen moving through the underground city on the eve of the auction.

## Key Events

### [[PP_11_121425_You_Cant_Just_Buy_the_Sun|PP 11 — You Can't Just Buy the Sun]] (12/14/25)
- Identified as **[[Krenko]]'s** HQ after his crew's goblin-faced blimp stole the **[[Immortal Sun]]** from the drowned [[Second Sun Labs|Simic lab]]; traced to [[Tin Street]], the party's old neighborhood.
- The party **long-rested on a rooftop** opposite the tower: [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]]'s watch caught a goblin messenger with [[Krenko]]'s letter; [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]]'s watch spotted a **shadowy figure** slipping into the HQ (Perception 17/21) and the **−9th-floor underground city** where the blimp was parked.
- Inside: **[[Rekka]]** routed them to **[[Kiku]]**, who revealed the **[[Immortal Sun]] will be auctioned tonight to the highest bidder** (alongside a Temporal Spear, Hell Spear, an Izzet signet, etc.). [[Krenko]] refused to meet.
- [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]] glimpsed the planeswalker **[[Narset]]** shimmering through the underground city. The party left plotting a heist (Plan B) while pursuing the buy (Plan A).

### [[PP_12_011126_The_Masquerade_Auction|PP 12 — The Masquerade Auction]] (01/11/26)
- Hosted the **masked auction** — a masquerade **ballroom** preceding the **amphitheater** auction, [[Krenko\|"Franco"]] emceeing (3 lots, 1 min each, highest bidder). The bar served literal **essence-of-sleep / paralysis / restraining cocktails.**
- The party fanned out for coverage: [[Ogre]] on a chandelier (bribed a guard 7 gp) befriending the Goliath guard **[[Kalmar]]**; [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus\|Orphie]] & [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]] on overwatch; [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]] & [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] stage-left by **[[Rekka]]**'s guarded door; [[Ava]] in the crowd.
- **Attendees:** [[Isiah Ja-Faank\|Isaiah]] ("Johnny," drunk on paralysis cocktails) with **[[Kwatli Pakal]]** ("Jareth Wig"); a purse-throwing **plump goblin lady**; **two jet-black-masked figures** (sent [[Sanis Reylana\|Sanis]] a sleep-spiked drink). Krenko's **anti-tamper barrier** foiled [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth\|Rin]]'s display-dimming Thaumaturgy.
- **Item 1, "Ogre's Tavern 2"** (an upgraded [[Ogre's Traveling Tavern\|Airstream]] replica) went to **[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]] for 41,000 gp** (Orzhov bank funds), after he froze the Isaiah/goblin-lady brawl with **Hold Person.**
- **Cliffhanger:** the covered, chain-hung **cage holding the [[Immortal Sun]]** (the final lot) sways above as **[[Narset]]** climbs toward it — the heist breaking open ([[Ogre]] nat-20 Perception).
