# First Church of Orzhov

The central bank-temple of the collapsed **[[Orzhov Syndicate]]**, standing deserted in the hollow Orzhov district. As of PP 11 it is the party's target for **reopening the Orzhov banking system** so the allied guilds can pool funds to bid on the [[Immortal Sun]].

> Also flagged back in T07 as a **route step** for the [[Niv-Mizzet]] quest — "Sis" ([[Pippi]]) is meant to **recalibrate its portal to the [[Implicit Maze]]'s entrance** at the First Church. The two purposes (bank reopening + Nexus portal) converge here.

## Description

A grand, abandoned institution in the Orzhov ghost-town district — the district has **no living or dead souls** since [[Kaya Cassir|Kaya]] was "dealt with" and the Syndicate collapsed. Restoring it as a functioning bank requires tellers and liquidity, which **[[Lord Aestra]] & [[Lady Aestra]]** (Orzhov refugees) are ready to rally once the party scouts the church.

## Key Events

### [[PP_11_121425_You_Cant_Just_Buy_the_Sun|PP 11 — You Can't Just Buy the Sun]] (12/14/25)
- Established as the **sub-objective of Plan A**: with the [[Orzhov Syndicate|Orzhov]] banking system dead, the party must **restart the bank at the First Church of Orzhov** so the guilds can pool auction funds.
- **[[Lord Aestra]] & [[Lady Aestra]]** (with surviving Orzhov refugees) agree to rally tellers and liquidity to reopen it once the party **scouts the deserted church**; [[Emmara Tandris]] pledges Selesnya funds once banking is live.
- The session ends with the party about to head here to reopen the bank.

### [[PP_12_011126_The_Masquerade_Auction|PP 12 — The Masquerade Auction]] (01/11/26)
- The party reached the deserted, **void** church (the whole Orzhov district scraped to silence — no spirits, bodies, or ambient magic) and found **[[Kaya Cassir|Kaya]] dying on the marble stairs**, half-ghostly/half-physical, her plane-shifting biology severed. She confessed she **opened the vaults and freed every spirit** on [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]'s false promise to save her home [[Tovada]]; the [[Eternals]] then consumed everything. **[[Ogre]] mercy-killed her** (she dispersed, not captured).
- Upstairs in the **Aestra family quarters**, [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] recovered a **diamond** (Chromatic Orb component), jewelry, and an **Orzhov loan-agreement document** he could read by touch — and innately **knew how to reinstate the bank.**
- Via the **basement portal**, Varis recalibrated the coordinates to **[[Pecunia]]** (the Orzhov Grand Vault on Ravnica's second moon) and, on the vault's Orzhov insignia, recited the ledger-ritual to **reopen the First Bank** — bonding to the guild's debt-magic (he can now conjure gold / issue loans). The bank reopening was the **Plan A enabler** and the T07 route step (where "Sis"/[[Pippi]] is to recalibrate the portal to the [[Implicit Maze]]).

### [[PP_15_022226_Reforging_the_Guildpact|PP 15 — Reforging the Guildpact]] (02/22/26)
- **The T07 route step pays off.** The party flew here (after a dragon-back drive-by killing ~100 [[Eternals]] to buy [[Lord Aestra]]'s ragtag defense time) and used the **basement portal** — **"Sis" ([[Pippi]]) recalibrated it from [[Pecunia]] to the [[Implicit Maze]]'s entrance.** Through it, the party descended into the maze for the Guildpact-reforging / [[Niv-Mizzet]]-revival endgame.
