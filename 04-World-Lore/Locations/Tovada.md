# Tovada

**[[Kaya Cassir|Kaya]]'s lost home** — a plane/realm she describes as **"stuck between reality and the ghostly realm,"** from which she alone escaped. A new, unexplored lore thread introduced as Kaya died.

> Spelling unconfirmed (Tovada / Tavada / Vada); canonical page form **Tovada**. A stub — almost nothing is known of it beyond Kaya's dying words.

## Description

Per the dying [[Kaya Cassir|Kaya]], Tovada is caught **between reality and the ghostly realm**; she was **"the only one who got out."** It was her desperation to save Tovada that [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] exploited — he **promised to save her home** in exchange for her unsealing the [[Orzhov Syndicate|Orzhov]] vaults and freeing every spirit, after which his [[Eternals]] devoured the district's souls and magic. Tovada itself remains lost and unreached.

## Key Events

### [[PP_11_011126_The_Masquerade_Auction|PP 11 — The Masquerade Auction]] (01/11/26)
- Named by the dying **[[Kaya Cassir|Kaya]]** as her home: *"stuck between reality and the ghostly realm; I was the only one who got out."*
- Revealed as the lever **[[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]** used to manipulate Kaya — his false promise to **save Tovada** led her to open the Orzhov vaults, dooming the district. An open thread.
