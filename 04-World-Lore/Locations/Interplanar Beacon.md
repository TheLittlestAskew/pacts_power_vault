# Interplanar Beacon

## Description
A device located at or near the top of the [[Fortress of Nivix]] ([[Izzet League]] headquarters). When activated, it calls every planeswalker in the multiverse to [[Ravnica]].

## Properties
- Calls all planeswalkers across the multiverse to Ravnica
- Was blocked by the [[Ghost Mind]]'s interference prior to Session 01
- Activated by [[Ral Zarek]] after the Ghost Mind collapsed

## Key Events

### [[PP_01_071325_Into_the_Ghost_Mind|Session 01 — Into the Ghost Mind]] (07/13/2025)
- Ral Zarek had been attempting to activate the beacon daily; the Ghost Mind's interference prevented it
- After the party caused the Ghost Mind to collapse, the interference was removed
- Ral successfully activated the beacon
- Hundreds of planeswalkers began arriving on Ravnica
- Combined with the [[Immortal Sun]], all arriving planeswalkers are now trapped
