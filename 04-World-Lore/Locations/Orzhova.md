# Orzhova

The towering cathedral-seat of the **[[Orzhov Syndicate]]** in the Orzhov district of the Tenth District. The Syndicate's central bank-temple here, the **[[First Church of Orzhov]]**, is where [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] reopened the First Bank and where the basement portal to the [[Implicit Maze]] sits — see that page for the reopening and the Nexus route step.

> This page is the broader Orzhov cathedral-district seat; the specific bank-temple events (Kaya's death, the bank reopening, the maze portal) live on **[[First Church of Orzhov]]**.

## Key Events

### [[PP_14_022226_Reforging_the_Guildpact|PP 14 — Reforging the Guildpact]] (02/22/26)
- **Front-line defense.** The party flew to the Orzhov district and did a **dragon-back drive-by killing ~100 [[Eternals]]** to buy **[[Lord Aestra]]**'s ragtag defense time, then used the **[[First Church of Orzhov]]** basement portal (recalibrated by [[Pippi|Sis]] from [[Pecunia]] to the [[Implicit Maze]]) to descend toward the Guildpact-reforging endgame.
