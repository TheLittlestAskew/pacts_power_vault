# Second Church of Orzhov

A second **[[Orzhov Syndicate|Orzhov]]** church, posted to the DM's **#Lore Dump** (02/23/25) — the counterpart to the **[[First Church of Orzhov]]** the party reopened (the bank/portal hub where [[Pippi]] recalibrated the route to the [[Implicit Maze]], and where [[Kaya Cassir|Kaya]] died).

## Campaign Role
A further node of the Orzhov religious-financial network that **[[Varis Aestra|Varis]]** comes to head as Guildmaster. Its specific role wasn't reached on-screen in the processed sessions; logged from the DM source to complete the Orzhov geography.
