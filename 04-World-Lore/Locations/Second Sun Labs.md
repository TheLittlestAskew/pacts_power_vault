# Second Sun Labs

An **underwater [[Simic Combine|Simic]] research facility** operated by the [[Second Sun]] — the confirmed **[[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] monstrosity factory** that manufactures **[[Eternals|Eternal]]-plated [[Zombie-Simic mutants]]** (the "monstrosities" the S07 intel described). The rooms imitate open water and the tide rises with the moons throughout, a standing environmental hazard.

## Layout (PP 8)
- **Airlock** — the entry; the gatekeeper [[Larissa]] (offscreen) cleared the party in.
- **Reef area** — waist/chest-deep water, **tide rising** (chest-to-shoulder depth by session's end).
- **Vivarium / specimen room** — tubes of octopus-, crab-, and shark-hybrid Simic mutants, many patched with **undead flesh**; staffed by jeering [[Second Sun]] researchers ("the lab rats"). A captive **crab-humanoid** pounds the glass mouthing *"help me."*
- **Inner office / meeting platform** — more tubes of fully-formed [[Zombie-Simic mutants]] (incl. the captive merfolk [[Vuthala]]); up a short stair is a **dry platform** (~5–8 ft above the water, ~60 ft across the room) where [[Lord Reylana]] & [[Lady Reylana]] sit. [[Prime Speaker Megandar]] emerged from this office.

## Campaign Role

One of the **two open guild gaps** for the [[Niv-Mizzet]] revival ritual ([[Simic Combine|Simic]]). Confirmed as the lab where surviving [[Flameseekers of Truth|Flameseekers]] / captives are experimented on (per S06) and where [[Salena|Selena]] was weaponized using **Reylana blood**. The [[Immortal Sun]] (referenced in S07) powers the work. Houses a captive-rescue thread (the crab-humanoid; [[Vuthala]]).

## Key Events

### [[PP_08_101925_Blood_Runs_Thicker_Than_Water|PP 8 — Blood Runs Thicker Than Water]] (10/19/25)
- The party (cleared in offscreen by [[Larissa]]) toured the facility with the janitor [[Carl]]; [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] cast **Water Breathing** on the whole party (24h) via the [[Mizzium Apparatus]] (29).
- In the **vivarium**, the party empowered [[Carl]] and humiliated the [[Second Sun]] researchers ([[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]]'s pizza-slap, 30; demanded the SOPs / code of conduct under intimidation, 13).
- A captive **crab-humanoid** begged for rescue; the party bluffed toward the inner office as "**auditors of the Second Sun**" — the ruse collapsed (Deception disadv 7).
- [[Prime Speaker Megandar]] opened the inner office, revealing tubes of fully-formed [[Zombie-Simic mutants]] (incl. [[Vuthala]]) and, on a dry platform, **[[Lord Reylana]] & [[Lady Reylana]]**.
- Lord Reylana confessed to killing [[Salena|Selena]] by design; [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] renounced his family. **Combat triggered, initiative rolled — deferred to the next session (rising-tide hazard noted for the fight).**
