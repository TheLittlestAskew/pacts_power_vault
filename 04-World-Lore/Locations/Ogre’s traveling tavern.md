# Ogre's Traveling Tavern

The party's portable tavern and mobile home base — a wagon-tavern pulled by the warforged mechanical horse [[Scrappy]]. Named for [[Ogre]]; [[Rin]] handles its finances and [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] cooks. Capable of driving the streets and (with [[Scrappy]]) flying. The party bought it together in their early days and have lived and traveled out of it ever since.

## Description
A rolling tavern/home that doubles as the party's transport and base of operations. Holds the party's furniture and belongings (including a **Drawer of Holding**). Usually parked at the Sunhome apartment building when not in use.

## Status — DESTROYED
In [[PP_02_072725_I_Cant_Rebuild_You|Transcript 02]], God-Eternal [[God-Eternal Oketra|Oketra]] targeted the flying Tavern after [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]'s flame arrow drew her attention:
- Her first arrow tore a massive hole through it (**78 piercing**; [[Rin]] dropped to 1 HP).
- [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] used [[Blazebringer]] to **detach the Tavern from [[Scrappy]]** and let it fall, getting the party clear.
- Oketra's **second arrow destroyed the falling Tavern** outright.

As of [[PP_03_081025_Thats_Why_No_One_Will_Remember_Your_Name|Transcript 03]], the wreck (with the **Drawer of Holding** and the party's belongings still inside) is unretrieved. The party intends to rebuild — "We can rebuild it. But I can't rebuild you." [[Scrappy]] is the sole surviving piece.
