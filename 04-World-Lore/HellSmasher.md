# HellSmasher

The **final form of [[Blazebringer]]** — a fiery, ancestral **maul/war-hammer** that [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] chose at the climax of the campaign, granted by her ancestor-goddess **[[Athena]]**. A weapon **for building as much as breaking**: *"the greatest bonds can be forged with a hammer."*

## Description / Nature
A massive two-handed maul wreathed in fire, dealing **1d20 damage, +10** — Blazebringer's reforged ultimate form. When Orphie's fire-axe finally "asked to become something new" in the final battle, Athena offered three forms — a **sword** (which would have been "Hellraiser"), an **axe**, or a **hammer.** Orphie chose the hammer (*"I've always wanted a hammer. Let's try something new."*), and Athena named it **HellSmasher**, with the lesson that *"the greatest bonds can be forged with a hammer"* — a tool for building as much as for breaking. Like Blazebringer, it retains the Amonkhet/[[Hazoret]]-metal heritage of Orphie's ancestral weapon.

## Campaign Role
The weapon that ends the campaign in Orphie's hands. In the finale she uses it to **cave [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] in** — smashing his armor, clawed feet, and (gleefully) his pinky toe — as the party slowly brings the dragon down. In her epilogue, HellSmasher and the family bloodline are the inheritance she means to pass down. The choice of a hammer over a sword/axe crystallizes Orphie's whole arc: the bruiser learning that strength can build, not just break.

### [[PP_21_053126_I_Miss_You_Buddy|PP 21 — I Miss You, Buddy]] (05/31/26)
- ⭐ **Forged at the climax.** Reaching for [[Athena]] through her unanswered-prayer moment, [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] is pulled into a vision where Athena offers Blazebringer's final form — sword ("Hellraiser"), axe, or hammer. Orphie **chooses the hammer** — *"I've always wanted a hammer. Let's try something new."* — and Athena names it **HellSmasher** (a maul, **1d20 + 10**), teaching *"the greatest bonds can be forged with a hammer."*

- **Epilogue:** Orphie sets out to find the **Ur-Dragon** (Bolas's progenitor), meaning to pass down the bloodline and the weapon — HellSmasher as the legacy of [[Athena]]'s line.
