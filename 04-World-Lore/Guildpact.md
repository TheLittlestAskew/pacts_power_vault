# Guildpact

The **Guildpact** is the foundational magical law of [[Ravnica]] — the binding contract/office that holds all **ten guilds** to peace and **anchors the plane's [[Leylines]].** It is both an idea (the law that keeps the guilds from open war) and a **power made flesh**: while a [[Living Guildpact]] stands, the Pact holds and the Leylines stay stable. Reforging it is the campaign's central quest.

## What it is
- **The law:** the ten-guild compact that distributes power across Ravnica and keeps any one guild from dominating — the source of the plane's order.
- **The embodiment:** the [[Living Guildpact]] — a single being who *is* the Pact made physical. [[Jace Beleren|Jace]] was the original. (See [[Living Guildpact]] for the embodiment's own page.)
- **The artifact / shreds:** the **physical First Guildpact** — torn to **shreds** when the Pact shattered. The party recovered the **Guildpact shreds (display case)** during the masquerade heist ([[PP_13_012526_Fucking_Barbarians|PP 13]]) and later placed them, with the **ten guild tokens** and [[Jace]]'s old speech-paper, on the pedestal at **[[Maze's End]]** to power the reforge.

## History
- **Shattered (Session 4):** when [[Jace Beleren|Jace]] — the Living Guildpact — was assassinated by [[Vraska]], the Guildpact **broke**, the [[Leylines]] began to fray, and Ravnica collapsed into the war that frames the campaign.
- **The quest:** recover the Guildpact shreds, gather a **token from each of the ten guilds**, descend the [[Implicit Maze]], and **reforge the Pact** while reviving [[Niv-Mizzet]] to embody it.
- **Reforged ([[PP_15_022226_Reforging_the_Guildpact|PP 15]] → [[PP_17_040226_The_Guildpact_Reborn|PP 17]]):** at [[Maze's End]], [[Ogre]]'s [[Black Lotus]] wish + the [[Firemind Vessel]] + the ten tokens completed the ritual. The Pact was re-created and **embodied in reborn [[Niv-Mizzet]]** (Large, white-aura, bearing the **decagon crest** of all ten guild colors), which **stabilized the rogue red [[Leylines|Leyline]].**
- **Peace ([[PP_21_053126_I_Miss_You_Buddy|PP 21]]):** with [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] defeated and sealed, Niv-Mizzet — as the Living Guildpact — **declares peace** across Ravnica.
