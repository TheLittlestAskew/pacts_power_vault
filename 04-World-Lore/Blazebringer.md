# Blazebringer

The **ancestral greataxe of the [[Athena|Levistus]] bloodline** — [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]]'s signature weapon and "emotional spine" — a fiery, **indestructible** two-handed axe handed down her family line and carried before her by her ancestor **[[Athena]]**. At the campaign's climax it asked to become something new and was **reforged by Athena into [[HellSmasher]]** (PP 21).

## Description / Nature
A heavy two-handed greataxe (**1d12 + 8 slashing, +10 to hit; Heavy, Two-Handed, Cleave**) wreathed in fire. It is partly forged of **Amonkhet / [[Hazoret]] metal**, which is why it **never rusts or breaks** — *"it'll never rust the way I never will."* The same axe was wielded by [[Athena]] generations ago, marking Orphie's path as a mirror of her ancestor's.

## Campaign Role
Orphie's defining weapon throughout the campaign — the tool of the party's tank, bouncer, and front line. Its lineage is also the thread that pulls Orphie's story toward [[Athena]], the Levistus bloodline, and the warning about the world-eating **oil** (Phyrexian-oil) that *"once killed [[Ogre]] dead on [[Dominaria]]."* In the finale, the weapon's reforging into [[HellSmasher]] — Orphie choosing a **hammer** over a sword or axe — crystallizes her arc: that strength can **build**, not just break.

## Key Events

### [[PP_07_100525_The_Reylana_Infiltration|PP 7 — The Reylana Infiltration]] (10/05/25)
- In a prophetic dream, [[Athena]] reveals **Blazebringer is partly forged of Amonkhet / [[Hazoret]] metal** — the source of its indestructibility (*"it'll never rust the way I never will"*).
- Athena ties the axe to a bloodline warning: an **oil on another world that doesn't wash off and has eaten whole planes** — *"Don't touch the oil. The oil is bad."*

### [[PP_21_053126_I_Miss_You_Buddy|PP 21 — I Miss You, Buddy]] (05/31/26)
- ⭐ **Reforged into [[HellSmasher]].** At the climax, the fire-axe "asks to become something new." Reaching [[Athena]] through her once-unanswered prayer, [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] is offered Blazebringer's final form — a **sword** (would be "Hellraiser"), an **axe**, or a **hammer.** She chooses the **hammer**, and Athena names it **[[HellSmasher]]** (a maul, **1d20 + 10**): *"the greatest bonds can be forged with a hammer."*
