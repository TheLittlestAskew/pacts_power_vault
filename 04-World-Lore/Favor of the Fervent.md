# Favor of the Fervent

[[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]]'s signature **medium armor** — scarlet-and-gold plate with pauldrons shaped like stylized **wings of flame**. When the wearer leaps into the air, the armor shimmers with holy energy. (Official item stats from the DM's **#Lore Dump**, 02/23/25.)

## Mechanics (as written)
- **AC:** 15 + Dex modifier (max 2) · **Stealth:** Disadvantage · **Weight:** 18
- **Zealous Devotion:** while **airborne** after **healing another target**, the wearer may cast **Sacred Flame** as a free action.

## Campaign Role
Fits Rin's airborne, healing-forward holy warrior identity — the **champion of [[Dol Arrah]]** (see [[Dolora]]). *Zealous Devotion* rewards her signature support-then-smite rhythm.
