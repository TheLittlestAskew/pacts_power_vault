# Leylines

The intricate lines of power that hold the plane of [[Ravnica]] together — the manifestation of Power on this plane, sprawled across the globe as the network also called the [[Implicit Maze]]. They converge at a central **Nexus** (the site where the [[Guildpact]] was forged). The first ten guildleaders bound the Leylines to peace and prosperity through the [[Guildpact]]; [[Jace Beleren|Jace]], as the Living Guildpact, was their embodiment.

## The Crisis — Leylines Unraveling (PP 13)
As of [[PP_13_021326_Hold_the_Wall|PP 13]], Ravnica's Leylines are **catastrophically coming apart at the seams** — threatening to **shatter the plane itself.** Diagnosed by the party ([[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]] Arcana 28; [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] Arcana 24; [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]]), the damage is **cumulative**:
- **[[Jace Beleren|Jace]]'s death** frayed them (the Living Guildpact was their anchor; the [[Guildpact]] shattered when he was killed).
- **[[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]'s [[Bolas's Citadel|Citadel]] is tapping them** — draining the Leylines directly.
- **Cumulative war stress** — mass death, guild-leader upheavals, and the destruction of the [[Azorius Senate|Azorius]] District — like cracks spreading through a single pane of glass.

A **raw Leyline crater** has opened in the **[[Azorius Senate|Azorius]] District**, exposing a **Leyline tentacle** that vaporizes dragons and Eternals alike. The party confirms that **reforging the [[Guildpact]] would help stabilize the Leylines** (with the shreds + the [[Niv-Mizzet]] box in hand) — making the Main Quest **doubly urgent** (it saves the world, not just the war).

> The cliffhanger: a Leyline surge pulses across the [[Selesnya Conclave|Conclave]] and strikes the **Great Tree of [[Vitu-Ghazi]]** — which then **uproots itself and stands up** (alignment/intent unknown).

## Relationship to the Implicit Maze
The Leylines **are** the network of the [[Implicit Maze]]; the **Nexus** at the maze's center is where they converge and where the [[Guildpact]] was forged. (The raw transcript also conflates the Leylines with the "implicit maze" — flagged.)

## Key Events

### [[PP_13_021326_Hold_the_Wall|PP 13 — Hold the Wall]] (02/13/26)
- The party senses (Arcana) the Leylines **unraveling** — Jace's death + [[Bolas's Citadel|Citadel]] tapping + war stress threatening to shatter the plane.
- A **Leyline crater** opens in the [[Azorius Senate|Azorius]] District, a raw Leyline tentacle vaporizing dragons and Eternals.
- Confirmed: **reforging the [[Guildpact]]** would help **stabilize** the Leylines.
- A Leyline surge **awakens the Great Tree of [[Vitu-Ghazi]]** (cliffhanger).

### [[PP_14_022226_Reforging_the_Guildpact|PP 14 — Reforging the Guildpact]] (02/22/26)
- **The Leyline moat & the five color-trials.** At the [[Implicit Maze]], the Nexus is ringed by a **moat of raw Leyline energy** (touch = disintegration) and gated by **five color-trials** (the WUBRG primordial colors — see [[Implicit Maze]]).
- **The Guildpact is reforged** at [[Maze's End]] ([[Ogre]]'s [[Black Lotus]] wish + the [[Firemind Vessel]] + the 10 tokens) and **[[Niv-Mizzet]] begins reviving** — the long-sought stabilizer for the failing Leylines (in progress).
- **The black Leyline is corrupted.** [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]] senses the **black Leyline is poisoned by [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]'s miasma** (the same corruption as [[Penny]]'s mark / [[The Second Sun]]), caused by **[[Bolas's Citadel]] rising from the [[Golgari Swarm|Golgari]] and tapping the lines.** The long-term cure is to **destroy the Citadel.** Out of that corruption + the party's shed hatreds rose the **[[Augur of Bolas|"Ogre of Bolas"]]** boss (cliffhanger).

### [[PP_16_040226_The_Guildpact_Reborn|PP 16 — The Guildpact Reborn]] (04/02/26)
- **The rogue red Leyline is stabilized.** Reborn [[Niv-Mizzet]] (the [[Living Guildpact]]) **stems the bleeding** of the unstable **red Leyline** — the one that **disintegrated the Tarkir dragons and Eternals near the [[Azorius Senate|Azorius]] District** (the S13 crater/tentacle). The fix is a **stabilization, not a reversal**: damage already dealt is permanent — *"There is no undo button… we technically do have one, but I don't think we want to use it."*
- **The Leyline power-grab.** Niv gathers all **ten Leylines** to recharge; the party, taking it personally, **grabs on too** (Constitution saves). [[Ogre]] (Con 28/24/21), [[Varis Aestra|Varis]], [[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]] (2 exhaustion deep, barely survives), and [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] (regains expended rage) **benefit** — restoring spell slots/resources, eyes flashing. **[[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] hesitates, grabs a swirling black-and-green Leyline at the wrong moment, and FAILS: 3 levels of exhaustion + loses all his highest-level spell slots.** (Raw Leyline contact remains dangerous — the moat-disintegration rule made personal.)
- **The corruption is not fully cured.** Even with the Guildpact reborn, the **black-Leyline corruption rooted in [[Bolas's Citadel]]** persists until the Citadel falls (the Citadel still stands at session's end). Stabilization buys time; it does not end the plane-critical crisis.
