# Zhalfir

The **home plane of [[Teferi]]** — long phased out of the multiverse and now **recovered** by the time-wizard. In PP 17, Teferi tears open a crack in reality and arrives at Ravnica's grand council with a **Zhalfir army** (the "Zhalfir army"), volunteered to aid Ravnica's war against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]].

> **Spelling note (raw transcript):** "Zhalfir" (Teferi's plane) — spelling flagged in the session note; no spell-check pass per Taylor.

## Campaign Role
A late-arc reinforcement payoff (mirroring the [[Ixalan]] and Tarkir dragon reinforcements that arrived once planeswalking was restored): with the [[Immortal Sun]] off-plane, [[Teferi]] can reach Ravnica and bring **Zhalfir's army** to the coalition. Teferi is later **called to the Zhalfir front lines** and departs (reachable via *Sending*), so the Zhalfir army anchors a second war front during the finale.

## Key Events

### [[PP_17_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 17 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26)
- **[[Teferi]] arrives with the Zhalfir army.** At the **grand council** at the surfaced [[Maze's End]], Teferi **tore open a crack** and brought a **Zhalfir army** through — his recovered home plane's forces — volunteering them for Ravnica's war.
- At session's end, **Teferi is called away to the Zhalfir front lines** and departs (reachable via *Sending*).
