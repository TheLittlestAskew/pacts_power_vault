# Tarkir

A plane of the multiverse defined by **dragons and warring clans** — the home of [[Ugin]] (the Spirit Dragon) and the source of much of the campaign's draconic lore. Long referenced across the campaign (Ugin's shifting "Tarkir timelines," [[Narset]]'s restoration of Tarkir's dragons, the Jeskai, and [[Shaylin]]'s Tarkir company [[Ravnica Trading Incorporated]]) — and the plane [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] departs to steward at the campaign's end.

## Campaign Role
The wellspring of the dragon-power threaded through the party — [[Varis Aestra|Varis]]'s Draconic/Ugin sorcery, [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]]'s ancestor [[Athena]]'s travels, and the dragon reinforcements [[Narset]] portaled to Ravnica's war. In the finale, [[Ugin]] **bestows the mantle of protecting and heralding dragons on Tarkir upon Varis**, who departs Ravnica to take it up — the campaign's draconic arc resolving on the dragon plane itself.

## Key Events

### [[PP_21_053126_I_Miss_You_Buddy|PP 21 — I Miss You, Buddy]] (05/31/26)
- ⭐ **Varis's destiny.** Before sealing himself away with [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]], **[[Ugin]] bestows on [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] the mantle of protecting and heralding the dragons of Tarkir** — an immense well of power and a call to the dragon plane.
- **Epilogue:** Varis **hands the [[Orzhov Syndicate|Orzhov]] Guild to his adopted father** and **departs for Tarkir** to steward Ugin's dragons.
