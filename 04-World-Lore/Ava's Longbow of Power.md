# Ava's Longbow of Power

A **gold dragon-accented magic longbow** belonging to the copper dragon **[[Ava]]**, **loaned to [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]** for the [[Immortal Sun]] heist.

> Spelling/name unconfirmed; canonical page form **Ava's Longbow of Power**.

## Description

Ava's own bow, ornamented in gold dragon styling. She **hissed at Sanis's Perun's-Last-Word bow** (the [[Argo Lot]]-linked weapon) but lent him **hers** instead for the auction job — a transactional gesture in keeping with her heist alliance.
