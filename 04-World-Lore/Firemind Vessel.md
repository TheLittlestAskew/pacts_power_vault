# Firemind Vessel

The container holding **[[Niv-Mizzet]]'s preserved soul** — the box the party has carried since they absorbed the Firemind's essence at the dragon's corpse (T04). Earlier referred to as the **Dragon Spirit Box** (carried by [[Varis Aestra|Varis]]); by PP 14 it is carried by [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]] and named the **Firemind Vessel**. At [[Maze's End]] it became the *vessel* the reforge ritual required, opening the portal that revives Niv-Mizzet.

## Campaign Role
The keystone of the **Revive [[Niv-Mizzet]]** quest. [[Ugin]] (T07) confirmed the box must be brought to the Nexus / center of the [[Implicit Maze]]; in PP 14 it served as the **vessel** for the barren circle above [[Maze's End]]'s pedestal, opening the ceiling portal at the Leyline confluence through which Niv-Mizzet emerges.

## Key Events

### [[PP_14_022226_Reforging_the_Guildpact|PP 14 — Reforging the Guildpact]] (02/22/26)
- Carried into [[Maze's End]] by [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]. As [[Ogre]]'s [[Black Lotus]] wish took hold, the Vessel **rose to the pedestal, opened, and formed a portal in the ceiling** at the five-color Leyline confluence — the Leyline colors filtered into 10 pairs and beamed into the guild seats.
- **Being consumed/transformed** as [[Niv-Mizzet]] is revived (emerging as a full dragon through the widening ceiling portal).

### [[PP_15_031326_A_Scared_Child|PP 15 — A Scared Child]] (03/13/26)
- **Active revival conduit.** Throughout the boss fight at [[Maze's End]], the Vessel's **ceiling portal stays open** — the conduit through which [[Ogre]] keeps **pulling [[Niv-Mizzet]] back** (claw, then arm, slowly emerging). The portal is the live tether for the revival, which remains **in progress** (gated by the [[Augur of Bolas|"Ogre of Bolas"]] fight) at the session's cut.
