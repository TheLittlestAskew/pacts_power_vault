# Infernal Embrace

[[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]]'s signature **light armor** — a form-fitting piece adorned with **[[god-metal|Hazoretium]]**, radiating oppressive heat. When the wearer **rages**, the gold veins ignite and run like liquid fire. (Official item stats from the DM's **#Lore Dump**, 02/23/25.)

## Mechanics (as written)
- **AC:** 13 + Dex modifier · **Stealth:** Disadvantage · **Weight:** 8
- **Shake it Off:** while raging and **under half maximum HP**, at the start of the wearer's turn, gain **1d12 + half barbarian level (round up)** as temporary hit points. The effect refreshes only when the previous temp HP from it is exhausted.

## Campaign Role
The thematic twin of Orphie's **Hazoret-metal** weapon [[Blazebringer]] (later [[HellSmasher]]) — both draw on the same Amonkhet/[[Hazoret]] heritage [[Athena]] describes. *Shake it Off* keeps the party's bruiser standing through the rages that define her front-line role.
