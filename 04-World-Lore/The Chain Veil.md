# The Chain Veil

A veil of chains worn by **[[Liliana Vess]]** — a powerful artifact that **controls the dead** (and constructs of the dead, such as the [[Eternals]]). It identifies its bearer: a **History check** ([[Rin]] 19) recognized the veil as Liliana's, exposing "Professor [[Onyx]]" as Liliana in [[PP_06_091725_Soul_Integrated_Soul_Storage_Get_It_Right|Transcript 06]].

## Powers (observed)
- **Controls the dead** — commands [[Eternals]]; Liliana illusioned ~15 Eternals to appear as *hundreds*.
- **Deflects magic** — tanked [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]]'s Scorching Ray with no effect.

## The Contract
The veil carries a **binding contract**. It originally belonged to **demons**; Liliana's "loophole" was to **kill the demons**, but the artifact then required a new contract holder, so she **contracted with [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]** instead. [[Rin]] (Arcana 21) confirmed this contract is **real and far stronger/stricter** than the one that once bound [[Penny]] (which [[Rakdos]] was able to transfer). The **consequence of breaking it is unknown** — Liliana insists she must remain on Bolas's side.
