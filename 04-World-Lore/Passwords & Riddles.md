# Passwords & Riddles

In-world **passwords, codes, and riddles** the DM posted to the **#Lore Dump** channel over the campaign. Collected here as a quick reference.

## Statue Passwords — "Amara, the Draconic Demon Blade" (11/03/24)
| Location | Password |
|---|---|
| Front Statues | **Aktru** |
| Courtyard Statues | **Mardu** |
| Inner Tower Statues | **Yasova** |

> "Mardu" and "Yasova" are **[[Tarkir]]** references (Mardu = the clan ruled by [[Dragonlord Kolaghan]]; Yasova Dragonclaw is a Temur Tarkir figure) — fitting for a **Draconic** demon blade and consistent with [[Varis Aestra|Varis]]'s "Ancestral Draconic Bulwark" / [[Ugin]]'s Tarkir mantle.

## Orzhov Tithe Inscription (02/16/25)
> *"None escape the tithe, not even in death. All must pay, yet only the rightful may decide. To those who seek passage, name the one who bore the tax unjustly."*

Tied to the **[[Orzhov Syndicate]]** / [[First Church of Orzhov]] passage puzzles (the "name the one who bore the tax unjustly" answer-gate).

## The Color-Cycle Riddle (02/22/26)
> *"From jungle to sea, from sea to stone, from stone to field, from field to bone, what am I?"*

Dated to **[[Maze's End]]** / the [[Implicit Maze]] color-trials — the cycle **jungle → sea → stone → field → bone** maps to the **WUBRG guild colors / Ravnican ecosystem cycle** the maze's five trials drew on (PP 14).

## Disembodied Voice (06/25/25)
> *"Gaze within and see yourself reflected… the secrets within the truth must be given."*

A self-reflection / honesty gate (the kind of "name your hatred / face yourself" challenge that recurs at the [[Ghost Mind]] and the [[Implicit Maze]] trials).
