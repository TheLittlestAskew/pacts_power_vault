# god-metal

A near-indestructible **metal of [[Amonkhet]]** associated with that plane's gods (e.g. [[Hazoret]]). It appears throughout the campaign as the material of god-tier and ancestral weapons — it does not rust or break, and god-metal armaments **teleport back to their wielder** after being thrown.

## Properties (observed)
- **Effectively unbreakable** — the reason [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]]'s [[Blazebringer]] never rusts or breaks (per [[Athena]], T07: partly forged of Amonkhet/[[Hazoret]] metal).
- **Teleporting armaments** — the **god-metal spears** thrown by the **God-Eternals** ([[God-Eternal Bontu|Bontu]], [[God-Eternal Kefnet|Kefnet]]) **return to the wielder** after striking (PP 14), making them very hard to capture. The same metal forged [[Tezzeret]]'s gear.
- Carries the god-power of Amonkhet's pantheon (the God-Eternals' spears deal massive necrotic/force damage and can paralyze).

## Campaign Role
A connective thread between [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]]'s ancestral axe, [[Tezzeret]]'s weaponry, and [[Nicol Bolas]]'s **God-Eternals** — all forged of the same Amonkhet god-metal. In [[PP_14_021326_Hold_the_Wall|PP 14]] the party identifies the God-Eternals' teleporting spears as god-metal, which frames [[Ogre]]'s recurring plan to **grab a god-spear and ride it back to the god** (since the spears warp home anyway).

## Key Events

### [[PP_07_100525_The_Reylana_Infiltration|PP 7 — The Reylana Infiltration]] (10/05/25)
- [[Athena]] revealed [[Blazebringer]] is partly forged of **Amonkhet / [[Hazoret]] metal** — why it never breaks.

### [[PP_14_021326_Hold_the_Wall|PP 14 — Hold the Wall]] (02/13/26)
- The **God-Eternals' spears** ([[God-Eternal Bontu|Bontu]]'s, [[God-Eternal Kefnet|Kefnet]]'s) are identified ([[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]] Arcana 28/29) as the **same Amonkhet god-metal** as [[Tezzeret]]'s gear — and confirmed to **teleport back to the wielder.**

### [[PP_18_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 18 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26)
- **Consumed into the Drillinator core.** The party's leftover stock of **"god metal"** (the [[Tezzeret]]/hazoretium material) was **forged into the stabilizing plate** of [[Dalya Bara|Dahlia]]'s **[[The Drillinator|Drillinator]]** — the **surfactant** that lets the unstable lightning + extracted-Eternal-soul core mix ("oil and water"). The plate only *harmonized* the souls once **[[Ogre]] imbued it with Spirits of Death** (his soul/death magic) — *"it feels right."* The god-metal is **spent** (consumed into the drill).
- Raw-transcript names for the material: **"Tezzy / hazoretium / god metal."**
