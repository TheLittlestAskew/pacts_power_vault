# Grixis's Lament

[[Ogre]]'s signature **rune-etched bracers** — light armor whose arcing inscribed runes trace erratic paths across the bracers, crackling with residual energy. Its surface ripples between **deep crimson and electric blue**, and the metal pulses with a black glow in dim light. (Official item stats from the DM's **#Lore Dump**, 02/23/25.)

## Mechanics (as written)
- **Passive:** +2 bonus to AC if wearing no armor and using no shield.
- **Tip the Scales (Bonus Action):** the wearer can redistribute a weapon bonus (**+3** currently) between **AC**, **Attack/Damage**, and **Magic** bonus for 60 seconds.
- **Last Grasp:** *"??? Ogre will find out."* — an unrevealed third property.

## Campaign Role
The likely in-fiction basis for the **"Shield Bracer (+3 AC)"** the vault tracks for Ogre — the *Tip the Scales* redistribution matches the +3 bonus Ogre shifts toward AC in combat (e.g. the **Dusk → Shield Bracer** loadout from PP 16). The Grixis (crimson/blue/black) coloring fits Ogre's death-magic, Eldritch-Blast fighting style.
