# Black Lotus

A legendary artifact carried by [[Ogre]] — a **conditional wish-spell** that can only be triggered by someone "strong enough to use it." Repeatedly framed as a Chekhov's gun for the campaign's finale.

## Properties (observed)
- A **wish-cast usable only by a planeswalker of considerable power** — [[Ogre]] does **not** qualify.
- **PP 13 framing:** described as a **"conditional wish spell"** whose **condition is unknown** ("must be strong enough to use it"). The exact trigger remains a mystery.
- **T03 reveal:** [[Ogre]] once unknowingly wished on it "to keep all his friends safe forever" — it did **not** work (he isn't a powerful enough planeswalker).

## Campaign Role
A latent finale tool. [[Ogre]] keeps it in his backpack with [[Pippi]]. In [[PP_13_021326_Hold_the_Wall|PP 13]] he inventories it among his **"MacGuffins"** (alongside the **shreds of the First [[Guildpact]]**) as the party confirms reforging the Guildpact would help stabilize the failing [[Leylines]] — flagging the Black Lotus's unknown condition as a wildcard for the endgame.

### [[PP_14_022226_Reforging_the_Guildpact|PP 14 — Reforging the Guildpact]] (02/22/26)
- **EXPENDED — the Chekhov's gun fires.** At [[Maze's End]], [[Ogre]] held up the Black Lotus over the pedestal (with the [[Guildpact]] shreds, [[Benji]]'s Dimir pin, and [[Jace Beleren|Jace]]'s S01 paper) and **wished for a new pact** (*"I wish for a new pact."*). Unlike his failed earlier attempt, **this time the petals unfurled and disintegrated and the wish took hold** — the long-mysterious **condition was being "powerful enough"** (the party reached the requisite power). The Black Lotus is **spent.**
