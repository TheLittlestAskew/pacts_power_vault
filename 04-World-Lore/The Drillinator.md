# The Drillinator

[[Dalya Bara|Dahlia]]'s massive **Izzet tunneling drill** — a **Lazotep-plated** bore driven by a core of **lightning + extracted Eternal souls**. Built to tunnel under Ravnica toward [[Bolas's Citadel]], it is the party's transport down to the [[The Rot Warren|Rot Warren]] and, ultimately, the means of opening a path to the Citadel for the final assault.

> **Naming note (raw transcript):** "the Drillinator." No spell-check pass per Taylor.

## Description / Build
- **Lazotep-plated bit**, smoothed by [[Orphea "Orphie" Levistus|Orphie]] hammering with [[Blazebringer]] (a "golf-club swing"), motivated by [[Teferi]]'s minor-illusion of **"Isaiah's face"** on the bit + [[Sanis Reylana|Sanis]]'s pep talk.
- **Core:** **Ravnican lightning + extracted Eternal souls** — which **wouldn't mix** ("oil and water"); the core needed a **surfactant**, reinforced with **"god metal"** (leftover **[[Tezzeret]]/hazoretium** [[god-metal]] the party had given Dahlia).
- **The key:** **[[Ogre]] imbued the god-metal plate with Spirits of Death** (his soul/death magic) — it *"felt right,"* and his magic is **what actually harmonized the Eternal souls with the metal.** [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] Fireballed it; **six combined efforts stabilized the core.**

## Campaign Role
The Izzet payoff of the "god metal" the party had been carrying since the God-Eternal fights — consumed into the Drillinator's stabilizing plate. Mechanically central: **Ogre's death/soul magic** is what makes the Eternal-soul core sing, tying back to his corrupted-by-Bolas powers and his unfulfilled "be a Guildpact" wish. The drill **bores through the Myconid colony** (triggering the Raise Dead restitution) and down to the **[[The Rot Warren|Rot Warren]]**, then opens the route to [[Bolas's Citadel]].
