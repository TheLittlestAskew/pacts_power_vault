# Ancestral Draconic Bulwark

[[Varis Aestra|Varis]]'s **signature draconic gear**, named in the DM's **#Lore Dump** Player-Characters table (*"Varis — Ancestral Draconic Bulwark user"*).

> ⚠ **Stats not captured.** The archived #Lore Dump is **truncated** before this item's stat block — only the name survives. Re-archive the channel to recover its mechanics.

## Campaign Role
Thematically central to Varis's arc: his **ancestral / draconic** heritage culminates in the finale when **[[Ugin]] mantles him with the dragons of [[Tarkir]]** ([[PP_21_053126_I_Miss_You_Buddy|PP 21]]), and he serves as the **[[Orzhov Syndicate]] Guildmaster** wielding the bank's debt-magic. The Bulwark is the bloodline-armor underpinning that draconic, protective identity.
