# Mizzium Cloaker

A rare **Izzet** invisibility device built from **Mizzium**. (Official item from the DM's **#Lore Dump**, 12/15/24.)

## Mechanics (as written)
- **Activation:** must roll **above a 5** to use; the required success roll **increases by 5 per use**, and **resets to 5 when repaired by an Izzet artificer.**
- **Effect:** **Invisible for 1 hour.** Taking damage, attacking, or casting a spell breaks invisibility. Certain ambient energy sources may cause malfunction.
- **Failure:** on a failed activation roll, the user takes **2d10 force damage** and is **blinded for 1 minute.**
- *Set: Buff, Jewelry.*

## Campaign Role
A strong candidate for **[[Rinestra "Rin" Genleth|Rin]]'s "invisibility gauntlet" that broke** in [[PP_07_100525_The_Reylana_Infiltration|PP 7]] — the escalating per-use difficulty + the "repaired by an Izzet artificer" reset line up with an Izzet device wearing out and needing repair.
