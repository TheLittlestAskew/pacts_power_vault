# The Second Sun

## Overview

The Second Sun is the hostile faction that formed after the [[Tenth District]] split following the shattering of the [[Guildpact]] and the party's 11-month coma. 

## Member Guilds

- [[Azorius Senate]]
- [[Golgari Swarm]]
- [[Cult of Rakdos]] (nominally, though Rakdos has a friendlier relationship with the party)

## Opposition

"The Allies" oppose The Second Sun:
- [[Boros Legion]]
- [[Gruul Clans]]
- [[Izzet League]]

The [[Selesnya Conclave]] serves as neutral ground. The [[Orzhov Syndicate]] has collapsed. [[House Dimir]] is unaligned.
