# Flameseekers of Truth

A **resistance movement** founded by [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]] inside [[Strixhaven]], revealed in [[PP_03_081025_Thats_Why_No_One_Will_Remember_Your_Name|Transcript 03]].

## Background
When [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]] infiltrated [[Strixhaven]] and discovered students were being abducted and turned into undead for their planeswalker sparks, she **started her own rebellion** — the Flameseekers of Truth — recruiting students to fight back. She was **betrayed by [[Onyx]]**, captured, and imprisoned for years. The current state of the movement is unknown.
