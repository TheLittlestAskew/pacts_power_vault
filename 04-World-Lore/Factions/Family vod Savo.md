# Family vod Savo

A **[[Golgari Swarm|Golgari]] family line**, identified in the DM's **#Lore Dump** by a **pin on a Golgari elf's robe**. Named members include **Myra Deathbloom** (see [[Myra]]) and **Saskia vod Savo** (see [[Saskia]]).

## The name connection
"**vod Savo**" is almost certainly the same family name as **[[Yarod Votsavo]]** — the **Golgari Lich** whose raw-transcript aliases include *"Yorvo Vod Savo."* This ties the vod Savo line directly to the **first Golgari Guild Leader / lich** who later resurfaces (PP 17) as the Swarm's leader. If so, the vod Savo family is the **Golgari necromantic bloodline** behind Sanis's pact-origin.

> ⚠ **Flagged, not asserted:** the vault currently portrays **[[Myra]]** and **[[Saskia]]** as easygoing **[[Selesnyan Conclave|Selesnya]]** "weed-girls" / refugee-intake helpers (PP 10). The DM source instead files **Myra Deathbloom** and **Saskia vod Savo** under **Golgari (vod Savo family)**. These may be the same individuals (Golgari kin encountered at the Selesnya relief camp) or distinct NPCs sharing first names — **left for confirmation with the DM; existing pages not overwritten.**
