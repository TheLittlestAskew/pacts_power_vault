# Ravnica Trading Incorporated

A trading company / covert crew led by **[[Shaylin]]** and tied to the planeswalker **[[Narset]]** — the organization behind the operation to seize the **[[Immortal Sun]]** and take it off-plane. Effectively [[Narset]]'s Tarkir troop operating under a commercial front on Ravnica.

> Spelling unconfirmed (raw transcript); canonical page form **Ravnica Trading Incorporated** (Ravnica Trading Inc.).

## Overview

A network of allies converging on the [[Immortal Sun]] heist. Its membership overlaps with [[Narset]]'s crew — the doppelganger guard **[[Kalmar]]** (planted inside [[Krenko Incorporated]] security) and the ex-[[Bone Mine]] guard **[[Rekka]]** (presumed the red-haired carriage driver) — all read by the party as friendly. Its purpose at the auction was the same as the party's: get the Sun out of [[Krenko]]'s hands and off Ravnica.
