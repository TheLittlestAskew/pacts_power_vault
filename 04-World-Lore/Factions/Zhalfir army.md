# Zhalfir army

The military force of **[[Zhalfir]]** — [[Teferi]]'s recovered home plane — brought to Ravnica through a crack in reality during the grand council. Volunteered by Teferi to **aid Ravnica's war** against [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]], they anchor a war front while Teferi is called to lead them.

## Campaign Role
A late-arc off-plane reinforcement (alongside the [[Ixalan]] armies and Tarkir dragons brought in once the [[Immortal Sun]] was removed). The Zhalfir army is Teferi's contribution to the coalition formally united at the council; Teferi departs to its **front lines** at session's end (reachable via *Sending*).

## Key Events

### [[PP_17_040526_Pacts_and_Power|PP 17 — Pacts and Power]] (04/05/26)
- **Arrived through a crack** opened by [[Teferi]] at the **grand council** (surfaced [[Maze's End]]) — *"It's very odd as a time wizard to be late to the party."*
- Volunteered to **aid Ravnica's war**; [[Teferi]] is later **called to the Zhalfir front lines** and departs to lead them.
