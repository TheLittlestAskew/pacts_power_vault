# Elder Spell

The magic at the heart of [[Nicol Bolas]]'s **spark harvesting**, revealed by [[Chandra Nalaar|Chandra]] in [[PP_03_081025_Thats_Why_No_One_Will_Remember_Your_Name|Transcript 03]].

## What It Does
- The **Elder Spell** is cast in tandem with a **[[Spark-Harvester Trident]]**. Once cast, a planeswalker's spark can be **harvested** using the trident.
- Per the party's understanding, the victim **dies first**, and the spark is then extracted; there is no way to resuscitate a planeswalker once their spark is harvested.
- [[Nicol Bolas]] **absorbs** the harvested sparks atop [[Bolas's Citadel]] (seen as orbs of light rising across the [[Tenth District]]).

## Countermeasures Under Discussion
The party debated preventing the extraction — protecting potential victims pre-death, or some armor/force-field that cancels the harvesting magic — and ultimately stopping Bolas before he gathers enough power. (See Dashboard Quest: *Stop the Spark Harvesting*.)
