# Boros Legion

## Overview

The Boros Legion is Ravnica's military guild, focused on justice and law enforcement through strength. Their headquarters is [[Sunhome]].

## Campaign Role

The Boros are one of the party's closest allied guilds. [[The Breakfast Club]]'s apartment is located in the Boros district. [[Molly]], a Boros officer, is a key ally who has provided information and support. [[Braun]], a former party companion, was a Boros-aligned character who left to train at the Boros Academy. After the shattering of the [[Guildpact]], the Boros allied with the [[Gruul Clans]] and [[Izzet League]] as "The Allies" against [[The Second Sun]].

[[Clarissa]] also started working at a bar in the Boros district after being rescued by the party.
