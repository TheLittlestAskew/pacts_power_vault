# Golgari Swarm

## Overview

The Golgari Swarm is Ravnica's guild of death, decay, and the Undercity. Their leader is [[Vraska]].

## Campaign Role

The Golgari have been antagonistic throughout the campaign. [[Vraska]] assassinated [[Jace Beleren]] at the [[Spring Festival]], shattering the [[Guildpact]]. Golgari undead poured from underground after the assassination. The Golgari aligned with [[Azorius Senate]] and [[Cult of Rakdos]] as "[[The Second Sun]]."

The party encountered Golgari creatures in the tunnels during early sessions (Goliaths, Drudge Beetles, Groodions, and the Rotworm [[Steve]]). A Golgari necromancer named Vaudahn fought the party in the sewers beneath [[New Prahv]].

Late in the campaign, the party discussed needing a Golgari representative for the [[Niv-Mizzet]] revival ritual, noting the Golgari and [[Simic Combine]] are the most difficult guilds to recruit.
