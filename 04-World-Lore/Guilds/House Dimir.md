# House Dimir

## Overview

House Dimir is Ravnica's guild of espionage, secrets, and information. Their guildmaster is [[Lazav]].

## Campaign Role

The Dimir are currently unaligned in the guild conflict. [[Benji]], a Dimir agent and old school friend of [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]], is a key contact. In early sessions, a Dimir insurgent beckoned Varis to follow after the party's arrest and provided him with items. Later, Benji served as a messenger and diplomat for the party, though he noted the Dimir Guild is "figuring their own thing out right now."

Benji also examined the [[Sole Integrated Storage]] (SIS) artifact with interest, and the DM noted part of SIS "belongs to the Dimir Guild."
