# Cult of Rakdos

## Overview

The Cult of Rakdos is Ravnica's guild of chaos, entertainment, and hedonistic destruction.

## Campaign Role

Despite initially being aligned with "[[The Second Sun]]," the Rakdos have a friendlier relationship with [[The Breakfast Club]] than expected. The party planned to return for Ragefest after an early encounter. [[Ogre]] performed Rakdos tricks with sausages and fire. Hikaru, a Rakdos contact, is considered an ally; [[Benji]] was sent to speak with them about fundraising.

Notably, Rakdos buildings remained intact while other guilds' buildings were destroyed during the Eternals crisis.
