# Orzhov Syndicate

## Overview

The Orzhov Syndicate is Ravnica's guild of religion, finance, and debt. Their basilica is [[Orzhova]]. They operate as both a church and a banking institution.

## Campaign Role

[[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]'s adopted father is an Orzhov priest at [[Orzhova]]. The Orzhov district has been devastated; by the later sessions it is described as a "ghost town" with no living or dead souls remaining. The remnants of the Orzhov Guild have relocated to the [[Selesnya Conclave]] to set up banking operations.

A key late-campaign objective involves reinstating the Orzhov bank to fund the party's participation in an auction for a planar artifact.
