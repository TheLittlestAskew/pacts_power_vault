# Izzet League

## Overview

The Izzet League is Ravnica's guild of arcane science, invention, and experimentation. Their headquarters is the [[Fortress of Nivix]]. The guild's Parun (founder) is [[Niv-Mizzet]].

## Campaign Role

The Izzet are allied with [[The Breakfast Club]]. [[Dahlia\|Dalya Bara/Dahlia]] became the Izzet guild leader with the party's involvement. [[Scrappy]], the party's mechanical Izzet horse, pulls [[Ogre's Traveling Tavern]]. The Izzet provided the party with key technology and allies throughout the campaign.

After the shattering of the [[Guildpact]], the Izzet aligned with the [[Boros Legion]] and [[Gruul Clans]] as "The Allies."

A key late-campaign quest involves reviving [[Niv-Mizzet]] using artifacts from every guild, taken to the [[Implicit Maze]] for a ritual. Niv-Mizzet's spirit is currently held in [[Varis Aestra\|Varis]]'s Dragon Spirit Box.
