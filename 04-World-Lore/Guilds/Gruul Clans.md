# Gruul Clans

## Overview

The Gruul Clans are Ravnica's guild of destruction, nature reclamation, and primal rage. Their stronghold is [[Skaarg]].

## Campaign Role

The Gruul were initially hostile, led by [[Domri Rade]] who was aligned with [[Nicol Bolas]]. [[Domri Rade]] participated in the assassination of [[Jace Beleren]] at the [[Spring Festival]]. However, the party later helped [[Borborygmos]] and Sharp Dog solidify control over the Gruul by dealing with Domri's faction. After the [[Tenth District]] split, the Gruul aligned with the [[Boros Legion]] and [[Izzet League]] as "The Allies."

[[Ogre]] notably punched the rival Gruul contender "in the crotch" to help Borborygmos retain leadership.
