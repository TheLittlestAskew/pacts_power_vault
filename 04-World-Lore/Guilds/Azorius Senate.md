# Azorius Senate

## Overview

The Azorius Senate is Ravnica's guild of law, order, and bureaucracy. Their headquarters is [[New Prahv]].

## Campaign Role

The Azorius have been antagonistic toward [[The Breakfast Club]] since the early sessions. [[Dovin Baan]], the Azorius guildleader, was one of the conspirators during the [[Spring Festival]] attack. The party was arrested by Azorius guards in Session 2 and held in the [[Eighth Precinct Jail]].

After the shattering of the [[Guildpact]], the Azorius aligned with the [[Golgari Swarm]] and [[Cult of Rakdos]] under the banner of "[[The Second Sun]]," an opposing faction in the civil split of the [[Tenth District]].
