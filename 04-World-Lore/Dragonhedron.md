# Dragonhedron

The **Dragon Spirit Box** — the dodecahedral container [[Varis Aestra|Varis]] has carried since the campaign's early sessions, which once held [[Niv-Mizzet]]'s preserved spirit. In the finale it becomes the **prison of [[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]]'s soul**, sealed by [[Ugin]].

## Description / Nature
A dragon-spirit vessel (the "Dragon Spirit Box" / "Dragonhedron"). Earlier in the campaign it absorbed and preserved [[Niv-Mizzet]]'s essence from the Fire-Mind's remains (T04) — later transformed into the [[Firemind Vessel]] that birthed the reborn Niv at [[Maze's End]] (PP 15–17). In the finale, with Niv long since reborn, the box serves its final purpose: a containment cell strong enough to hold an **Elder Dragon's soul.**

## Campaign Role
[[Nicol Bolas|Bolas]] **cannot be destroyed, only contained** — he is, per [[Ugin]], "part of what made existence." After the party breaks his siphon and his spark is harvested, his soul is **sealed into the Dragonhedron**, and **Ugin takes the box into self-exile** as Bolas's eternal warden. The vessel that began as a tool to *revive* one dragon ends the campaign as the *prison* of another — the multiverse's longest family feud sealed inside it.

## Key Events

### [[PP_21_053126_I_Miss_You_Buddy|PP 21 — I Miss You, Buddy]] (05/31/26)
- ⭐ **Becomes Bolas's prison.** With Bolas down but undyable (an elder dragon, part of existence), **[[Ugin]] appears fully corporeal** and counsels that Bolas can only be contained. Bolas's soul is **sealed into the Dragonhedron** ([[Varis Aestra|Varis]]'s Dragon Spirit Box), and **Ugin sentences himself to imprison Bolas** in his own realm — bowing, thanking the party for ending a multi-millennia family feud, and **vanishing with the box.**
- Note: [[Ogre]] had first tried to siphon Bolas's soul with Duskblade and **failed** (Bolas is too primordial to reap) — confirming containment, not destruction, was the only path.
